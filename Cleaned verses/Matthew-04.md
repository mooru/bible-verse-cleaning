###### v1
a. then Jesus was led up into the desert by the Spirit
b. to be tempted by the devil

###### v2
a. and having fasted forty days and forty nights
b. finally He was hungry

###### v3
a. and the tempter having come to Him said
b. if You are (the) Son of God
c. speak so that these stones may become bread

###### v4
a. but He having answered said
b. it is written
c. not by bread alone shall (a) person live
d. but by each word coming out through (the) mouth of God

###### v5
a. then the devil took Him along to the holy city
b. and made Him stand on the pinnacle of the temple

###### v6
a. and he says to Him
b. if You are (the) Son of God
c. throw Yourself down
d. for it is written that
e. to His angels He gives command about You
f. and they will carry You by (their) hands
g. so that You may not strike Your foot against (a) stone

###### v7
a. Jesus said to him
b. it is also written
c. you shall not tempt (the) Lord your God

###### v8
a. again the devil takes Him along to (a) very high mountain
b. and he shows Him all the kingdoms of the world
c. and also their splendour

###### v9
a. and he said to Him
b. this all I will give to You
c. if having fallen down You worship me

###### v10
a. then Jesus says to him
b. go away Satan
c. for it is written

a. now having heard that

a. and having left Nazareth

###### v14

b. He saw two brothers
c. Simon the one called Peter
d. and Andrew his brother
e. throwing (a) net into the sea

###### v19

###### v20

###### v22

c. and preaching the good news of the Kingdom
