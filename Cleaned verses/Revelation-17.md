###### v1
a. then one of the seven angels came
b. of those having the seven bowls
c. and he spoke with me saying
d. come here I will show you the judgment of the great prostitute
e. the one sitting upon many waters

###### v2
a. with whom the kings of the earth committed fornication
b. and those dwelling on the earth became drunk
c. from the wine of her fornication

###### v3
a. then he carried me away into (the) desert by (the) Spirit
b. and I saw (a) woman sitting on (a) scarlet beast
c. being full of names of blasphemy
d. having seven heads and ten horns

###### v4
a. now the woman had been clothed in purple and scarlet
b. and was adorned with gold and precious stone and pearls
c. holding (a) golden cup in her hand
d. being full of what is detestable
e. as well as the defiled things of her fornication

###### v5
a. further on her forehead (a) secret name was written
b. Babylon the great
c. the mother of the sexually immoral persons and of the detestable things of the earth

###### v6
a. then I saw the woman being drunk from the blood of God's people
b. and from the blood of the martyrs for Jesus
c. and seeing her I marvelled with great amazement

###### v7
a. then the angel said to me
b. why did you marvel?
c. I will tell you the mystery of the woman
d. and of the beast the one carrying her
e. the one having the seven heads and the ten horns

###### v8
a. the beast that you saw
b. he was and is not
c. and he is being about to come up from the abyss
d. and he goes into destruction
e. then those dwelling upon the earth will be made amazed
f. of whom their name has not been written in the book of life
g. since (the) creation of (the) world
h. seeing the beast
i. because he was and he is not yet he is present

###### v9
a. in this case the mind (must) be having wisdom
b. the seven heads are seven mountains
c. where the woman sits on them
d. further they are seven kings

###### v10
a. the five were destoyed
b. the one is
c. the other one came not yet
d. now when he would come
e. he must stay (a) little

###### v11
a. then the beast who was and is not
b. surely he himself is (the) eighth
c. yet he is from the seven
d. also he goes along to destruction

###### v12
a. now the ten horns that you saw
b. they are ten kings
c. who did not yet receive (a) kingdom
d. but for one hour they receive like kings authority along with the beast

###### v13
a. these have one intention
b. yet they give their power and authority to the beast

###### v14
a. these will make war against the Lamb
b. yet the Lamb will conquer them
c. because He is Lord of lords and King of kings
d. and those with Him (are) called and chosen and faithful

###### v15
a. also he says to me
b. the waters that you saw
c. where the prostitute sits
d. are peoples and crowds and nations and languages

###### v16
a. and the ten horns that you saw as well as the beast
b. these ones hate the prostitute
c. and they will cause her to be desolate and naked
d. and they will eat her flesh
e. and they will burn her down with fire

###### v17
a. because God put into their minds
b. to accomplish His purpose
c. indeed to accomplish one purpose
d. and also to give their kingdom to the beast
e. until the words of God will be accomplished

###### v18
a. now the woman that you saw
b. is the great city
c. the one having rule over the kings of the earth
