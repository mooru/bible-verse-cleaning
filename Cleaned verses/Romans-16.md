###### v1
a. I now recommend to you Phoebe our sister in the faith
b. being [also] (a) deacon of the church in Cenchreae

###### v2
a. so that you may welcome her in (the) Lord fitting of God's people
b. and help her in what ever task she may need of you
c. for she also became (a) helper of many
d. also of me myself

###### v3
a. greet Prisca and Aquila
b. my fellow workers in Christ Jesus

###### v4
a. who for my person risked their own life
b. to whom not only I am thankful
c. but also all the churches of the Gentiles

###### v5
a. (greet) also the church in their house
b. greet my dear Epaenetus
c. who is (the) first of Asia (who turned) to Christ

###### v6
a. greet Mary who worked very hard for you

###### v7
a. greet Andronicus and Junias
b. my relatives and also my fellow prisoners
c. who are outstanding among the apostles
d. who also before me have become in Christ

###### v8
a. greet Ampliatus my dear one in (the) Lord

###### v9
a. greet Urbanus our fellow worker in Christ
b. and my dear Stachys

###### v10
a. greet Apelles the respected one in Christ
b. greet those from the ones of Aristobulus

###### v11
a. greet Herodion my relative
b. greet those out of those of Narcissus those being in (the) Lord

###### v12
a. greet Tryphaena and Tryphosa those having worked hard in (the) Lord
b. greet Persis the dear one
c. who worked very hard in (the) Lord

###### v13
a. greet Rufus the chosen one in (the) Lord
b. and his mother and mine

###### v14
a. greet Asyncritus Phlegon Hermes Patrobas Hermas
b. and the fellow believers with them

###### v15
a. greet Philologus and Julia
b. Nereus and his sister
c. also Olympas
d. and all God's people with them

###### v16
a. greet one another with (a) holy kiss
b. all the congregations of Christ greet you

###### v17
a. I then encourage you fellow believers
b. to watch out for those (causing) divisions and sin
c. acting against the teaching that you were taught
d. indeed keep away from them

###### v18
a. for such people do not serve our Lord Christ
b. but their own physical desires
c. and by fine language and flattery
d. they mislead the minds of the naive

###### v19
a. nevertheless your obedience became known to all
b. about you I am indeed happy
c. and I want you to be wise concerning the good
d. and untainted concerning the evil

###### v20
a. and the God of peace will soon crush Satan to be under your complete control
b. the grace of our Lord Jesus (be) with you

###### v21
a. Timothy my fellow worker greets you
b. and Lucius and Jason and Sosipater my relatives

###### v22
a. I Tertius the one having written the letter greet you in (the) Lord

###### v23
a. Gaius my host and (that) of the whole church greets you
b. Erastus the treasurer of the city greets you
c. and Quartus our fellow believer

###### v25
a. now to the One able to make you strong
b. in accordance with my gospel
c. and also the preaching of Jesus Christ
d. in accordance with (the) revelation of (the) mystery
e. kept quiet since all time

###### v26
a. but now revealed
b. and also by prophetic passages
c. in accordance with (the) command of the eternal God
d. to obedience of faith
e. made known to all the nations

###### v27
a. to (the) only wise God
b. through Jesus Christ
c. to Whom (is) the glory forever
d. amen
