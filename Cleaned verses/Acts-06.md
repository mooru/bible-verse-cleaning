###### v1
a. now in these days while the disciples were greatly increasing
b. there was (a) complaint of the Greek-speaking Jews
c. against the native Jews
d. because their widows were being disregarded in the daily provision

###### v2
a. then the twelve having called together the whole group of disciples
b. they said
c. it is not right (that) we having neglected the message of God
d. to handle finances

###### v3
a. so then fellow believers carefully select seven men from you being spoken well of
b. full of (the) Holy Spirit and wisdom
c. whom we will put in charge over this necessary work

###### v4
a. but we will devote ourselves to prayer and the ministry of the message

###### v5
a. then the statement pleased the opinion of the whole group
b. so they chose Stephen
c. (a) man full of faith and (the) Holy Spirit
d. and Philip and Prochorus and Nicanor and Timon
e. and Parmenas and Nicolaus (a) proselyte an Antiochean

###### v6
a. whom they let stand before the apostles
b. then having prayed they placed their hands on them

###### v7
a. and the message of God kept spreading
b. and the number of the disciples in Jerusalem was increasing very greatly
c. and (a) large number of the priests obeyed the faith

###### v8
a. now Stephen full of grace and power
b. was doing great wonders and signs among the people

###### v9
a. then some of those from the synagogue stood up
b. the one called of Free Men
c. and of (the) Cyrenians and of (the) Alexandrians
d. and those from Cilicia and Asia
e. arguing with Stephen

###### v10
a. yet they were not able to oppose his wisdom
b. and also the Holy Spirit by Whom he was speaking

###### v11
a. then they bribed men saying that
b. we have heard him speaking blasphemous statements
c. against Moses and God

###### v12
a. and they stirred up the people and the elders and the experts in the Law
b. then having attacked they seized him
c. and they brought (him) in the midst of the Sanhedrin

###### v13
a. and then they set up witnesses liars saying
b. this man does not stop speaking words
c. against [this] holy place and the Law

###### v14
a. for we have heard him saying that
b. this Jesus the Nazarene will destroy this place
c. and He will change the customs which Moses handed down to us

###### v15
a. then looking straight at him all those sitting in the Sanhedrin
b. they saw his face like (the) face of (an) angel
