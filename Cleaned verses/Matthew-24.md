###### v1
a. then Jesus having gone out from the temple departed
b. and His disciples came to (Him) to show Him the buildings of the temple

###### v2
a. then He having answered said to them
b. you see all these things do you not?
c. truly I say to you
d. surely not (a) stone will be left here upon (a) stone
e. which will not be torn down

###### v3
a. then He having sat down on the Mount of Olives
b. the disciples approached Him privately saying
c. tell us
d. when will this happen
e. and what (will be) the sign of Your coming
f. and (the) end of the world system?

###### v4
a. then Jesus having answered said to them
b. beware so that someone does not deceive you

###### v5
a. because many will come in My Name saying
b. I am the Messiah
c. and they will deceive many

###### v6
a. and you will be about to hear of wars and news of wars
b. take note
c. do not be alarmed
d. because it must happen
e. but it is not yet the end

###### v7
a. for nation will make war against nation
b. and kingdom against kingdom
c. and famines and eathquakes will happen throughout places

###### v8
a. but all these things (are the) beginning of great sufferings

###### v9
a. then they will hand you over for suffering
b. and they will kill you
c. and you will be hated by all the nations because of My Name

###### v10
a. and then many will give up believing
b. and they will betray one another
c. and they will hate one another

###### v11
a. then many false prophets will be raised up
b. and they will deceive many

###### v12
a. and because of the increase of lawlessness
b. the love of many will diminish greatly

###### v13
a. but the one having endured to (the) end
b. this one will be saved

###### v14
a. yet this gospel of the Kingdom will be preached among all the people on earth
b. as (a) witness to all the nations
c. and then the end will come

###### v15
a. when then you see the abomination which defiles
b. the one spoken (about) by Daniel the prophet
c. standing in (the) holy place
d. the one reading (this) must understand

###### v16
a. then those in Judea must flee to the mountains

###### v17
a. the one on the housetop must not come down
b. to take the things out of his house

###### v18
a. and the one in the field must not turn back
b. to pick up his coat

###### v19
a. further horror to those being pregnant
b. and to those nursing a baby in those days

###### v20
a. and pray that
b. your flight may not happen in winter and not on (the) Sabbath

###### v21
a. because then there will be great suffering
b. such as has not happened since (the) beginning of (the) world until now
c. neither will certainly not happen

###### v22
a. and if not those days have been reduced in number
b. any people would not have been saved
c. but because of those chosen
d. those days will be reduced in number

###### v23
a. then if someone says to you
b. look
c. here (is) the Messiah
d. or here
e. do not believe (it)

###### v24
a. because false messiahs and false prophets will be raised up
b. and they will cause great signs and wonders
c. so as to deceive if possible also the elect

###### v25
a. look
b. I have warned you

###### v26
a. if then they say to you
b. look
c. He is in the desert
d. do not go out
e. look
f. (He is) in the inner room
g. do not believe (it)

###### v27
a. because just as the lightning goes out from (the) east
b. and becomes visible as far as (the) west
c. thus will be the coming of the Son of Man

###### v28
a. where ever the corpse may be
b. there the vultures will be gathered

###### v29
a. immediately then after the suffering of those days
b. the sun will be darkened
c. and the moon will not give its light
d. and the stars will fall from the sky
e. and the powers of the sky will be shaken

###### v30
a. and then the sign of the Son of Man will appear in (the) sky
b. and then all the nations of the earth will mourn
c. then they will see the Son of Man
d. coming on the clouds of the sky
e. with power and great glory

###### v31
a. then He will send out His angels
b. with great trumpet sound
c. then He will cause His elect to come together from the four winds
d. from (the) ends of (the) sky as far as their ends

###### v32
a. and learn the parable from the fig tree
b. when already its branch became tender
c. and the leaves sprout
d. know that
e. the summer (is) near

###### v33
a. so also you when you see all these things
b. know that
c. it is near very soon

###### v34
a. truly I say to you that
b. this generation will not at all pass away
c. until all these things would happen

###### v35
a. the sky and the earth will pass away
b. but My words will never pass away

###### v36
a. now about those days and hours nobody knows
b. neither the angels of heaven nor the Son
c. except the Father alone

###### v37
a. because just as (it was) in the days of Noah
b. so will be the coming of the Son of Man

###### v38
a. because as it was during the days [those] before the flood
b. they were eating and drinking
c. marrying and being given in marriage
d. until which day Noah went into the ark

###### v39
a. yet they knew not
b. until the flood came
c. and it destroyed all
d. so will be [also] the coming of the Son of Man

###### v40
a. then two men will be in the field
b. one is taken along
c. and one is left behind

###### v41
a. two women grinding grain with the mill
b. one is taken along
c. and one is left behind

###### v42
a. therefore be alert
b. because you do not know
c. on which day your Lord comes

###### v43
a. but know that namely
b. if the master of the houshold knew
c. at which period of night the thief comes
d. he would have stayed awake
e. and he would not have allowed his house to be broken in

###### v44
a. because of this also you must be prepared
b. because you imagine not at which hour
c. the Son of Man comes

###### v45
a. who then is the faithful and wise slave
b. whom the owner appointed over his household servants
c. to give them their food on time?

###### v46
a. fortunate (is) that slave whom
b. his master having come
c. he will find doing like this

###### v47
a. truly I say to you that
b. he will appoint him over all the things belonging to him

###### v48
a. but if that bad slave should say in his inner self
b. my master is late

###### v49
a. and then he would begin to beat his fellow slaves
b. and he would eat and drink with the drunkards

###### v50
a. the master of that slave will come
b. on (a) day that he does not expect
c. and at (an) hour that he does not know

###### v51
a. then he will cut him in two
b. yes he will let his share be with the hypocrites
c. there will be weeping and gnashing of the teeth
