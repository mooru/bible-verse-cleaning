###### v1
a. now we ask you fellow believers
b. about the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ
c. and our gathering together to Him

###### v2
a. so that you not be quickly upset because of your way of thinking
b. and not be alarmed
c. not by (your) frame of mind
d. not by (a) statement
e. not by (a) letter
f. as (said to be) from us
g. namely that the day of the Lord has happened

###### v3
a. let no one deceive you in no way
b. because however the rebellion happens first
c. and then the man of lawlessness is revealed
d. the son of destruction

###### v4
a. the one being hostile
b. and rising up in pride against everything called (a) god or object  
c. so that he sits in the Temple of God
d. showing himself that he is God

###### v5
a. do you not remember that
b. being still with you
c. I told you these things?

###### v6
a. yes now you know what is preventing
b. so that he be revealed in his own time

###### v7
a. for the mystery of lawlessness is already at work
b. (there is) only One preventing now
c. until he becomes from among

###### v8
a. and then the lawless one will be revealed
b. whom the Lord [Jesus] will do away with
c. with the breath of His mouth
d. in fact He will destroy (him)
e. at the appearance of His coming

###### v9
a. (the lawless one) of whose coming is
b. in accordance with (the) work of Satan
c. with all power
d. and signs
e. and wonders of falsehood

###### v10
a. and with all deception of unrighteousness
b. for those perishing
c. because of them they did not accept the love of the truth
d. so that they may have been saved

###### v11
a. and because of this God sends upon them
b. (the) practice of deceptive belief
c. with the result that they believed the lie

###### v12
a. so that they all may be condemned
b. the ones not having believed the truth
c. but having preferred the unrighteousness

###### v13
a. now we are under obligation
b. to give thanks to God always for you fellow believers
c. beloved by (the) Lord
d. because God chose you first
e. for salvation
f. through dedication of (the) Spirit
g. and by faith in (the) truth

###### v14
a. by which [also] He called you through our gospel
b. for (the) experience of (the) glory of our Lord Jesus Christ

###### v15
a. so then fellow believers stand firm
b. and hold to the teachings
c. that you were taught
d. either by speaking
e. or by (a) letter from us

###### v16
a. now our Lord Jesus Christ Himself
b. and God our Father
c. the One having loved us
d. and having given (us) everlasting encouragement
e. and abundant hope
f. by means of (His) grace

###### v17
a. may He encourage your inner selves
b. and may He strengthen (you)
c. in every task
d. and (in) good speech
