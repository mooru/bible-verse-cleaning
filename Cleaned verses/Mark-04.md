###### v1
a. now again He began to teach beside the sea
b. then (a) very large crowd gathered around Him
c. so that He having got into (a) boat
d. sat (in it) on the sea
e. and the whole crowd was beside the sea on the land

###### v2
a. then He taught them many things by means of parables
b. and He said to them in His teaching

###### v3
a. listen
b. pay attention
c. the sower went out to sow

###### v4
a. and it happened during the sowing
b. some indeed fell along the road
c. and the birds came
d. and they ate it up

###### v5
a. and other fell on the rocky ground
b. where it had not much soil
c. and then it sprouted leaves
d. because it has not depth of soil

###### v6
a. and when the sun rose
b. it was scorched
c. and because it had no root
d. it withered

###### v7
a. and other fell among the thorn plants
b. and the thorn plants grew
c. and choked it
d. and so it did not produce fruit

###### v8
a. and other fell into the good soil
b. and growing up it produced fruit
c. and increasing in size
d. and yielded one thirty and one sixty and one hundred

###### v9
a. then He said
b. who has ears to hear must hear

###### v10
a. and when He was alone
b. those around Him were asking Him along with the twelve about the parables

###### v11
a. and He said to them
b. to you the secret of the Kingdom of God has been given
c. but to those the ones outside everything happens by parables

###### v12
a. so that
b. seeing they may see yet they may not understand
c. and hearing they may hear yet they may not comprehend
d. so that they may not change their ways
e. and so it is forgiven for them

###### v13
a. then He says to them
b. you do not understand this parable
c. indeed how will you understand all the parables?

###### v14
a. the one sowing sows the message

###### v15
a. now these are those along the road
b. where the message is sown
c. and when they may hear (it)
d. right away Satan comes
e. and he takes away the message sown to them

###### v16
a. now these are those being sown on the rocky ground
b. who when they would hear the message
c. immediately they receive it with joy

###### v17
a. yet they do not have root in themselves
b. indeed they are temporary
c. afterwards trouble having happened
d. or persecution because of the message
e. immediately they give up believing

###### v18
a. and others are those being sown among the thorn plants
b. these are those hearing the message

###### v19
a. and the worries of the world system
b. and the deception of wealth
c. and the strong desires about other things entering in
d. choke the message
e. and it becomes without fruit

###### v20
a. now those are
b. the ones having sown on the good soil
c. these ones hear the message
d. and they accept (it)
e. and they bear fruit one thirty and one sixty and one hundred

###### v21
a. then He said to them
b. does not the lamp happen to be that
c. it is placed under the container or under the bed?
d. (is it) not that
e. it is placed on the lampstand?

###### v22
a. because nothing is hidden
b. except that it will be revealed
c. and nothing became secret
d. but that it will come in the open

###### v23
a. if someone has ears to hear
b. let him listen

###### v24
a. then He said to them
b. pay attention to what you hear
c. by which measure you apportion
d. it will be apportioned to you
e. even it will be added to you

###### v25
a. because the one who has
b. to him will be given
c. and the one who does not have
d. even what he has
e. it will be taken away from him

###### v26
a. then He said
b. thus is the Kingdom of God
c. like (a) man throws the seed on the soil

###### v27
a. and he sleeps and he wakes up night and day
b. and then the seed sprouts leaves
c. and it grows
d. how he himself does not know

###### v28
a. by itself the soil produces fruit
b. first (a) blade
c. then (an) ear of wheat
d. then (the) full grain in the ear of wheat

###### v29
a. but when the harvest is ripe
b. immediately he begins to harvest
c. because the harvest has come

###### v30
a. then He said
b. with what will we compare the Kingdom of God
c. or by what parable will we explain it?

###### v31
a. like (a) seed of (the) mustard plant
b. which when it is sown on the soil
c. being smaller than all the seeds the ones on the earth

###### v32
a. yet when it is sown
b. it sprouts and grows
c. and it becomes bigger than all the garden plants
d. and it makes large branches
e. so that under its shade the wild birds can nest

###### v33
a. and with many such parables He spoke to them the message
b. just as they were able to hear

###### v34
a. and without (a) parable He did not speak to them
b. but privately He explained everything to His own disciples

###### v35
a. then He says to them on that day
b. when it became evening
c. let us cross over to the other side

###### v36
a. then having dismissed the crowd
b. they take Him along just as He was in the boat
c. and now other boats were with Him

###### v37
a. then (a) great storm of wind arose
b. and the waves splashed into the boat
c. so that the boat became filled already

###### v38
a. and He was in the stern of the boat
b. sleeping on the cushion
c. so they wake Him up
d. and they say to Him
e. teacher are You not concerned that we die?

###### v39
a. then having woken up He rebuked the wind
b. and He said to the sea
c. become calm
d. become quiet
e. then the wind ceased
f. and (a) great calm occurred

###### v40
a. and He said to them
b. why are you cowards?
c. do you still not have faith?

###### v41
a. and they feared (a) great fear
b. then they said to one another
c. who possibly is this One that
d. even the wind and also the sea obeys Him?
