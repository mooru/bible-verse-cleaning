###### v1
a. then I saw another sign in heaven
b. big and marvelous
c. seven angels having the last seven plagues
d. because with them the wrath of God  was ended

###### v2
a. then I saw like (a) glass sea
b. having been mixed with fire
c. and (I saw) those conquering free from the beast and free from its image
d. and free from the number of his name
e. those having stood at the glass sea
f. holding harps of God

###### v3
a. and they sang the song of Moses the servant of God
b. and also the song of the Lamb saying
c. great and wonderful (are) Your deeds
d. Lord God the Almighty
e. righteous and true (are) Your ways
f. the King of the nations

###### v4
a. who would not ever show (You) great reverence o Lord
b. and will glorify Your Name?
c. because only (You are) Holy
d. because all the nations will be there
e. and they will bow down and worship before You
f. because Your righteous acts were made known

###### v5
a. then after these things I saw
b. indeed the temple of the tent of the testimony was opened in heaven

###### v6
a. and the seven angels went out
b. [the ones] having the seven plagues
c. out of the temple
d. having been clothed in pure bright linen
e. and having golden belts girded around the chest

###### v7
a. then one of the four living beings gave to the seven angels
b. seven golden bowls being full of the wrath of God
c. the One living forever and ever

###### v8
a. and the temple was filled of smoke
b. by the glory of God
c. and by His power
d. and no one was able to enter into the temple
e. until the seven plagues of the seven angels would come to an end
