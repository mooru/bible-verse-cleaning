###### v1
a. then the Pharisees gather together against Him
b. as well as some of the Law experts having come from Jerusalem

###### v2
a. and they having seen some of His disciples that
b. with defiled hands that means unwashed
c. they eat bread

###### v3
a. because the Pharisees as well as all the Jews
b. if they do not wash their hands with (the) fist
c. they do not eat
d. keeping the tradition of the elders

###### v4
a. and (coming) from (the) marketplace
b. if they would not wash
c. they do not eat
d. and there are many other things that they received instruction to keep
e. washings of cups and jars and copperware [and dining couches]

###### v5
a. then the Pharisees and the Law experts ask Him
b. why do Your disciples not behave
c. according to the tradition of the elders
d. but they eat bread with defiled hands

###### v6
a. then He said to them
b. Isaiah prophesied accurately about you hypocrites
c. as it is written [that]
d. this nation honours Me with their lips
e. but the inner self of them is far away from Me

###### v7
a. and they worship Me in vain
b. teaching teachings (being) commandments of people

###### v8
a. rejecting the commandment of God
b. they hold on to the traditions of people

###### v9
a. then He said to them
b. certainly you reject the commandment of God
c. so that you may maintain your tradition

###### v10
a. indeed Moses said
b. honour your father and your mother
c. and the one insulting father or mother
d. he must die by death

###### v11
a. but you say
b. if (a) person says to his father or to his mother
c. corban that means gift
d. what ever from me you would have been helped

###### v12
a. you allow him no longer to do nothing for his father or for his mother

###### v13
a. you invalidate the authority of the message of God
b. by your tradition that you handed down
c. and many such similar things you do

###### v14
a. then having called to Himself again the crowd
b. He said to them
c. you all listen to Me
d. and have insight

###### v15
a. there is nothing outside the person
b. coming into him
c. that can make him unclean
d. but the things coming out of the person
e. are the things making the person unclean

###### v17
a. then when He went into (the) house away from the crowd
b. His disciples asked Him about the parable

###### v18
a. and He says to them
b. are you likewise also without understanding?
c. do you not understand that
d. everything outside coming into the person
e. can not make him unclean

###### v19
a. because it does not go into his inner self but into his belly
b. and then it goes out into the toilet
c. making all food ritually clean?

###### v20
a. then He said that
b. what comes out of the person
c. that thing makes the person unclean

###### v21
a. because from out of the inner self of people the evil reasonings come out
b. sexual immoralities thefts murders

###### v22
a. adulteries greed evil deeds
b. treachery immorality jealousy
c. blasphemy arrogance foolishness

###### v23
a. all these evil things come out from the inner being
b. and they make the person unclean

###### v24
a. then having stood up from there
b. He went away to the regions of Tyre
c. then having gone into (a) house
d. He wanted no one to know (it)
e. yet He was not able to escape notice

###### v25
a. and as soon as (a) woman having heard about Him
b. of whom her little daughter had (an) evil spirit
c. having come she fell down at His feet

###### v26
a. now the woman was (a) Greek
b. by descent (a) Syrophoenician woman
c. and she asked Him that
d. He should cast out the demon from her daughter

###### v27
a. then He said to her
b. allow the children to be given food first
c. for it is not fitting to take the food of the children
d. and to throw to the dogs

###### v28
a. then she answered and says to Him
b. Sir even the dogs eat underneath the table from the crumbs of the children

###### v29
a. then He said to her
b. because of this saying you may go
c. the demon has gone out of your daughter

###### v30
a. then having gone away to her house
b. she found the child
c. having been put on the bed
d. and the demon having gone out

###### v31
a. then again having left from the regions of Tyre
b. He went through Sidon
c. to the Sea of Galilee
d. within the regions of Decapolis

###### v32
a. then they bring to Him (a) deaf man (who) also could hardly speak
b. and they ask Him earnestly that
c. He should lay His hand on him

###### v33
a. then having taken him away from the crowd privately
b. He put His fingers into his ears
c. and having spit He touched his tongue

###### v34
a. and having looked up to the sky
b. He sighed and says to him
c. ephphatha
d. that means be opened

###### v35
a. then [immediately] his abilities to hear were opened
b. and the chain of his tongue was loosened
c. and he spoke correctly

###### v36
a. then He commanded them that
b. they should tell no one
c. but as much as He commanded them
d. (the) more excessively they spoke

###### v37
a. and they were extremely greatly astonished saying
b. He has done all things well
c. indeed He makes the deaf hear
d. and [the] mute speak
