###### v1
a. let every person submit to (the) controlling authorities
b. for there is no authority except under God
c. and those existing are established by God

###### v2
a. so that the one opposing the authority
b. has resisted the ordinance of God
c. and those having resisted will receive judgment for themselves

###### v3
a. because the rulers are not (a) source of fear
b. to good work
c. but to evil (work)
d. now you want not to fear the authority
e. do good
f. and you will have praise from it

###### v4
a. because he is (a) servant of God to you for the good
b. but fear when you do evil
c. because he does not bear the sword in vain
d. for he is (a) servant of God
e. (a) punisher concerning wrath to the one doing evil

###### v5
a. therefore (it) is necessary to obey
b. not only because of the punishment
c. but also because of the conscience

###### v6
a. indeed because of this you also pay tribute taxes
b. for they are servants of God
c. serving personally for this same thing

###### v7
a. pay to all what one should
b. tribute to the one (asking) tribute
c. tax to the one (asking) tax
d. fear to the one fear (is due)
e. honour to the one honour (is due)

###### v8
a. owe no one nothing
b. except to love one another
c. because the one loving the other
d. has given true meaning to the Law

###### v9
a. because do not commit adultery do not murder do not steal do not covet
b. and if (there is) some other commandment
c. it is summed up in this statement [by namely]
d. you must love your neighbour as yourself

###### v10
a. love does not do harm to the neighbour
b. love therefore (is the) full measure of the Law

###### v11
a. further this understanding the age that
b. (it is) time already that you wake out of sleep
c. since now our salvation (is) much nearer
d. than when we believed

###### v12
a. the night drew to a close
b. and the day has come near
c. let us then lay aside the works of darkness
d. [and] let us put on the weapons of the light

###### v13
a. let us behave decently as in (the) day
b. not in orgies and in drunkenness
c. not in sexual immorality and in licentiousness
d. not in strife and in envy

###### v14
a. but clothe yourselves with the Lord Jesus Christ
b. and do not make provision for (the) desires of the sinful nature
