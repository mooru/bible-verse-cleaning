###### v1
a. Jesus having said this
b. He went out with His disciples across the brook of the Kidron
c. where there was (a) garden
d. into which He Himself went and also His disciples

###### v2
a. now also Judas the one betraying Him knew the place
b. because Jesus often gathered there with His disciples

###### v3
a. Judas then having taken the cohort and servants from the chief  
b. comes there with lanterns and torches and weapons

###### v4
a. Jesus then knowing everything happening to Him
b. went out and says to them
c. who are you looking for?

###### v5
a. they answered Him
b. Jesus the Nazarene
c. He says to them
d. I am (the One)
e. and also Judas the one betraying Him stood with them

###### v6
a. when He then said to them
b. I am (the One)
c. they moved to the back
d. and they fell down on the ground

###### v7
a. then again He asked them
b. who are you looking for?
c. and they said
d. Jesus the Nazarene

###### v8
a. Jesus answered
b. I said to you that
c. I am (the One)
d. if then you seek Me
e. let these go away

###### v9
a. so that the word may come true that He spoke that
b. those You gave Me I did not lose no one of them

###### v10
a. then Simon Peter having (a) sword drew it
b. and he struck the slave of the high priest
c. and he cut off his right ear
d. and (the) name for the slave was Malchus

###### v11
a. Jesus then said to Peter
b. put the sword in the sheath
c. the cup that the Father has given Me
d. will I surely not drink it?

###### v12
a. then the band of soldiers and the commanding officer and the servants of the Jews arrested Jesus
b. and they bound Him

###### v13
a. and they took (Him) first to Annas
b. for he was (the) father-in-law of Caiaphas
c. who was high priest of that year

###### v14
a. and Caiaphas was the one having advised the Jews that
b. it is advantageous that one Man dies for the people

###### v15
a. then Simon Peter and another disciple followed Jesus
b. and that disciple was well known to the high priest
c. and he went with Jesus into the courtyard of the high priest

###### v16
a. but Peter stood outside at the entrance
b. the other disciple then went out
c. the acquaintance of the high priest
d. and spoke to the door girl
e. and he brought Peter in

###### v17
a. the slave girl doorkeeper then says to Peter
b. are you not also one of the disciples of this Man?
c. he says
d. I am not

###### v18
a. now the slaves and the servants stood there
b. having made (a) charcoal fire
c. because it was cold
d. and they were warming themselves
e. and also Peter was standing with them
f. and warming himself

###### v19
a. then the high priest questioned Jesus about His disciples and about His teaching

###### v20
a. Jesus answered him
b. I have spoken openly to the world
c. I always taught in (the) synagogue and in the temple
d. where all the Jews gather
e. and in secret I spoke nothing

###### v21
a. why do you ask Me?
b. ask those having heard what I said to them
c. pay attention these people know what I said

###### v22
a. but when He said these things
b. one of the servants having stood nearby
c. gave Jesus (a) slap saying
d. is this how You answer the high priest?

###### v23
a. Jesus answered him
b. if I spoke wrongly
c. witness about the wrong
d. but if correctly
e. why do you strike Me?

###### v24
a. then Annas sent Him away bound to Caiaphas the high priest

###### v25
a. now Simon Peter was standing and warming himself
b. they then said to him
c. are you not also one of His disciples?
d. he denied (it) and said
e. I am not

###### v26
a. one of the slaves of the high priest says
b. being (a) relative of the one whose ear Peter cut off
c. did I not see you in the garden with Him?

###### v27
a. then again Peter denied (it)
b. and immediately (a) rooster crowed

###### v28
a. then they took Jesus from Caiaphas to the palace
b. and it was early morning
c. yet they did not enter themselves into the palace
d. so that they may not be defiled
e. but could eat the Passover meal

###### v29
a. so then Pilate went outside to them and said
b. what accusation do you bring [against] this Man?

###### v30
a. they replied and said to him
b. if this Man was not doing evil
c. we would not have handed Him over to you

###### v31
a. Pilate then said to them
b. you take Him
c. and you judge Him according to your Law
d. the Jews said to him
e. for us it is not allowed to kill nobody

###### v32
a. so that the statement of Jesus that He spoke may come true
b. indicating by what death He was about to die

###### v33
a. then again Pilate went into the palace
b. and then he called Jesus
c. and he said to Him
d. are You the king of the Jews?

###### v34
a. Jesus answered
b. do you say this by yourself
c. or did others say (it) to you about Me?

###### v35
a. Pilate answered
b. I am surely not (a) Jew?
c. Your people and the chief priests handed You over to me
d. what did You do?

###### v36
a. Jesus answered
b. My Kingdom is not of this world
c. if My Kingdom were of this world
d. My servants [would] have fought
e. so that I should not be handed over to the Jews
f. but now My Kingdom is not from here

###### v37
a. then Pilate said to Him
b. are You not (a) king?
c. Jesus answered
d. you say that I am (a) king
e. I was born for this
f. and for this I have come into the world
g. so that I may witness to the truth
h. everyone being of the truth
i. listens to My voice

###### v38
a. Pilate says to Him
b. what is truth?
c. and having said this
d. he again went out to the Jews
e. and he says to them
f. I do not find any guilt in Him

###### v39
a. but there is (a) custom to you that
b. I will release one for you at the Passover
c. do you then desire (that) I should release for you the king of the Jews?

###### v40
a. then they shouted again saying
b. not this Man but Barabbas
c. and Barabbas was (a) robber
