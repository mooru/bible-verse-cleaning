###### v1
a. then He said to His disciples
b. it is impossible that what causes people to sin do not happen
c. nevertheless horror through whom it happens

###### v2
a. it is advantageous for him
b. if (the) stone of a mill is put around his neck
c. and he is thrown into the sea
d. than that he causes one of these little ones to sin

###### v3
a. pay attention to yourselves
b. if your brother sins
c. rebuke him
d. and if he repents
e. forgive him

###### v4
a. and if seven times in (a) day he would have sinned concerning you
b. and seven times he would have turned around to you saying
c. I repent
d. you must forgive him

###### v5
a. then the apostles said to the Lord
b. give faith to us

###### v6
a. but the Lord said
b. if you have faith like (the) seed of (the) mustard plant
c. you would have said to [this] mulberry tree
d. be uprooted
e. and be planted in the sea
f. and it would have obeyed you

###### v7
a. now who of you having (a) slave ploughing or tending sheep
b. who to (the) one coming in from the field
c. he will say to him
d. quickly having come sit down to eat

###### v8
a. certainly will he not indeed say to him
b. make ready what I will eat
c. and having girded yourself serve me
d. while I would eat and drink
e. and after these things you will eat and drink?

###### v9
a. surely he does not have (a) thanks for the slave
b. because he did the things commanded?

###### v10
a. likewise also you when you would have done all the things  
b. must say that
c. we are slaves not deserving special praise
d. we have done what we were under obligation to do

###### v11
a. now it happened while He was on His way to Jerusalem
b. indeed He was going through (the) middle of Samaria and Galilee

###### v12
a. and then when He has come into a certain village
b. ten leper men met up with [Him]
c. who stood at a distance

###### v13
a. and they cried out saying
b. Jesus Master have mercy on us

###### v14
a. then having seen (them) He said to them
b. having gone show yourselves to the priests
c. and so it happened while they were on their way
d. they were healed

###### v15
a. now one of them having seen that he was healed
b. came back praising God with (a) loud voice

###### v16
a. and he fell down on (his) face at His feet
b. thanking Him
c. now he was (a) Samaritan

###### v17
a. then Jesus having replied said
b. were not indeed the ten healed?
c. now where (are) the nine?

###### v18
a. were they not found having turned back to give praise to God
b. except this foreigner?

###### v19
a. then He said to him
b. having stood up go
c. your faith has saved you

###### v20
a. then having been asked by the Pharisees
b. when the Kingdom of God comes
c. He replied to them and said
d. the Kingdom of God does not come with close observation

###### v21
a. they will not even say
b. look here or there
c. pay attention
d. because the Kingdom of God is within you

###### v22
a. then He said to the disciples
b. days will come
c. when you will desire greatly to see one of the days of the Son of Man
d. yet you will not see

###### v23
a. surely they will say to you
b. look there
c. [or] look here
d. do not go
e. and do not run after (what (they say))

###### v24
a. because like the lightning flashing
b. it shines from the (one side) under the sky to the (other side)  
c. like this the Son of Man will be [on His day]

###### v25
a. but first it is necessary that He suffers much
b. and be rejected by this generation

###### v26
a. and just as it happened in the days of Noah
b. like this also it will be in the days of the Son of Man

###### v27
a. they were eating (and) drinking
b. they were marrying (and) given in marriage
c. until which day Noah entered into the ark
d. and then came the flood
e. and destroyed everything

###### v28
a. likewise just as it happened in the days of Lot
b. they were eating (and) drinking
c. they were buying (and) selling
d. they were planting (and) building

###### v29
a. but on which day Lot went out from Sodom
b. fire and sulphur rained from (the) sky
c. and it destroyed everything

###### v30
a. according to the same it will be
b. on (the) day that the Son of Man is revealed

###### v31

b. and his belongings in the house
c. do not let him come down to take them away
d. and the one on (the) land likewise let him not go back to what (is)  

###### v32

b. he will lose it
c. and who ever loses (it)
d. he will keep it alive

###### v34
a. I say to you
b. on that night there will be two on one bed
c. the one will be accepted
d. yet the other one will be put out of the way

###### v35
a. there will be two grinding grain at the same (place)
b. the one will be accepted
c. and the other one will be put out of the way

a. then having replied they say to Him
b. under what circumstances Lord?
c. then He said to them
d. where the body (is)
