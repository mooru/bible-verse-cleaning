###### v2
a. now I praise you
b. because you have remembered me in everything
c. also just as I instructed you
d. continue to believe and practise the teachings

###### v3
a. further I want you to know that
b. Christ is the head of every man
c. and the husband (is the) head of (his) wife
d. and God (is the) head of Christ

###### v4
a. every man praying or prophesying having his head covered
b. puts his head to shame

###### v5
a. and every woman praying or prophesying with her head uncovered
b. puts her head to shame
c. for it is one and the same for the one having been shaven

###### v6
a. for if (a) woman has not covered her head
b. let her also cut her hair
c. but if (it is) disgraceful for (a) woman to be cut or shaven
d. let her head be covered

###### v7
a. because indeed (a) man ought not to have his head covered
b. being (the) image and glory of God
c. but the woman is (the) glory of (her) husband

###### v8
a. because man is not from woman
b. but woman (is) from man

###### v9
a. for also man was not created for the sake of woman
b. but woman (was created) for the sake of man

###### v10
a. because of this the wife ought to have (a) symbol of authority on   

###### v11
a. however neither woman (is) independent of man
b. nor man (is) independent of woman
c. in union with (the) Lord

###### v12
a. for just as woman (is) out of man
b. likewise also man because of woman
c. yet all things (are) from God

###### v13
a. judge with regard to you yourselves
b. is it fitting (that a) wife prays to God uncovered?

###### v14
a. also does not nature self teach you that
b. (a) man on the one hand if he wears long hair
c. it is (a) dishonour to him

###### v15
a. on the other hand if (a) woman wears long hair
b. it is (an) honour to her?
c. because her hair has been given [to her] instead of clothing

###### v16
a. now if someone seems to be quarrelsome
b. we do not have such (a) custom
c. also not the churches of God

###### v17
a. now commanding this I do not praise (you)
b. that you come together not for the better but for the worse

###### v18
a. because indeed most important
b. when you come together in church
c. I hear there are divisions among you
d. and partly I believe some

###### v19
a. now indeed divisions must be among you
b. so that [also] the genuine ones may become clearly known among you

###### v20
a. now you having come together at the same (place)
b. it is not to eat (the) Lord's supper

###### v21
a. because each takes his own meal ahead of time so as to eat
b. so then the one is indeed hungry
c. and the other is drunk

###### v22
a. for surely not (it can be that) you do not have houses to eat and to drink?
b. or do you despise the church of God?
c. and do you put to shame those not having? (food)
d. what shall I say to you?
e. shall I praise you?
f. in this I do not praise (you)

###### v23
a. I indeed received instruction from the Lord
b. what also I handed over to you namely
c. the Lord Jesus in the night in which He was betrayed
d. He took bread

###### v24
a. and having given thanks
b. He broke bread
c. and He said
d. this is My body for your sake
e. do this to My remembrance

###### v25
a. likewise also the cup with the meal saying
b. this cup is the new covenant by My blood
c. do this
d. as often as you drink (it)
e. for the remembrance of Me

###### v26
a. for as often as you eat this bread
b. and you drink the cup
c. you proclaim widely the death of the Lord
d. until He would come

###### v27
a. so that who ever would eat the bread or drink the cup of the Lord in an improper manner
b. will be guilty of the body and the blood of the Lord

###### v28
a. now let (a) person examine himself
b. and let him in this way eat from the bread
c. and let him drink from the cup

###### v29
a. because the one eating and drinking
b. eats and drinks condemnation on himself
c. not regarding the body as more valuable

###### v30
a. because of this (there are) among you many sick and ill
b. and many are dead

###### v31
a. but if we examine ourselves carefully
b. we would not be condemned

###### v32
a. but being judged by [the] Lord
b. we are disciplined
c. so that we may not be condemned with the world

###### v33
a. as a result my fellow believers coming together in order to eat
b. wait for one another

###### v34
a. if someone is hungry
b. let him eat at home
c. so that you do not come together for condemnation
d. and the remaining things I will instruct when I would come
