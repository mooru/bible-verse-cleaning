###### v1
a. but about the times and the occasions fellow believers
b. you have no need (that it) be written to you

###### v2
a. for you yourselves know exactly that
b. (the) day of (the) Lord comes thus like (a) thief in (the) night

###### v3
a. when they say peace and safety
b. then immediate destruction overtakes them
c. like the birth pains to the one being pregnant
d. and they would certainly not escape

###### v4
a. but you fellow believers are not in (the) darkness that
b. the day should overpower you like (a) thief

###### v5
a. for you all are persons of light and persons of (the) daylight
b. we are not of night and not of darkness

###### v6
a. so then let us not sleep like the others
b. but let us be alert and sober-minded

###### v7
a. because those sleeping
b. they sleep at night
c. and those getting drunk
d. they get drunk at night

###### v8
a. but we being of (the) day
b. let us be sober-minded
c. putting on (the) breastplate of faith and of love
d. and as helmet (the) hope of salvation

###### v9
a. because God did not subject us to anger
b. but to (the) experience of salvation
c. through our Lord Jesus Christ

###### v10
a. the One having died for us
b. so that whether we may be alive
c. or whether we may be dead
d. we would live together with Him

###### v11
a. therefore encourage one another
b. and build up one the (other) one
c. just as also you do

###### v12
a. now we ask you fellow believers
b. to respect those labouring among you
c. and having guided you with regard to (the) Lord
d. and also instructing you

###### v13
a. and to regard them very highly
b. with love
c. due to their work
d. live in peace among yourselves

###### v14
a. further we appeal to you fellow believers
b. admonish the lazy ones
c. encourage the discouraged ones
d. help the weak ones
e. be patient with all

###### v15
a. see to it that no one repays someone evil for evil
b. but always pursue the good for one another as well as for all

###### v16
a. always rejoice

###### v17
a. pray without ceasing

###### v18
a. in everything give thanks
b. for this (is the) will of God for you in Christ Jesus

###### v19
a. do not withstand the Spirit

###### v20
a. do not despise prophecies

###### v21
a. further evaluate everything
b. continue to follow the good

###### v22
a. keep away from any form of evil

###### v23
a. now may the God of peace Himself make you completely holy
b. and may your entire spirit and soul and body be kept  
c. at the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ

###### v24
a. faithful (is) the One calling you
b. He who also will accomplish (it)

###### v25
a. fellow believers pray [also] for us

###### v26
a. greet all the fellow believers with (a) holy kiss

###### v27
a. I put you under oath by the Lord
b. that the letter be read to all the fellow believers

###### v28
a. the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ (be) with you
