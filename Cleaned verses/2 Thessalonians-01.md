###### v1
a. Paul and Silvanus and Timothy
b. to the church of the Thessalonians
c. in union with God our Father and (the) Lord Jesus Christ

###### v2
a. grace to you and peace
b. from God our Father and (the) Lord Jesus Christ

###### v3
a. we are under obligation to thank God always for you fellow believers
b. inasmuch as it is fitting
c. because your faith increased exceedingly
d. and the love of each one of you all multiplies towards one another

###### v4
a. so that we ourselves boast about you to the churches of   
b. because of your endurance and faith
c. in all your persecutions
d. and (in) your sufferings
e. that you endure

###### v5
a. (it is) proof of the righteous judgment of God
b. for you to be regarded worthy of the Kingdom of God
c. for which you also suffer

###### v6
a. surely indeed (it is) proper in the judgment of God
b. to repay distress to those causing you hardship

###### v7
a. yet for you the ones suffering hardships
b. (He repays) relief together with us
c. at the revelation of the Lord Jesus
d. from heaven with angels of His might

###### v8
a. with (a) fire of flame
b. giving punishment
c. to those not respecting God
d. and to those not obeying the gospel of our Lord Jesus

###### v9
a. who will suffer punishment
b. namely eternal ruin
c. away from (the) presence of the Lord
d. and away from the amazing might of His strength

###### v10
a. when He comes
b. to be honoured
c. by His people
d. and to be marvelled (at)
e. by all those having believed
f. because our testimony was believed
g. by you on that day

###### v11
a. for this also we pray always concerning you
b. that our God may regard you worthy of His calling
c. and cause every desire of goodness to be (in you)
d. as well as (every) work of faith with power

###### v12
a. so that the Name of our Lord Jesus may be honoured by you
b. and you by Him
