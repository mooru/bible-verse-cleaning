###### v1
a. now Jesus full of (the) Holy Spirit
b. returned from the Jordan
c. and then by the Spirit He was led into the wilderness

a. being tempted by the devil for forty days
b. and He did not eat nothing during those days
c. now those (days) having ended
d. He was hungry

###### v3
a. then the devil said to Him
b. if You are (the) Son of God
c. say to this stone that
d. it should become bread

###### v4
a. then Jesus replied to him
b. it has been written that
c. the human being shall not live by bread alone

###### v5
a. then having led Him up
b. he showed Him all the kingdoms of the world in (a) moment of time

###### v6
a. and then the devil said to Him
b. I will give you all this power as well as their glory
c. because it has been given over to me
d. and to who ever I want
e. I give it

###### v7
a. You then if You bow in worship before me
b. all will be yours

###### v8
a. then having replied Jesus said to him
b. it has been written
c. (the) Lord your God you shall worship
d. and Him only you shall venerate

###### v9
a. and then he brought Him into Jerusalem
b. and he let (Him) stand on the pinnacle of the temple
c. then he said to Him
d. if You are (the) Son of God
e. throw Yourself down from here

###### v10
a. for it has been written that
b. He will command His angels with regard to You
c. to protect You

###### v11
a. also that
b. they will lift  You up on (their) hands
c. so that You do not bump Your foot against (a) stone

###### v12
a. then having answered Jesus said to him that
b. it has been said
c. you shall not test (the) Lord your God

###### v13
a. then having ended every temptation
b. the devil departed from Him until (a) time

###### v14
a. then Jesus returned by the power of the Spirit to Galilee
b. and report went out through all the surrounding region about Him

###### v15
a. also He himself taught in their synagogues
b. being praised by all

###### v16
a. and so He came into Nazareth
b. where He was brought up
c. and He entered
d. according to the custom for Him on the day of the Sabbath
e. into the synagogue
f. and then He stood up to read

###### v17
a. so (the) book of the prophet Isaiah was given to Him
b. then having unrolled the book
c. He found the passage where it was written

###### v18
a. (the) Spirit of (the) Lord (is) upon me
b. for the sake of which He appointed me
c. to bring the good news to (the) poor
d. He has sent me
e. to proclaim release for captives
f. and gaining of sight to (the) blind
g. to send (the) oppressed into liberty

###### v19
a. to announce (the) pleasing time of (the) Lord

###### v20
a. then having rolled up the book
b. having given (it) back to the servant
c. He sat down
d. and the eyes of all in the synagogue were fixed on Him

###### v21
a. then He began to say to them that
b. today this Scripture has been fulfilled in your ears

###### v22
a. and so all spoke well of Him
b. indeed they were amazed at the words of grace
c. the ones proceeding out of His mouth
d. and they said
e. is He not (the) son of Joseph?

###### v23
a. then He said to them
b. no doubt you will quote to Me this parable
c. physician heal yourself
d. whatever we heard happened in Capernaum
e. do (it) also here in your hometown

###### v24
a. then He said
b. truly I say to you that
c. no prophet is acceptable in his hometown

###### v25
a. now truly I say to you
b. there were many widows in the days of Elijah in Israel
c. when the sky was closed for three years and six months
d. so that (a) great famine occurred in all the land

###### v26
a. yet to no one of them Elijah was sent
b. except to Zarephath of Sidon
c. to (a) woman (being a) widow

###### v27
a. and there were many lepers in Israel at the time of the prophet Elisha
b. and none of them was healed except Naaman the Syrian

###### v28
a. then all were filled with anger in the synagogue
b. having heard these things

###### v29
a. and having stood up
b. they drove Him out outside the town
c. and they led Him up to (a) cliff of the mountain
d. on which their town was built
e. so that they could throw Him down a cliff

###### v30
a. but He having moved on through their midst
b. He went away

###### v31
a. then He went down to Capernaum (a) town of Galilee
b. and He was teaching them on the Sabbaths

###### v32
a. and they were greatly impressed with His teaching
b. because His message was with authority

###### v33
a. now in the synagogue was (a) man
b. having (an) evil spirit of (a) demon
c. and he cried out with (a) loud voice

###### v34
a. ah what for us and for You Jesus (the) Nazarene?
b. did You come to destroy us?
c. I know You who You are
d. the Holy One of God

###### v35
a. then Jesus spoke sternly to him saying
b. become quiet
c. and come out from him
d. then the demon having thrown him in their midst
e. he went out from him
f. having done no harm to him

###### v36
a. then astonishment happened to all
b. and they spoke with one another saying
c. what message (is) this that
d. with authority and power He commands the unclean spirits
e. and then they go out?

###### v37
a. so news went out about Him
b. to every place of the surrounding region

###### v38
a. then going away from the synagogue
b. He went into the house of Simon
c. now (the) mother-in-law of Simon was having (a) high fever
d. and they asked Him about her

###### v39
a. then having stood over her
b. He rebuked the fever
c. and it left her
d. then immediately having stood up
e. she served them

###### v40
a. now as the sun was setting
b. all of who had sick ones with various diseases
c. they brought them to Him
d. and He having laid His hands on each one of them
e. He healed them

###### v41
a. so then also demons went out from many
b. shouting and saying that
c. You are the Son of God
d. then having rebuked (them)
e. He did not allow them to speak
f. because they knew that He is the Messiah

###### v42

d. yet the crowds were looking for Him
e. and so they came up to Him
f. and they restrained Him not to go away from them

###### v43
a. but He said to them that

c. because for this I was sent

###### v44
