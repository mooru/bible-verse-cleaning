###### v1
a. then the fifth angel blew the trumpet
b. and I saw (a) star having fallen out of the sky onto the earth
c. also the key of the deep pit of the abyss was given to him

###### v2
a. then he opened the deep pit of the abyss
b. and smoke went up from the deep pit
c. like smoke of (a) large furnace
d. then the sun was darkened as well as the air
e. by means of the smoke of the deep pit

###### v3
a. then out of the smoke locusts went out onto the land
b. and power was given to them
c. just like the scorpions of the land have power

###### v4
a. and it was said to them that
b. they shall not harm the grass of the land
c. and not any plant and not any tree
d. except the people
e. who do not have the seal of God on their foreheads

###### v5
a. and to them it was allowed that
b. they should not kill them
c. but that they should torture (them) for five months
d. and their torment (was) like (a) torment of (a) scorpion
e. when it stings (a) person

###### v6
a. now in those days the people will seek death
b. and by no means they shall find it
c. indeed they will desire greatly to die
d. yet death flees from them

###### v7
a. and the similarities of the locusts (were) similar to horses
b. having been made ready for war
c. and on their heads (were) like crowns as of gold
d. yet their faces (were) like faces of people

###### v8
a. also they had hair like hair of women
b. and their teeth were like (that) of lions

###### v9
a. further they had breastplates like breastplates made of iron
b. and the sound of their wings
c. (is) like (the) sound of chariots of many horses
d. running into battle

###### v10
a. further they have tails similar to scorpions and stingers
b. indeed in their tails (is) their power
c. to harm the people for five months

###### v11
a. they have over them as king the angel of the abyss
b. (the) name to him (is) in Hebrew  Abaddon
c. and in Greek he has (the) name Apollyon

###### v12
a. the first horror passed
b. pay attention
c. there is coming still two horrors after these

###### v13
a. then the sixth angel blew the trumpet
b. and I heard one voice
c. from the [four] corners of the golden altar
d. the one in front of God

###### v14
a. saying to the sixth angel
b. the one having the trumpet
c. untie the four angels
d. the ones having been bound at the great river Euphrates

###### v15
a. then the four angels were released
b. the ones having been made ready for the hour and day and month and year
c. so that they may kill (a) third of the people

###### v16
a. now the number of the soldiers of the cavalry
b. (was) incalculable
c. I heard the number of them

###### v17
a. and thus I saw the horses in the vision
b. and those sitting upon them
c. wearing breastplates fiery red and  blue and sulphurous yellow
d. and the heads of the horses (were) like heads of lions
e. and out of their mouths went out fire and smoke and sulphur

###### v18
a. by these three plagues
b. were killed (a) third of the people
c. by the fire and the smoke and the sulphur
d. departing out of their mouths

###### v19
a. because the power of the horses is in their mouths
b. and also in their tails
c. because their tails (are) similar to snakes
d. having heads
e. and by these they harm

###### v20
a. yet the rest of the people
b. who were not killed by these plagues
c. did also not repent from the works of their hands
d. so that they will not worship the demons and the golden idols
e. and also the ones of silver and of bronze and of stone and of wood
f. which neither can see nor hear nor walk

###### v21
a. also they did not repent from their murders
b. neither from their sorcery
c. neither from their fornication
d. neither from their thefts
