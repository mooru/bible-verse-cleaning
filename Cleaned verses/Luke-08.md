a. then later it happened

c. preaching from town to village
d. indeed bringing the good news of the Kingdom of God
e. and the twelve with Him

###### v2
a. also some women who were healed from evil spirits and illnesses
b. Mary the one called Magdalene
c. from whom seven demons went out

###### v3
a. and Joanna (the) wife of Chuza (a) foreman of Herod
b. and Susanna and many others
c. who took care of them  by means of their possessions

###### v4
a. now (a) large crowd assembling
b. and from town to town people coming to Him
c. He spoke (to them) by (a) parable

###### v5
a. (a) sower went out to sow his seed
b. and while he is sowing
c. some then fell along the road
d. indeed it was trampled on
e. also the wild birds ate it up

###### v6
a. and other fell on the rocky ground
b. and it grew
c. it withered because it did not have moisture

###### v7
a. and another fell among the thorn plants
b. now the thorn plants having grown with (it)
c. it caused the plants to die

###### v8
a. and another fell in the good soil
b. and having grown
c. it produced fruit hundred times as much
d. saying these things
e. He called out
f. the one having ears to hear let him hear

###### v9
a. then His disciples asked Him
b. what this parable could be

###### v10
a. then He said
b. to you it has been given to know the mysteries of the Kingdom of God
c. but to the rest (it has been given) in parables
d. so that seeing they would not perceive
e. and hearing they would not understand

###### v11
a. now this parable stands for (the following)
b. the seed is the message of God

###### v12
a. and those along the road are the ones having heard
b. afterwards the devil comes
c. and he takes away the message from their minds
d. so that not having believed they would be saved

###### v13
a. but those among the rocky ground
b. who when they would hear
c. they receive the message with joy
d. yet they do not have root
e. who believe for a while
f. though in time of temptation they fall away

###### v14
a. now the (part) having fallen among the thorn plants
b. these are the ones having heard
c. yet living under the control of cares and riches and pleasures of life
d. they are overwhelmed
e. and they (do) not bear mature fruit

###### v15
a. but the (part) in the good soil
b. these ones are those
c. having heard the message with (a) fine and good inner self
d. they continue to believe and practice
e. and they produce results with endurance

###### v16
a. further no one having lit (a) lamp
b. covers it with (a) bowl
c. or puts (it) under (a) bed
d. but puts (it) on (the) lampstand
e. so that those entering may see the light

###### v17
a. for there is nothing hidden
b. that will not become evident
c. and (there is) nothing secret
d. that will certainly not become known
e. indeed it will come into the open

###### v18
a. therefore pay attention how you listen and obey
b. for who ever has
c. to him will be given
d. and who ever does not have
e. also what he thinks to have
f. will be taken away from him

###### v19
a. now then His mother and brothers came to Him
b. yet they could not come near to Him because of the crowd

###### v20
a. then it was told to Him
b. Your mother and Your brothers stand outside
c. wanting to see You

###### v21
a. then He having replied said to them
b. these ones are My mother and My brothers
c. the ones hearing and doing the message of God

###### v22
a. now it happened on one of the days
b. then He embarked into (a) boat and also His disciples
c. and He said to them
d. let us go over to the other side of the lake
e. and then they set sail

###### v23
a. now while they were sailing He fell asleep
b. then (a) storm of wind came down onto the lake
c. and they were swamped with water
d. and they were in danger

###### v24
a. then having approached (Him)
b. they woke Him up
c. saying
d. Master Master we are dying
e. then He having been woken
f. rebuked the wind and the surge of water
g. so they ceased and it became calm

###### v25
a. and then He said to them
b. where (is) your faith?
c. now having feared they were amazed
d. saying to one another
e. who possibly is this One that
f. He also commands the winds and the water
g. and so they obey Him

###### v26
a. then they sailed toward the land of the Gerasenes
b. which is opposite Galilee

a. then to Him having gone out onto the land
b. a certain man from the town drew near
c. having demons
d. and for (a) long time he did not wear (a) garment
e. and he did not stay in (a) house
f. but among the graves

###### v28
a. then having seen Jesus
b. having cried out
c. he fell down in front of Him
d. and he said with (a) loud voice
e. what for me and for You Jesus Son of God the Most High?
f. I beg You
g. do not torment me

###### v29
a. because He commanded the evil spirit
b. to go out from the man
c. because many times he seized him
d. also he was bound with chains
e. and guarded closely with chains on the feet
f. then having broken the chains
g. he was driven along by the demon into deserted places

###### v30
a. then Jesus asked him
b. what is your name?
c. and He said Legion
d. because many demons went into him

###### v31
a. and then they pleaded with Him
b. that He would not command them to go away into the abyss

###### v32

c. and they asked Him earnestly that
d. He should allow them to go into them
e. and so He allowed them

###### v33

###### v36
