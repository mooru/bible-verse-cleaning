###### v2
a. look I Paul say to you that
b. if you are circumcised
c. Christ will be of no help to you

###### v3
a. and I declare again to each person being circumcised that
b. he is obliged to keep the whole Law

###### v4
a. you will be cut off from Christ
b. you who are justified by the Law
c. you no longer experienced grace

###### v5
a. because we by faith through (the) Spirit
b. eagerly expect (the) hope of righteousness

###### v6
a. because in Christ Jesus
b. neither circumcision has any ability
c. nor uncircumcision
d. but faith working through love

###### v7
a. you were progressing well
b. who prevented you (so as) not to obey [the] truth?

###### v8
a. that which persuaded (you was) not from the One calling you

###### v9
a. (a) little yeast adds yeast to the whole mixture

###### v10
a. I am convinced as to you in (the) Lord that
b. you will think nothing differently
c. but the one upsetting you
d. will suffer condemnation
e. who ever he may be

###### v11
a. but I fellow believers
b. if I still preach circumcision
c. why am I still persecuted?
d. then the offence of the cross would have ceased

###### v12
a. indeed I wish that those agitating you will cut themselves off

###### v13
a. yes you were called to freedom fellow believers
b. only not the freedom to the point of (an) excuse for the human nature
c. but through love serve one another

###### v14
a. for the whole Law is proclaimed by one statement
b. by namely you shall love your neighbour as yourself

###### v15
a. but if you harm and utterly destroy one another
b. beware (that) you are not destroyed by one another

###### v16
a. I now say
b. live by (the) Spirit
c. and you will never accomplish (the) desire of (the) sinful nature

###### v17
a. because the sinful nature desires against the Spirit
b. and the Spirit against the sinful nature
c. because these are hostile toward one another
d. so that what ever you do not want (to do)
e. those things you do

###### v18
a. but if you are led by (the) Spirit
b. you are not under the control of the Law

###### v19
a. now the works of the sinful nature are evident
b. these are
c. fornication immorality licentiousness

###### v20
a. idolatry sorcery enmities
b. discord jealousy furies
c. hostility discord division

###### v21
a. envy drunkenness orgies
b. and the things similar to these
c. as to which I warn you
d. just as I said before that
e. those doing such things
f. will not receive (the) kingdom of God

###### v22
a. but the outcome of the Spirit is
b. love joy peace
c. patience kindness goodness

###### v23
a. faithfulness gentleness self-control
b. against such things there is no law

###### v24
a. but those (who are) of Christ [Jesus]
b. crucified the sinful nature
c. with its passions and desires

###### v25
a. if we live by (the) Spirit
b. by (the) Spirit also we will behave

###### v26
a. let us not be falsely proud
b. provoking one another
c. being envious of one another
