###### v1
a. now I Paul myself ask you earnestly
b. on account of the meekness and gentleness of Christ
c. (I) the one who in person on the one hand (is) gentle among you
d. on the other hand being absent I am bold among you

###### v2
a. I then plead that not being present to be bold in confidence
b. by which I think to dare against some
c. those thinking about us
d. that we live according to (the) physical nature

###### v3
a. for while living in (the) physical nature
b. we do not engage in war according to (the) physical nature

###### v4
a. for the weapons of our warfare (are) not natural
b. but powerful for God
c. for tearing down fortresses
d. destroying false reasonings

###### v5
a. indeed every arrogance rising up against the knowledge of God
b. and taking control of every thought
c. for the purpose of obedience to Christ

###### v6
a. and being in a state of readiness
b. to punish every disobedience
c. when your obedience is made complete

###### v7
a. you look at outward appearances
b. if someone is convinced in himself to be of Christ
c. let this one consider again concerning himself that
d. just as (he) himself (is) of Christ
e. likewise also we (are)

###### v8
a. [and] if indeed I would boast about something excessive
b. about our authority
c. which the Lord gave for building up
d. and not for tearing you down
e. I will not be shamed

###### v9
a. so that I may not seem
b. as if (I) would terrify you through my letters

###### v10
a. (some say) that on the one hand the letters imply (to be) fierce and severe
b. but on the other hand the presence of the person (is) weak
c. and the message is despising

###### v11
a. let such one consider this that
b. just like we are by word through letters being absent
c. like that (we are) also by deed being present

###### v12
a. because we would not dare to classify
b. or to compare ourselves to some
c. of those recommending themselves
d. but they measuring themselves by themselves
e. and comparing themselves by themselves
f. they do not understand

###### v13
a. but we will not boast beyond measure
b. but according to the measure of the rule
c. of which God has apportioned to us (a) measure
d. to reach as far as also you

###### v14
a. for not as having not come to you
b. we overextended ourselves
c. because we came also to you for the sake of the gospel of Christ

###### v15
a. not boasting beyond measure about another's labours
b. but having hope
c. while causing your faith to increase
d. to be held in high honour among you
e. in accordance with our rule for abundance

###### v16
a. to preach the gospel in the (areas) beyond you
b. not to have boasted about another's area (that has already) been prepared

###### v17
a. now let the one boasting boast about (the) Lord

###### v18
a. because not the one commending himself
b. that one is regarded as worthy
c. but whom the Lord recommends
