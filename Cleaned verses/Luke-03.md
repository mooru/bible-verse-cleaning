###### v1
a. now in (the) fifteenth year of the rule of Tiberius Caesar
b. Pontius Pilate being governor of Judea
c. and Herod being tetrarch of Galilee
d. and Philip his brother being tetrarch of Iturea and (the) region of Trachonitis
e. and Lysanias being tetrarch of Abilene

###### v2
a. when Annas and Caiaphas (were) high priest
b. (a) word of God came to John the son of Zechariah in the wilderness

###### v3
a. and so he went into [the] whole region of the Jordan
b. preaching (a) baptism of repentance for forgiveness of sins

###### v4
a. as written in (the) book of (the) words of Isaiah the prophet
b. (a) voice of one shouting in the wilderness
c. make ready the way of (the) Lord
d. make His paths straight

###### v5
a. every valley shall be filled
b. and every mountain and hill shall be made low
c. and the crooked (roads) will become to be straight
d. and the rough (places) become to (be) smooth roads

###### v6
a. and all people shall see the salvation of God

###### v7
a. therefore he was saying to the multitudes coming out
b. to be baptised by him
c. brood of vipers who made known to you
d. to flee from the coming wrath?

###### v8
a. so then produce results worthy of repentance
b. and do not begin to say in yourselves
c. we have (a) father namely Abraham
d. indeed I say to you that
e. God is able
f. to raise from these stones children for Abraham

###### v9
a. further already also the axe lies at the root of the trees
b. every tree then not producing good fruit
c. is cut down and thrown into fire

###### v10
a. then the crowds asked him saying
b. what then shall we do?

###### v11
a. then having replied he said to them
b. the one having two coats
c. let him give to the one not having
d. also the one having food
e. let him do likewise

###### v12
a. now also tax collectors came to be baptised
b. and they said to him
c. teacher what shall we do?

###### v13
a. then he said to them
b. collect nothing more beyond what has been commanded to you

###### v14
a. then also soldiers asked him saying
b. what shall also we do?
c. then he said to them
d. you should extort nobody
e. and you should not make false charges
f. and be satisfied with your wages

###### v15
a. now the people being in expectation
b. and all were thinking carefully in their inner selves about John
c. whether he himself could be the Messiah

###### v16
a. John replied saying to all
b. I indeed baptise you with water
c. but the One greater than I is coming
d. of Whom I am not worthy to loosen the strap of His sandals
e. He will baptise you with (the) Holy Spirit and fire

###### v17
a. whose winnowing shovel (is) in His hand
b. to clean out His threshing floor
c. and to gather together the wheat into His barn
d. but the chaff He will burn with unquenchable fire

###### v18
a. so then also urging many other things
b. he told the people the good news

###### v19
a. but Herod the tetrarch being rebuked by him
b. because of Herodias the wife of his brother
c. and because of all (the) evil things that Herod did

###### v20
a. he added also this in addition to all
b. [indeed] he shut up John in prison

###### v21
a. now it happened when all the people were baptised
b. also Jesus having been baptised
c. and was praying
d. that the heaven was opened

###### v22
a. and the Holy Spirit descended on Him in (a) bodily form like (a) dove
b. and (a) voice from heaven came
c. You are My beloved Son
d. in You I took pleasure

###### v23
a. now Jesus was beginning when He was about thirty years (old)
b. being (the) son as was supposed
c. of Joseph the son of Eli
