###### v1
a. then having called the twelve together
b. He gave them power and authority over all the demons as well as to heal diseases

###### v2
a. then He sent them out
b. to preach the Kingdom of God
c. and to heal [those being ill]

###### v3
a. also He said to them
b. do not take anything for the journey
c. not (a) walking stick nor (a) traveller's bag nor bread nor money and not [each] to have two shirts

###### v4
a. then into what ever house you enter
b. stay there
c. and then go out from there

###### v5
a. and whoever would not receive you
b. going out from that town
c. shake off the dust from your feet
d. so as to be (a) witness against them

###### v6
a. so then going out they went through the villages
b. preaching the good news
c. and healing everywhere

###### v7
a. now Herod the tetrarch heard all the things happening
b. and he was perplexed
c. because of what is being said by some that
d. John was raised from (the) dead

###### v8
a. and by some that Elijah appeared
b. also (by) others that
c. some prophet of the ancient (times) came back to life

###### v9
a. but Herod said
b. I beheaded John
c. now who is this one about whom I hear such things?
d. and so he tried to see Him

###### v10
a. now the apostles having returned
b. told Him fully as much as they did
c. then taking them along
d. He withdrew privately into (a) town called Bethsaida

###### v11
a. now the crowd having known (it)
b. followed Him
c. then having welcomed them
d. He spoke to them about the Kingdom of God
e. and He healed those having need of healing

###### v12
a. now the day began to draw to a close
b. then the twelve having come said to Him
c. dismiss the crowd
d. so that having gone into the surrounding villages and farms
e. they may lodge
f. and that they may find food
g. because we are here in (a) deserted place

###### v13
a. then He said to them
b. you give them to eat
c. but they said
d. there are not to us more than five loaves and two fish
e. unless if at all we having gone could buy for this whole crowd food

###### v14
a. because (the) men were about five thousand
b. then He said to His disciples
c. let them sit down to eat in groups (of) [about] fifty each

###### v15
a. and they did so
b. so then they let them all sit down to eat

###### v16
a. then having taken the five loaves and the two fish
b. having looked up to the sky
c. he asked God to bless them
d. then He broke (the loaves) and gave to the disciples
e. to give the food to the crowd

###### v17
a. then they ate and all were satisfied
b. and then what was left over for them was picked up
c. twelve baskets of pieces

###### v18
a. now it happened while He was praying alone
b. His disciples were with Him
c. and He asked them saying
d. who do the crowd say I am?

###### v19
a. then they having answered said
b. John the Baptiser
c. but others (say) Elijah
d. and others (say) that
e. some prophet of the ancient (times) raised to life

###### v20
a. then He said to them
b. but you who do you say I am?
c. then Peter having replied said
d. the Messiah of God

###### v21
a. but He having commanded ordered them
b. to say this to no one

###### v22
a. saying that
b. the Son of Man must suffer much
c. and be rejected by the elders and chief priests and Law experts
d. and be killed
e. and then be raised on the third day

###### v23
a. then He said to all
b. if someone wants to come after Me
c. let him disregard himself
d. and let him take up his cross from day to day
e. and let him follow Me

###### v24
a. for who ever wants to save his life
b. he will lose it
c. but who ever would lose his life for My sake
d. this one will rescue it

###### v25
a. for what can (a) person accomplish
b. having gained the whole world
c. but having lost or forfeited himself?

###### v26
a. for who ever is ashamed of Me and My words
b. this one the Son of Man will be ashamed of
c. when He comes in His glory and of His Father and of the holy angels

###### v27
a. now I say to you truly
b. there are some of those standing here
c. who will certainly not experience death
d. until they would have seen the Kingdom of God

###### v28
a. now it happened about eight days after these words
b. [then] having taken along Peter and John and James
c. He went up on the mountain to pray

###### v29
a. and it happened while He was praying
b. the appearance of His face (became) different
c. and His clothing became dazzling bright

###### v30
a. now pay attention
b. two men spoke together with Him
c. who were Moses and Elijah

###### v31
a. these ones having appeared in glory
b. spoke about His death
c. which was about to happen in Jerusalem

###### v32
a. now Peter and those with him were becoming very sleepy
b. but having become fully awake
c. they saw His glory as well as the two men standing with Him

###### v33
a. then it happened while they depart from Him
b. Peter said to Jesus
c. Master it is good that we are here
d. now let us make three tents
e. one for You and one for Moses and one for Elijah
f. not knowing what he says

###### v34
a. now while he was saying these things
b. there was (a) cloud
c. and it cast a shadow upon them
d. then they were afraid when they entered into the cloud

###### v35
a. further there was (a) voice out of the cloud saying
b. this is My Son the chosen One
c. listen to Him

###### v36
a. then when the voice came
b. Jesus was found alone
c. and they kept quiet
d. further they told no one during those days
e. nothing of what they have seen

###### v37
a. now (it) happened on the next day
b. when they came down from the mountain
c. (a) large crowd met up with Him

###### v38
a. now pay attention
b. (a) man from the crowd shouted saying
c. Teacher I plead with You take notice of my son
d. because he is my only son

###### v39
a. now pay attention
b. (a) spirit takes hold of him
c. and immediately he shouts
d. and he throws him into a fit with foam
e. and he hardly goes away from him hurting him severely

###### v40
a. also I begged Your disciples that they cast it out
b. yet they could not

###### v41
a. then having replied Jesus said
b. o unbelieving and perverted generation
c. until when will I be with you?
d. and will I be patient with you?
e. bring your son here

###### v42
a. now still while he was coming
b. the demon knocked him to the ground
c. and threw (him) into a fit
d. then Jesus rebuked the evil spirit
e. and He healed the child
f. and so He gave him back to his father

###### v43
a. now they all were greatly amazed at the mighty power of God
b. now all marvelling at everything that He was doing
c. He said to His disciples

###### v44
a. you must put into your ears these words
b. indeed the Son of Man is set to be delivered into (the) hands of people

###### v45
a. yet they did not understand this statement
b. indeed it was hidden from them
c. so that they were not able to understand it
d. yet they were afraid to ask Him about this statement

###### v46
a. then (an) argument began among them
b. who of them would be (the) greater

###### v47
a. now Jesus having known the argument of their minds
b. having taken hold of (a) child
c. He let him stand with Himself

###### v48
a. then He said to them
b. who ever would receive this child because of My Name
c. accepts Me
d. and who ever would accept Me
e. accepts the One having sent Me
f. for the one least among you all
g. this one is being (the) great one

###### v49
a. then John having replied said
b. Master we saw someone in Your Name driving out demons
c. and we prevented Him
d. because he does not follow with us

###### v50
a. then Jesus said to him
b. do not prevent (him)
c. because who is not against you
d. is for you

###### v51
a. now it happened when the days of His ascension are completed
b. then He decided firmly to make His way into Jerusalem

###### v52
a. and so He sent messengers ahead of Him
b. then having gone they entered into (a) town of the Samaritans
c. so as to prepare for Him

###### v53
a. yet they did not welcome Him
b. because His person was travelling to Jerusalem

###### v54
a. now the disciples James and John having seen (this) said
b. Lord do You want that we call fire to come down from heaven
c. and destroy them?

###### v55
a. then having turned around He reprimanded them

###### v56
a. and so they left to another town

###### v57
a. now while they were going along on the road
b. someone said to Him
c. I will follow You where ever You go

###### v58
a. then Jesus said to Him
b. the foxes have holes
c. and the wild birds make nests
d. but the Son of Man does not have where He may lie down to rest

###### v59
a. then He said to another
b. follow Me
c. but he said
d. [Lord] allow me having gone first to take care of my father until his death

###### v60
a. then He said to him
b. that is not what I meant
c. but you having gone proclaim the Kingdom of God

###### v61
a. then also another said
b. I will follow You Lord
c. but first allow me to say goodbye to those in my family

###### v62
a. then Jesus said [to him]
b. no one placing his hand on the plough
c. and then looking to what is behind
d. is suitable for the Kingdom of God
