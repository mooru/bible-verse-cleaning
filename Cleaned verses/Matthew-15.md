###### v1
a. then came toward Jesus from Jerusalem Pharisees and experts in the Law saying

###### v2
a. who do Your disciples disobey the tradition of the elders?
b. because they do not wash [their] hands
c. whenever they eat bread

###### v3
a. but having answered He said to them
b. why do you also disobey the commandment of God
c. for the sake of your tradition?

###### v4
a. because God said
b. honour your father and your mother
c. and let the one reviling father or mother die by death

###### v5
a. but you say
b. who ever says to his father or to his mother
c. (a) gift (is) what ever help you may get from me

###### v6
a. he will certainly not honour his parents
b. indeed you disregard the message of God by means of your tradition

###### v7
a. hypocrites
b. rightly Isaiah prophesied about you saying

###### v8
a. this nation honours Me with their lips
b. but their inner being is far away from Me

###### v9
a. and they worship Me in vain
b. teaching doctrines (that are) commandments of people

###### v10
a. then having called the crowd to Him He said to them
b. listen and understand

###### v11
a. not the thing entering into the mouth
b. defiles the person
c. but the thing departing out of the mouth
d. this thing defiles the person

###### v12
a. then the disciples having approached says to Him
b. do You know that
c. the Pharisees having heard Your statement took offense?

###### v13
a. but He having replied said
b. every plant that My heavenly Father did not plant
c. it will be uprooted

###### v14
a. refuse to listen to them
b. they are blind guides [of blind people]
c. now when (a) blind leads (a) blind
d. both will fall into (a) ditch

###### v15
a. then Peter having replied said to Him
b. explain to us the parable [this]

###### v16
a. and then He said
b. are you also still without understanding?

###### v17
a. do you not understand that
b. everything entering into the mouth
c. moves on into the stomach
d. and is expelled into (a) toilet?

###### v18
a. but the things going out from the mouth
b. go out from the inner being
c. and these things defile the person

###### v19
a. for out of the inner being evil reasonings go out
b. murders adulteries fornications
c. thefts false testimonies blasphemies

###### v20
a. these are the ones defiling the person
b. but to eat with unwashed hands
c. do not defile the person

###### v21
a. then having gone out from there
b. Jesus went off to the regions of Tyre and Sidon

###### v22
a. now look
b. (a) Canaanite woman from those regions having gone out
c. shouted saying
d. have mercy on me Lord Son of David
e. my daughter is severely demon possessed

###### v23
a. but He replied to her not (a) word
b. then His disciples having approached (Him)
c. requested Him saying
d. send her away
e. for she shouts from behind us

###### v24
a. but He having answered said
b. I was not sent except for the lost sheep of (the) people of Israel

###### v25
a. but having come she prostated herself before Him saying
b. Lord  help me

###### v26
a. but He having replied said
b. it is not fitting to take the food of the children
c. and to throw (it) to the dogs

###### v27
a. but she said
b. yes Lord
c. yet indeed the dogs eat from the crumbs
d. the ones falling from the table of their owners

###### v28
a. then Jesus having replied said to her
b. o woman
c. great (is) your faith
d. let it be to you as you wish
e. and her daughter was healed from that hour

###### v29
a. then having departed from there
b. Jesus went along the sea of Galilee
c. and having gone up onto the mountain
d. He sat down there

###### v30
a. and (a) large crowd came to Him
b. having with themselves lame blind crippled deaf and mute
c. and also many others
d. and they put them down at His feet
e. and then He healed them

###### v31
a. so that the crowd was amazed
b. seeing (the) mute speaking
c. (the) crippled healthy
d. and (the) lame walking around
e. and (the) blind seeing
f. and then they glorified the God of Israel

###### v32
a. then Jesus having called to Himself His disciples said
b. I feel compassion for the crowd that
c. they are already staying on three days with Me
d. and they have not anything they could eat
e. and I do not want to let them go away quite hungry
f. lest they faint on the road

###### v33
a. then the disciples say to Him
b. from where (is) to us in (a) desert so much bread
c. so as to feed (a) crowd (of) so many?

###### v34
a. then Jesus says to them
b. how many loaves have you?
c. and they said
d. seven and (a) few small fish

###### v35
a. then having ordered the crowd to sit down on the ground

###### v36
a. he took the seven loaves and the fish
b. and having given thanks
c. He broke (them)
d. and He gave to the disciples
e. and the disciples (gave) to the crowd

###### v37
a. indeed they all ate
b. and they were satisfied
c. then they took up the left over of the pieces
d. seven baskets full

###### v38
a. and those having eaten were four thousand men
b. apart from women and children

###### v39
a. then having sent away the crowd
b. He embarked into the boat
c. and then He went to the region Magadan
