###### v1
a. Paul and Silvanus and Timothy
b. to the church of (the) Thessalonians
c. in union with God (the) Father and (the) Lord Jesus Christ
d. grace to you and peace

###### v2
a. we give thanks to God always for all of you
b. making mention in our prayers

###### v3
a. continuously remembering and mentioning
b. of your work of faith
c. and of the hard work of your love
d. and of the endurance of your hope
e. with regard to our Lord Jesus Christ
f. before our God and Father

###### v4
a. knowing beloved fellow believers
b. your selection by God

###### v5
a. because our gospel did not come among you
b. by means of merely speaking
c. but also by power
d. indeed by (the) Holy Spirit
e. yes [by] great complete certainty
f. just as you know
g. what kind of (people) we were [among] you for your sake

###### v6
a. surely you became our imitators
b. in fact (imitators) of the Lord
c. you having accepted the message
d. with much suffering
e. with joy coming from (the) Holy Spirit

###### v7
a. so that you became (an) example
b. to all those believing
c. (living) in Macedonia and in Achaia

###### v8
a. for by you the message of the Lord was proclaimed
b. not only in Macedonia
c. also [in] Achaia
d. certainly in every place
e. your faith with regard to God went forth
f. so that we do not have need to say anything

###### v9
a. for they themselves report about us
b. what kind of acceptance we had with you
c. and how you turned away from the idols to God
d. to serve (the) living and true God

###### v10
a. and to wait for His Son
b. from heaven
c. Whom He raised from [the] dead
d. Jesus the One rescuing us
e. from the coming punishment
