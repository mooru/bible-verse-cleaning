###### v1
a. and when He opened the seventh seal
b. there was silence in heaven about half an hour

###### v2
a. then I saw the seven angels who were standing before God
b. and to them seven trumpets were given

###### v3
a. also another angel came
b. and he stood at the altar
c. having (a) golden censer
d. and many incense offerings was given to him
e. so that he will put the prayers of all the people of God
f. upon the golden altar the one in front of the throne

###### v4
a. and smoke of the incense offerings went up
b. with the prayers of God's people
c. from (the) hand of the angel before God

###### v5
a. then the angel took the censer
b. and he filled it from the fire of the altar
c. and he threw (it) onto the earth
d. and then occurred thunders and sounds and lightnings and (an) earthquake

###### v6
a. now the seven angels the ones having the seven trumpets
b. prepared them so that they may blow the trumpets

###### v7
a. then the first trumpet sounded
b. and then occurred hail and fire
c. having been mixed with blood
d. and it was thrown onto the earth
e. and (a) third of the land  was burned up
f. also (a) third of the trees was burned up
g. indeed all green grass was burned up

###### v8
a. then the second angel blew the trumpet
b. and (something) like (a) big mountain burning with fire
c. was thrown into the sea
d. and (a) third of the sea became blood

###### v9
a. and (a) third of the creatures of those in the sea died
b. the ones having life
c. and (a) third of the boats were destroyed utterly

###### v10
a. then the third angel blew the trumpet
b. and (a) great star fell from the sky
c. burning like (a) torch
d. and it fell upon (a) third of the rivers
e. and upon the fountains of the waters

###### v11
a. and the name of the star is called Wormwood
b. and (a) third of the waters became to be bitter
c. and many of the people died because of the waters
d. because they were made bitter

###### v12
a. then the fourth angel blew the trumpet
b. and (a) third of the sun was struck
c. also (a) third of the moon
d. as well as (a) third of the stars
e. so that (a) third of them became dark
f. and the day would not shine for (a) third of it
g. also the night likewise

###### v13
a. then I looked
b. and I heard one eagle
c. flying in midheaven
d. saying with (a) loud voice
e. horror horror horror to those living on the earth
f. at the time of the other sounds of the trumpet
g. of the three angels the ones about to blow the trumpet
