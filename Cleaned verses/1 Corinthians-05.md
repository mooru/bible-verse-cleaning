###### v1
a. sexual immorality is actually heard about among you
b. yes such sexual immorality which (is) not even among the pagans
c. so that someone has (the) wife of his father

###### v2
a. in fact you are very proud
b. indeed were you rather not at all sad
c. so that he should have been removed from among you
d. the one having done this deed?

###### v3
a. because I then being absent in the body
b. but being present in the way of thinking
c. like being present I have already decided (that)
d. the one thus having done this

###### v4
a. in the Name of [our] Lord Jesus
b. you and also my way of thinking having been gathered together
c. with the power of our Lord Jesus

###### v5
a. (that) the one of such a kind be delivered to Satan
b. for (the) destruction of his body
c. so that his inner being be saved on the day of the Lord

###### v6
a. your boasting (is) not fitting
b. do you not know that
c. (a) little yeast makes the whole batch of dough rise?

###### v7
a. clean out the old yeast
b. so that you may be (a) new batch of dough
c. in as much as you are without yeast
d. for indeed Christ was sacrificed (as) our Passover lamb

###### v8
a. so that we may celebrate
b. not with old yeast
c. and not with yeast of evil and of wickedness
d. but with (bread) without yeast of sincerity and of truth

###### v9
a. I wrote to you in my letter
b. not to associate with sexually immoral persons

###### v10
a. not at all (meaning) the sexually immoral persons of this world
b. or the greedy persons and robbers or worshippers of idols
c. because then you must go out of the world

###### v11
a. and so I wrote to you not to be associated
b. if someone being called (a) fellow believer
c. may be (a) sexually immoral person or (a) greedy     
d. or slanderer or drunkard or robber
e. also not to eat together with such a one

###### v12
a. certainly what (is it) for me to judge the outsiders?
b. do you surely not judge the insiders?

###### v13
a. now then God judges the outsiders
b. exclude the evil one from you yourselves
