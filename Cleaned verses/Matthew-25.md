###### v1
a. then the Kingdom of Heaven will be like ten young girls
b. who having taken their lamps
c. they went out for (the) meeting of the bridegroom

###### v2
a. now five of them were foolish and five wise

###### v3
a. because the foolish ones having taken their lamps
b. did not take oil with them

###### v4
a. but the wise ones took oil in containers along with their lamps

###### v5
a. then as the bridegroom was late
b. they all grew drowsy and were sleeping

###### v6
a. now in the middle of (the) night (a) shout came
b. look the bridegroom
c. go out for (the) meeting [of him]

###### v7
a. then all those young girls were woken up
b. and they adorned their own lamps

###### v8
a. then the foolish ones said to the wise ones
b. give to us from your oil
c. because our lamps are going out

###### v9
a. but the wise ones replied saying
b. never it will not at all be sufficient for us and you
c. go rather to those selling (oil)
d. and buy for yourselves

###### v10
a. now while they were going to buy
b. the bridegroom came
c. and those prepared went in with him to the wedding
d. and then the door was shut

###### v11
a. now later come also the other young girls saying
b. sir sir
c. open for us

###### v12
a. but he having answered said
b. truly I say to you
c. I do not know you

###### v13
a. be alert then
b. because you do not know the day neither the hour

###### v14
a. yes (it is) like (a) man leaving home on a journey
b. he called his personal slaves
c. and he handed over to them his possessions

###### v15
a. now to the one then he gave five talents
b. and to another two
c. and to another one
d. to each according to his own ability
e. and then he left home on a journey immediately

###### v16
a. being on his way the one having received the five talents
b. did business with them
c. and he gained five others

###### v17
a. likewise the one (having) the two
b. he gained two others

###### v18
a. but the one having received one
b. having gone he dug (in the) ground
c. and he hid the money of his master

###### v19
a. now after (a) long time the master of those slaves comes
b. and he checks (the) account with them

###### v20
a. now having come the one having received the five talents
b. presented to (him) five other talents
c. saying master
d. five talents you entrusted to me
e. look
f. five other talents I gained

###### v21
a. his master said to him
b. well done good and faithful slave
c. over (a) little you were faithful
d. over much I will appoint you
e. enter into the joy of your master

###### v22
a. [then] also having come the one (with) the two talents said
b. master
c. two talents you entrusted to me
d. look
e. two other talents I gained

###### v23
a. his master said to him
b. well done good and faithful slave
c. over (a) little you were faithful
d. over much I will appoint you
e. enter into the joy of your master

###### v24
a. now having come also the one having received the one talent
b. he said master
c. having known you that
d. you are (a) demanding man
e. harvesting where you did not sow
f. and gathering from where you did not scatter

###### v25
a. now having been afraid
b. I having gone hid your talent in the ground
c. look
d. you have yours

###### v26
a. then having answered his master said to him
b. worthless and lazy slave
c. you knew that
d. I harvest where I did not sow
e. and I gather from where I did not scatter?

###### v27
a. you should indeed have deposited my money with the bankers
b. and then I having come I would have been repaid mine with interest

###### v28
a. take then the talent away from him
b. and give (it) to the one having the ten talents

###### v29
a. indeed to each one being able
b. there will be given
c. yes he will be given more than enough
d. but from the one not being able
e. even what he has
f. will be taken away from him

###### v30
a. now then throw the useless slave out into the outer darkness
b. there will be weeping and gnashing of the teeth

###### v31
a. further when the Son of Man comes in His glory
b. and all the angels with Him
c. then He will sit on (the) throne of His glory

###### v32
a. and all the nations will be gathered together before Him
b. then He will separate them from each other
c. just as the shepherd separates the sheep from the goats

###### v33
a. yes He will put the sheep first among His right
b. and then the goats among (His) left

###### v34
a. then the King will say to those among His right
b. come here blessed ones of My Father
c. receive the Kingdom prepared for you since (the) creation of (the) world

###### v35
a. because I was hungry
b. and you gave Me to eat
c. I was thirsty
d. and you gave Me to drink
e. I was (a) stranger
f. and you took Me in

###### v36
a. (I was) naked
b. and you clothed Me
c. I was ill
d. and you took care of Me
e. I was in prison
f. and you came to Me

###### v37
a. then the righteous ones will answer Him saying
b. Lord
c. when did we see You being hungry
d. and we gave (You) food
e. or being thirsty
f. and we gave (You) to drink?

###### v38
a. now when did we see You (a) stranger
b. and we took (You) in
c. or naked
d. and we clothed? (You)

###### v39
a. and when did we see You being ill
b. or in prison
c. and we came to You?

###### v40
a. then having answered the King will say to them
b. truly I say to you
c. even as much as you did (it) to one of the least of these people of Mine
d. you did (it) to Me

###### v41
a. then He will also say to those among (His) left
b. go away from Me [the] cursed ones into the everlasting fire
c. the one prepared for the devil and for his angels

###### v42
a. because I was hungry
b. and you did not give Me to eat
c. I was thirsty
d. and you did not give Me to drink

###### v43
a. I was (a) stranger
b. and you did not take Me in
c. (I was) naked
d. and you did not clothe Me
e. (I was) ill
f. and in prison
g. and you did not visit Me

###### v44
a. then also they themselves will answer saying
b. Lord
c. when did we see You being hungry or thirsty or (a) stranger or naked or ill or in prison
d. and we did not take care of You?

###### v45
a. then it will be answered to them saying
b. truly I say to you
c. even as much as you did not do (it) to one of these least ones
d. you also did not do (it) to Me

###### v46
a. yes these ones will go away into everlasting punishment
b. but the righteous ones into eternal life
