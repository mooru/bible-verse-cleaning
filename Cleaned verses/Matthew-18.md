###### v1
a. at that time the disciples approached Jesus saying
b. who is possibly (the) greater in the Kingdom of Heaven?

###### v2
a. then having called (a) child to Him
b. He placed it in their midst

###### v3
a. and then He said
b. truly I say to you
c. if you do not change
d. and become like the children
e. you will by no means enter into the Kingdom of Heaven

###### v4
a. whoever then humbles himself like this child
b. this one is the greater in the Kingdom of Heaven

###### v5
a. and who ever accepts one such child on the basis of My Name
b. accepts Me

###### v6
a. and who ever causes to sin one of these little ones believing in Me
b. it is advantageous to him that
c. (a) donkey millstone be hung around his neck
d. and he be caused to sink in the deep of the sea

###### v7
a. horror to the world due to those causing sin
b. indeed causing sin is inevitable to happen
c. but horror to the person through whom the causing to sin happens

###### v8
a. but if your hand or your foot causes you to sin
b. cut it off
c. and throw (it) from you
d. it is better for you to enter into your life crippled or lame
e. than having two hands or two feet
f. to be thrown into the eternal fire

###### v9
a. and if your eye causes you to sin
b. take it out
c. and throw (it) from you
d. it is better for you to enter one-eyed into your life
e. than having two eyes
f. to be thrown into the hell of the fire

###### v10
a. see to it that you do not despise one of these little ones
b. for I say to you that
c. their angels in heaven always see the face of My Father in heaven

###### v12
a. what does it seem to you?
b. if a hundred sheep belong to a certain man
c. and one from them should wander about
d. will he not leave the ninety nine on the mountains
e. and having gone he seeks the one wandering about?

###### v13
a. and if he happens to find it
b. truly I tell you that
c. he rejoices over it more
d. than over the ninety nine not having wandered about

###### v14
a. thus it is not (the) will in the opinion of your Father in heaven that
b. one of these little ones should be lost

###### v15
a. now if your fellow believer sins [against you]
b. go rebuke him between you and him alone
c. if he listens to you
d. you gained your fellow believer

###### v16
a. but if he does not listen
b. take one or two along with you in addition
c. so that because of (the) speaking of two witnesses or of three every word may remain firmly

###### v17
a. but if he refuses to obey them
b. tell (it) to the church
c. but if also he refuses to obey the church
d. let him be to you like the heathen and the tax collector

###### v18
a. truly I say to you
b. what ever you prohibit on the earth
c. will be prohibited in heaven
d. and what ever you permit on the earth
e. will be permitted in heaven

###### v19
a. again [truly] I say to you that
b. if two agree among you on the earth
c. about every undertaking what ever they may ask for
d. it will happen to them  from My Father in heaven

###### v20
a. because where two or three are gathered together in My Name
b. there I am in their midst

###### v21
a. then Peter having approached said to Him
b. Lord  how often will my fellow being sin against me
c. and will I forgive him?
d. (is it) up to seven times?

###### v22
a. Jesus says to him
b. I do not say to you up to seven times
c. but up to seventy times seven

###### v23
a. because of this the Kingdom of Heaven  was compared to (a) man being (a) king
b. who wanted to check account with his slaves

###### v24
a. when he having begun to check
b. one debtor of ten thousand talents was brought to him

###### v25
a. then he not being able to pay
b. the master commanded that he be sold
c. and also his wife and his children
d. also everything whatever he has
e. and (that) payment be made

###### v26
a. the slave then having fallen down
b. prostrated himself before him saying
c. be patient with me
d. then I will pay you all

###### v27
a. now the master having felt compassion for that slave
b. set him free
c. and he cancelled the debt for him

###### v28
a. now that slave having gone out
b. he found one of his fellow slaves
c. who owed him one hundred denarii
d. and having seized him
e. he choked (him) saying
f. pay because of whatever you owe

###### v29
a. his fellow slave now having fallen down
b. pleaded with him saying
c. be patient with me
d. surely I will pay you

###### v30
a. but he was not willing
b. and having gone he threw him in jail
c. until he should pay what is owed

###### v31
a. his fellow slaves then having seen the things happened
b. they were greatly distressed
c. and having gone they explained to their master all the things having happened

###### v32
a. then having called him
b. his master says to him
c. wicked slave
d. all that debt I cancelled for you
e. because you asked me earnestly

###### v33
a. should also you not have mercy on your fellow slave
b. as also I had mercy on you?

###### v34
a. then his master having become angry
b. he handed him over to the prison guards
c. until he should pay all what is owed

###### v35
a. thus also My heavenly Father will do to you
b. if not each one forgives his fellow being from your inner selves
