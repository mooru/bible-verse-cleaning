###### v1
a. after these things having departed from Athens
b. he went to Corinth

###### v2
a. and having found a certain Jew by name Aquila
b. (a) Pontian as to race
c. having recently come from Italy
d. as well as Priscilla his wife
e. because Claudius had commanded
f. all the Jews to leave from Rome
g. he approached them

###### v3
a. and because of to be of the same trade
b. he was staying with them
c. and he was working
d. because they were tentmakers by occupation

###### v4
a. then he was arguing in the synagogue during every Sabbath
b. he was persuading Jews as well as Greeks

###### v5
a. so when Silas as well as Timothy arrived at land from Macedonia
b. Paul was continuing to apply himself to the message
c. testifying to the Jews
d. that Jesus is the Messiah

###### v6
a. but they being hostile
b. and slandering
c. having shaken out his clothes
d. he said to them
e. your blood (is) on your head
f. I (am) pure
g. from now on I am going to the Gentiles

###### v7
a. then having departed from there
b. he went into (the) house of someone by name Titius Justus
c. (who) was worshipping God
d. whose house was next to the synagogue

###### v8
a. now Crispus the leader of the synagogue
b. believed in the Lord with his whole household
c. and many of the Corinthians hearing
d. believed and were baptised

###### v9
a. so the Lord said in (the) night through (a) vision to Paul
b. do not fear
c. but speak
d. and do not be silent

###### v10
a. because I am with you
b. and no one will attack you
c. to hurt you
d. because there is for Me many people in this city

###### v11
a. so he remained for one year and six months
b. teaching the message of God among them

###### v12
a. now while Gallio was (the) proconsul of Achaia
b. the Jews attacked Paul with one mind
c. and they brought him to the judgment place

###### v13
a. saying that
b. this one persuades the people to worship God contrary to the Law

###### v14
a. but while Paul was about to start speaking
b. Gallio said to the Jews
c. if indeed it were some crime or wicked wrongdoing
d. o Jews
e. I would accepted a complaint from you

###### v15
a. but if it is (a) dispute
b. about (a) word and names and (the) law according to you
c. you see to it yourselves
d. I do not want to be (the) judge of these things

###### v16
a. and then he drove them away from the judgment place

###### v17
a. so all having taken hold of Sosthenes the leader of the synagogue
b. they were beating (him) in front of the judgment seat
c. in fact nothing of these things was a concern to Gallio

###### v18
a. then Paul still having stayed on for many days
b. having said goodbye to the fellow believers
c. he sailed away to Syria
d. and with him Priscilla and Aquila
e. having shaved his head in Cenchreae
f. because he made (a) vow

###### v19
a. they then arrived at Ephesus
b. and he left them there
c. then himself having gone into the synagogue
d. he argued with the Jews

###### v20
a. and they asking him
b. to stay for (a) longer period of time
c. he did not consent

###### v21
a. but having said goodbye
b. and having said
c. I will return to you again
d. God being willing
e. he set sail from Ephesus

###### v22
a. then having arrived at land at Caesarea
b. having gone up
c. and having greeted the church
d. he went down to Antioch

###### v23
a. and having spent some time
b. he departed
c. going on from one place to another in the region of Galatia and Phrygia
d. stengthening all the disciples

###### v24
a. then (a) certain Jew by name Apollos an Alexandrian
b. by nature (an) eloquent man
c. he arrived in Ephesus
d. being competent in the Scriptures

###### v25
a. this one was instructed in the Way of the Lord
b. and showing enthusiasm
c. he was speaking
d. and he was accurately teaching the things concerning Jesus
e. having known only the baptism of John

###### v26
a. and this one began to speak boldly in the synagogue
b. so Priscilla and Aquila having heard him
c. they led him aside
d. and they explained more accurately to him the Way [of God]

###### v27
a. and he intending to cross over to Achaia
b. the fellow believers having urged
c. they wrote to the disciples to receive him
d. he having arrived
e. he much helped those having believed through the grace

###### v28
a. because he was vigorously refuting the Jews publicly
b. proving by means of the Scriptures that Jesus is the Messiah
