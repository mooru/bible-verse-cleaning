###### v1
a. seek to attain love
b. and set your hearts on the things from the Spirit
c. but even more that you may prophesy

###### v2
a. because the one speaking in (an) ecstatic language
b. speaks not to people but to God
c. because nobody understands
d. but he speaks mysteries by the Spirit

###### v3
a. and the one prophesying
b. speaks to people strengthening and encouragement and consolation

###### v4
a. the one speaking in (an) ecstatic language strengthens himself
b. but the one prophesying strengthens (the) church

###### v5
a. now I like (that) you all speak in ecstatic languages
b. but even more that you may prophesy
c. now the one prophesying (is) greater than the one speaking in ecstatic languages
d. except however he would interpret
e. so that the church may receive strengthening

###### v6
a. but now fellow believers if I would come to you
b. speaking in ecstatic languages
c. what will I benefit you
d. unless I will speak to you
e. either by revelation or by knowledge
f. either by prophecy or [by] teaching?

###### v7
a. similarly lifeless things producing (a) sound
b. either (a) flute or (a) lyre
c. if it would not produce (a) distinction to the sounds
d. how will the flute playing or the lyre playing be known?

###### v8
a. and in fact if (a) trumpet would produce (an) indistinct sound
b. who will get ready for battle?

###### v9
a. likewise also you by means of the tongue
b. if you would not produce (an) intelligible word
c. how will it be known what is being said?
d. because you will be speaking into (the) air

###### v10
a. there are probably so many kinds of languages in (the) world
b. yet no one (is) without meaning

###### v11
a. if therefore I would not know the meaning of the sound
b. I will be (a) foreigner to the one speaking
c. also the one speaking (will be a) foreigner to me

###### v12
a. and so because you are enthusiasts of spirits
b. seek for the strengthening of the church
c. so that you may have a greater benefit

###### v13
a. therefore the one speaking in (an) ecstatic language
b. let him pray that he would translate

###### v14
a. [for] if I would pray in (an) ecstatic language
b. my inner being prays
c. but my mind is unproductive

###### v15
a. what then is it?
b. I will pray with my inner being
c. but I will also pray with my mind
d. I will sing praises with my inner being
e. but I will also sing praises with my mind

###### v16
a. because if you praise [with] (your) inner being
b. the one being in the role of the ordinary person
c. how will he say amen on the basis of your thanksgiving?
d. because he does not understand what you say

###### v17
a. you then indeed give thanks well
b. but the other one is not strengthened

###### v18
a. I thank God (that)
b. I speak in ecstatic languages more than all of you

###### v19
a. but in church I wish to speak five words with my mind
b. so that I will also teach others
c. than countless words in (an) ecstatic language

###### v20
a. fellow believers do not be children in your way of thinking
b. but be like a child in evil
c. and be mature in your way of thinking

###### v21
a. in the Law it has been written that
b. by speaking in strange languages
c. and by (the) lips of others
d. I will speak to this nation
e. and not even in this way will they listen to Me
f. says (the) Lord

###### v22
a. as a result ecstatic languages are for (a) sign
b. not for the believers but for the non-Christians
c. but prophecy (is a sign)
d. not for the non-Christians but for the believers

###### v23
a. if then the whole church gather together for the same purpose
b. and they all speak in ecstatic languages
c. and then ordinary persons or non-Christians would enter
d. will they not say that you are insane?

###### v24
a. further if all would prophesy
b. and then some non-Christian or ordinary person should enter
c. he is convicted by all
d. he is evaluated carefully by all

###### v25
a. the secrets of his inner self become evident
b. and in this way having fallen down on (his) face
c. he will worship God
d. reporting that
e. really God is among you

###### v26
a. what then is it fellow believers?
b. whenever you would gather together
c. each one has (a) song of praise
d. has (a) teaching
e. has (a) revelation
f. has (an) ecstatic language
g. has (an) interpretation
h. let all things be for strengthening

###### v27
a. if someone speaks in (an) ecstatic language
b. like two or at the most three
c. and then in succession
d. further let one interpret

###### v28
a. but if there is no interpreter
b. let him keep silent in church
c. and let him speak to himself and to God

###### v29
a. further let two or three prophets speak
b. and let the others evaluate carefully

###### v30
a. but if it was revealed to someone else sitting
b. let the first be silent

###### v31
a. because you all can prophesy one by one
b. so that all may learn
c. and all may be encouraged

###### v32
a. and let (the) inner being of prophets be subjected to prophets
b. because God is not of rebellion but of peace

###### v34
a. let the women be silent in the churches
b. because it is not allowed for them to speak
c. but let them comply
d. just as also the Law says

###### v35
a. but if they want to learn something
b. let they ask their own husbands questions at home
c. for it is disgraceful for (a) woman to speak in church

###### v36
a. or did the message of God go out from you
b. or did it reach to you only?

###### v37
a. if someone wants to be (a) prophet or (a) spiritual person
b. let him acknowledge what I write to you that
c. it is (a) command of (the) Lord

###### v38
a. and if someone fails to understand
b. let him be ignored

###### v39
a. as a result [my] fellow believers
b. set your heart on (it) to prophesy
c. and do not prevent the speaking in ecstatic languages

###### v40
a. but let everything be with propriety and in accordance with good order
