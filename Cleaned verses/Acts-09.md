###### v1
a. but Saul still breathing threat and murder
b. against the disciples of the Lord
c. having gone to the high priest

###### v2
a. he asked from him letters to the synagogues in Damascus
b. so that if he would find some of the Way being men as well as women
c. he might bring (them) bound to Jerusalem

###### v3
a. but while traveling
b. it happened that he approached Damascus
c. then suddenly (a) light from the sky flashed around him

###### v4
a. and having fallen to the ground
b. he heard (a) voice saying to him
c. Saul Saul why do you persecute Me?

###### v5
a. then he said
b. who are you Lord?
c. then He (said)
d. I am Jesus Whom you persecute

###### v6
a. but stand up and enter into the city
b. and it will be said to you what you must do for what reason

###### v7
a. now the men the ones traveling with him stood speechless
b. though hearing the voice
c. but seeing no one

###### v8
a. then Saul stood up from the ground
b. though his eyes having been opened
c. he was seeing nothing
d. then leading him by hand they took (him) into Damascus

###### v9
a. and for three days he was not being able to see
b. and he did not eat and did not drink

###### v10
a. now there was a certain disciple in Damascus by name Ananias
b. and the Lord said to him in (a) vision Ananias
c. and he said
d. certainly I (am here) Lord

###### v11
a. and the Lord (said) to him
b. having stood up go to the narrow street the one called Straight
c. then in (the) house of Judas look for (someone) by name Saul from Tarsus
d. pay attention then
e. he is praying

###### v12
a. and he saw (a) man [in (a) vision] Ananias by name
b. having come in and having placed [the] hands on him
c. so that he could see again

###### v13
a. but Ananias answered
b. Lord I heard from many about this man
c. how much harm he did to Your people in Jerusalem

###### v14
a. and here he has authority from the chief priests
b. to imprison all those upon whom Your Name is called

###### v15
a. but the Lord said to him
b. go because this one is (a) person of My choice
c. to make My Name known
d. before nations as well as kings and (the) people of Israel

###### v16
a. because I will show him
b. how much he must suffer for the sake of My Name

###### v17
a. so Ananias went away and came into the house
b. and having placed his hands on him he said
c. Saul fellow believer the Lord has sent me
d. Jesus the One having been seen by you
e. on the road by which you were coming
f. so that you may be able to see again and may be filled with (the) Holy Spirit

###### v18
a. then immediately there fell from his eyes (something) like scales
b. and he was able to see again
c. then having stood up he was baptised

###### v19
a. and having taken food he regained strength
b. and he was with the disciples in Damascus for some days

###### v20
a. then right away he preached Jesus in the synagogues
b. that He is the Son of God

###### v21
a. and all those hearing were greatly astonished and were saying
b. is not he the one having destroyed in Jerusalem
c. those calling upon this Name
d. and he had come here for this purpose
e. in order to bring them bound to the chief priests?

###### v22
a. then Saul was strengthened more
b. and he was causing [the] Jews consternation
c. the ones living in Damascus
d. showing for certain that this One is the Messiah

###### v23
a. and when many days were completed
b. the Jews plotted to kill him

###### v24
a. but their plot became known to Saul
b. further also they were closely watching the gates day as well as night
c. so that they could kill him

###### v25
a. but his disciples having taken (him) by night
b. they let him down through the city wall
c. lowering (him) in (a) large basket

###### v26
a. and having come into Jerusalem
b. he was trying to join the disciples
c. indeed all were afraid of him
d. not believing that he is (a) disciple

###### v27
a. but Barnabas having taken hold of him
b. brought (him) to the apostles
c. and he told them fully how he saw the Lord on the road
d. and that He spoke to him
e. and how in Damascus he spoke boldly in the Name of Jesus

###### v28
a. and he stayed with them
b. living among (them) in Jerusalem
c. speaking boldly in the Name of the Lord

###### v29
a. he was speaking as well as disputing against the Greek-speaking  Jews
b. and then they were trying to kill him

###### v30
a. but the fellow believers having found out
b. brought him down to Caesarea
c. and they sent him away to Tarsus

###### v31
a. so the church then throughout the whole Judea and Galilee and Samaria had peace
b. being made more able
c. and living in reverence of the Lord
d. and by the encouragement of the Holy Spirit (the church) was increasing greatly

###### v32
a. then it happened (that) Peter while travelling around through all
b. he also came down to God's people the ones living in Lydda

###### v33
a. and he found there a certain man by name Aeneas
b. lying for eight years on (a) bed
c. who was paralysed

###### v34
a. and then Peter said to him
b. Aeneas Jesus Christ heals you
c. stand up and make your bed for yourself
d. then immediately he got up

###### v35
a. and all those living in Lydda and Sharon saw him
b. they who turned to the Lord

###### v36
a. further in Joppa there was a certain disciple by name Tabitha
b. which translated means Dorcas
c. she was full of good works and acts of charity which she was doing

###### v37
a. and it happened in those days that she having been ill died
b. and having washed (her) they placed [her] in (the) upstairs room

###### v38
a. now Lydda being near Joppa
b. the disciples having heard that Peter is in it
c. they sent two men to him pleading
d. you should not delay to come to us

###### v39
a. and having stood up Peter went with them
b. he having come
c. they led (him) up to the upstairs room
d. and all the widows stood beside him
e. weeping and showing tunics and clothes
f. that Dorcas made being with them

###### v40
a. then Peter having sent all outside
b. and having knelt down prayed
c. and having returned to the body he said
d. Tabitha rise to life
e. then she opened her eyes
f. and having seen Peter she sat up

###### v41
a. then having given her (a) hand he caused her to stand up
b. and having called God's people and the widows
c. he presented her alive
