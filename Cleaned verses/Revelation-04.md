###### v1
a. after these things I looked
b. now pay attention
c. (a) door having been opened in the heaven
d. and the first voice that I heard
e. like (a) trumpet speaking with me saying
f. come up here
g. and I will show you what must happen after these things

###### v2
a. immediately I was in (the) Spirit
b. now pay attention
c. (a) throne was in heaven
d. and on the throne One sitting

###### v3
a. and the One sitting (was) similar in appearance to (a) jasper and carnelian stone
b. further (there was a) circle of light around the throne
c. similar in appearance to one made of emerald

###### v4
a. and around the throne twenty four thrones
b. and on the thrones twenty four elders sitting
c. having been clothed in white garments
d. and on their heads golden crowns

###### v5
a. and going out of the throne lightnings and sounds and thunders
b. and seven lamps of fire burning in front of the throne
c. which are the seven spirits of God

###### v6
a. and before the throne like (a) sea of glass just like crystal
b. and in the middle of the throne and around the throne four living beings
c. being full of eyes on the front and on the back

###### v7
a. now the first living being similar to (a) lion
b. and the second living being similar to (a) calf
c. and the third living being having the face like (a) man
d. and the fourth living being similar to (an) eagle flying

###### v8
a. further the four living beings one by one of them having each six wings
b. on the outside and on the inside they were full of eyes
c. and they have no stop day and night saying
d. holy holy holy (is the) Lord God Almighty
e. the One (that) was and the One being and the One coming

###### v9
a. and when the living beings will give
b. glory and honour and thanksgiving
c. to the One sitting on the throne
d. to the One living forever and ever

###### v10
a. the twenty four elders will fall down
b. in front of the One sitting on the throne
c. and they will worship the One living forever and ever
d. and they will put their crowns in front of the throne saying

###### v11
a. You are worthy our Lord and God
b. to receive the glory and the honour and the power
c. because You created everything
d. indeed by Your will they existed and were created
