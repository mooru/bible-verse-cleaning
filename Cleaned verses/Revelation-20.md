###### v1
a. further I saw (an) angel coming down from heaven
b. holding the key of the abyss
c. and (a) large chain in his hand

###### v2
a. then he seized the dragon
b. the ancient snake
c. who is (the) devil and also Satan
d. then he bound him (a) thousand years

###### v3
a. and he threw him into the abyss
b. and he shut and sealed (it) above him
c. so that he may not anymore deceive the nations
d. until the thousand years came to an end
e. after these things he must be loosened for (a) short time

###### v4
a. further I saw thrones
b. and they sat upon them
c. indeed authority to judge was given to them
d. also (I saw) the souls of those having been beheaded
e. because of the testimony of Jesus
f. and also because of the message of God
g. and whoever did not worship the beast
h. and not his image
i. and did not receive the mark on the forehead
j. and on their hands
k. surely they became alive again and reigned with Christ a thousand years

###### v5
a. the rest of the dead did not become alive again
b. until the thousand years came to an end
c. this (is) the first resurrection

###### v6
a. fortunate and holy (is) the one having part in the first resurrection
b. over these ones the second death has no power
c. certainly they will be priests of God and of Christ
d. and they will reign with Him [the] thousand years

###### v7
a. then when the thousand years came to an end
b. Satan will be loosened from his prison

###### v8
a. and then he will go out to deceive the nations
b. the ones at the four corners of the earth
c. the Gog and Magog
d. to gather them together for the battle
e. of which their number (is) like the sand of the sea

###### v9
a. and they went up onto the breadth of the earth
b. then they surrounded the camp of God's people
c. and also the beloved city
d. and then fire came down out of heaven
e. and destroyed them utterly

###### v10
a. then the devil the one deceiving them
b. was thrown into the lake of fire and sulphur
c. where also the beast and the false prophet (were)
d. then they will be tormented day and night forever and ever

###### v11
a. then I saw (a) great white throne
b. and also the One sitting upon it
c. where from His presence the earth and the sky fled
d. indeed (a) place for them was not found

###### v12
a. then I saw the dead
b. the important ones and the unimportant ones
c. standing before the throne
d. and then (the) books were opened
e. also another book was opened
f. that is the one of life
g. then the dead were judged
h. by means of the things written in the books
i. according to their deeds

###### v13
a. then the sea gave up the dead the ones in it
b. also death and the world of the dead gave up the dead the ones in them
c. and each according to their deeds were judged

###### v14
a. then death and the world of the dead were thrown into the lake of fire
b. this death is the second one
c. the lake of fire

###### v15
a. then if someone was not found
b. having been written in the book of life
c. he was thrown into the lake of fire
