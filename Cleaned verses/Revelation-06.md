###### v1
a. now I saw when the Lamb opened one of the seven seals
b. and I heard one of the four living beings
c. speaking like (a) voice of thunder
d. come

###### v2
a. indeed I saw
b. now pay attention
c. (there was a) white horse
d. and the one sitting upon it
e. holding (a) bow
f. and (a) crown was given to him
g. and he went out conquering
h. indeed so that he may conquer

###### v3
a. and when He opened the second seal
b. I heard the second living being saying
c. come

###### v4
a. and another horse came out (a) fiery red one
b. and to the one sitting upon it
c. to him was given to take the peace from the earth
d. indeed so that they will slaughter one another
e. and (a) large sword was given to him

###### v5
a. and when He opened the third seal
b. I heard the third living being saying
c. come
d. and I saw
e. now pay attention
f. (there was a) black horse
g. and the one sitting upon it
h. holding (a) balance scale in his hand

###### v6
a. and I heard like (a) voice
b. in the midst of the four living beings saying
c. (a) quart of wheat for (a) coin
d. and three quarts of barley for (a) coin
e. yet do not harm the olive oil and the wine

###### v7
a. then when He opened the fourth seal
b. I heard (the) voice of the fourth living being saying
c. come

###### v8
a. and I saw
b. now pay attention
c. (there was a) pale greenish gray horse
d. and the one sitting upon it
e. (the) name for him (was) [the] death
f. and the power of death followed along with him
g. and power was given to them over the fourth part of the earth
h. to kill with sword and with famine
i. and by plague and by the animals of the earth

###### v9
a. then when He opened the fifth seal
b. I saw underneath the altar
c. the souls of those having been slain
d. for the sake of the message of God
e. and for the sake of the witness that they had

###### v10
a. then they cried out with (a) loud voice saying
b. until when Lord the Holy and True One
c. do You not judge and give justice for our blood
d. among those living upon the earth?

###### v11
a. then to them each (a) long white robe was given
b. and it was said to them that
c. they will remain for still (a) short time
d. until also the number of their fellow servants indeed their fellow believers would be complete
e. the ones being about to be killed just like also they

###### v12
a. then I saw when He opened the sixth seal
b. indeed (a) big earthquake happened
c. and the sun became black like (a) hairy sackcloth
d. and the whole moon became like blood

###### v13
a. and the stars of the sky fell upon the earth
b. like (a) fig tree drops its late figs
c. being shaken by (a) terrible wind

###### v14
a. and the sky was moved away like (a) book being rolled up
b. and every mountain and island were removed from their places

###### v15
a. then the kings of the earth and the very important persons and the commanding officers
b. as well as the rich and the powerful
c. also every slave and free person
d. hid themselves in the caves and in the rocky crags of the mountains

###### v16
a. then they say to the mountains and to the rocks
b. fall upon us
c. and hide us from (the) face of the One sitting upon the throne
d. and from the anger of the Lamb

###### v17
a. because the great day of their wrath came
b. and who is able to remain firmly?
