###### v1
a. then passing by
b. He saw (a) man blind since birth

###### v2
a. then His disciples asked Him saying
b. Teacher who sinned this man or his parents
c. so that he was born blind?

###### v3
a. Jesus answered
b. neither this man sinned
c. nor his parents
d. but so that the acts of God may be revealed in him

###### v4
a. we must do the works of the One having sent Me
b. while it is day
c. night comes
d. when no one can work

###### v5
a. as long as I am in the world
b. I am (the) light of the world

###### v6
a. having said this
b. He spat on the ground
c. and He made mud from the saliva
d. and then He rubbed the mud on his eyes

###### v7
a. and He said to him
b. go wash in the pool of Siloam
c. [that is translated having been sent out]
d. he then went off
e. and he washed himself
f. and he went being able to see

###### v8
a. so then the neighbours and those seeing him before that
b. he was (a) beggar
c. said
d. is this not the one sitting and begging?

###### v9
a. others said that
b. it is he
c. others said
d. not so but he is similar to him
e. that man was saying that
f. I am (the one)

###### v10
a. they then said to him
b. how [then] were your eyes opened?

###### v11
a. that man answered
b. the Man called Jesus made mud
c. and then He rubbed (it) on my eyes
d. and He said to me namely
e. go off to Siloam and wash
f. having gone then and washed
g. I gained sight

###### v12
a. then they said to him
b. where is that Man?
c. he says
d. I do not know

###### v13
a. they bring him to the Pharisees
b. the one formerly blind

###### v14
a. now it was (a) Sabbath on which day Jesus made the mud
b. and opened his eyes

###### v15
a. again then also the Pharisees asked him
b. how he gained sight
c. and he said to them
d. He put mud on my eyes
e. then I washed myself
f. and I can see

###### v16
a. then some from the Pharisees said
b. this Man is not from God
c. because He does not keep the Sabbath
d. [but] others said
e. how can (a) sinful man do such signs?
f. so there was (a) division among them

###### v17
a. they then say again to the blind man
b. what do you say about Him
c. because He opened your eyes?
d. and he said that
e. He is (a) prophet

###### v18
a. but the Jews did not believe about him that
b. he was blind and gained sight
c. until they called the parents of him having regained sight

###### v19
a. and they asked them saying
b. is this man your son
c. who you say that
d. he was born blind?
e. how then is he able to see now?

###### v20
a. his parents then answered and said
b. we know that
c. this man is our son
d. and that
e. he was born blind

###### v21
a. but how he is now able to see
b. we do not know
c. or who opened his eyes
d. we do not know
e. ask him
f. he is an adult
g. he will speak for himself

###### v22
a. this things his parents said
b. because they feared the Jews
c. for the Jews had already agreed that
d. if anyone would confess Him (as) Messiah
e. he will be expelled from the synagogue

###### v23
a. because of this his parents said that
b. he is an adult
c. ask him

###### v24
a. they then called the man for (a) second time
b. the one who was blind
c. and they said to him
d. promise before God to tell the truth
e. we know that
f. this Man is (a) sinner

###### v25
a. that man then answered
b. whether He is (a) sinner
c. I do not know
d. one thing I know that
e. being blind
f. now I am able to see

###### v26
a. they then said to him
b. what did He do to you?
c. how did He open your eyes?

###### v27
a. he answered them
b. I told you already
c. yet you did not listen
d. why do you want to hear again?
e. you do surely not also want to become His disciples?

###### v28
a. and then they slandered him and said
b. you are (a) disciple of that Man
c. but we are disciples of Moses

###### v29
a. we know that
b. God has spoken to Moses
c. but this Man we do not know from where He is

###### v30
a. the man answered and said to them
b. as to this indeed it is remarkable that
c. you do not know from where He is
d. yet He opened my eyes

###### v31
a. we know that
b. God does not pay attention to sinners
c. but if someone is devout
d. and does His will
e. He pays attention to that one

###### v32
a. since all time it was not heard that
b. someone opened the eyes of one having been born blind

###### v33
a. if this Man was not from God
b. He could not do anything

###### v34
a. they answered and said to him
b. you were completely born in sins
c. and you teach us?
d. then they drove him outside

###### v35
a. Jesus heard that
b. they drove him outside
c. and having found him He said
d. do you believe in the Son of Man?

###### v36
a. that man answered and said
b. indeed who is He Sir
c. so that I may believe in Him?

###### v37
a. Jesus said to him
b. surely you have seen Him
c. indeed the One speaking with you
d. that One is He

###### v38
a. and then he said
b. I believe Lord
c. and then he worshipped Him

###### v39
a. further Jesus said
b. for judgment I came into this world
c. so that those not seeing may see
d. and those seeing may become blind

###### v40
a. those from the Pharisees being with Him heard this
b. and so they said to Him
c. we are surely not also blind?

###### v41
a. Jesus said to them
b. if you were blind
c. you would not have had guilt
d. but now you say that
