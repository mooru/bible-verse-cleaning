###### v1
a. to the messenger of the church in Ephesus write
b. this says the One holding on to the seven stars in His right hand
c. the One walking among the seven golden lampstands

###### v2
a. I know your works and labour and your endurance
b. and that you can not endure evil ones
c. indeed you examined the ones calling themselves apostles
d. yet they are not
e. indeed you found them liars

###### v3
a. surely you have endurance
b. and you endured on behalf of My Name
c. yet you have not given up

###### v4
a. but I have against you that
b. you gave up your first love

###### v5
a. remember then from where you have fallen
b. and repent
c. and do the first works
d. but if not I am coming to you
e. and I will remove your lampstand from its place
f. if you would not repent

###### v6
a. yet you hold on to this namely
b. you hate the works of the Nicolaitans
c. which I also hate

###### v7
a. the one being able to hear let him pay attention to
b. what the Spirit says to the churches
c. to the one conquering
d. I will give to him to eat from the tree of life
e. which is in the paradise of God

###### v8
a. and to the messenger of the church in Smyrna write
b. this says the First and the Last
c. Who became dead and became alive again

###### v9
a. I know your hardship and your poverty
b. yet you are rich
c. and the blasphemy from those saying they themselves are Jews
d. yet they are not
e. but (they are a) synagogue of Satan

###### v10
a. fear nothing what you are about to suffer
b. pay attention
c. the devil is about to throw some of you into prison
d. so that you may be tested
e. and you will experience hardship for ten days
f. be faithful until death
g. and I will give to you the prize of life

###### v11
a. the one being able to hear let him pay attention to
b. what the Spirit says to the churches
c. the one conquering will not at all be hurt by the second death

###### v12
a. and to the messenger of the church in Pergamum write
b. these things says the One having the sharp two-edged sword

###### v13
a. I know where you live
b. where the throne of Satan (is)
c. indeed you hold on to My Name
d. and you did not deny your faith in Me
e. even in the days of Antipas
f. My witness My faithful one
g. who was killed among you
h. where Satan dwells

###### v14
a. but I have (a) few things against you namely
b. you have there ones holding on to the teaching of Balaam
c. who taught Balak
d. to bring about (a) trap before the people of Israel
e. to eat sacrificial meat
f. and to commit sexual immorality

###### v15
a. likewise also you have
b. those similarly holding on to the teaching of [the] Nicolaitans

###### v16
a. repent then
b. but if not
c. I am coming to you soon
d. and I will wage war against them with the sword of My mouth

###### v17
a. the one being able to hear
b. let him pay attention to what the Spirit says to the churches
c. to the one conquering I will give to him of the hidden manna
d. and I will give to him (a) white pebble
e. and on the pebble (a) new name having been written
f. that no one knows
g. except the one receiving (it)

###### v18
a. and to the messenger of the church in Thyatira write
b. these things says the Son of God
c. the One having his eyes like (a) flame of fire
d. and his feet like fine bronze

###### v19
a. I know your works
b. indeed your love and your faith
c. and your ministry as well as your endurance
d. and your last works (are) more appropriate than the first

###### v20
a. but I have against you that
b. you allow the woman Jezebel
c. the one calling herself (a) prophetess
d. indeed she teaches and deceives my servants
e. to commit sexual immorality
f. and to eat sacrificial meat

###### v21
a. and I gave her time
b. so that she may repent
c. yet she is not willing to repent from her immorality

###### v22
a. pay attention
b. I am making her sick
c. and those committing adultery with her
d. resulting in (a) great affliction
e. unless they would repent from her deeds

###### v23
a. also her children I will kill with death
b. then all the churches will know that
c. I am the One searching desires and minds
d. yes I shall give to you each according to your deeds

###### v24
a. further I speak to you the rest those in Thyatira
b. as many as do not hold on to this teaching
c. who did not acknowledge the deep secrets of Satan
d. as they call (it)
e. I do not put on you another burden

###### v25
a. except what you have
b. hold on to until I would arrive

###### v26
a. further the one conquering and obeying My works until (the) end
b. I will give to him authority to rule over the nations

###### v27
a. and he will rule them with (an) iron rod
b. just as clay containers are broken into pieces

###### v28
a. just as I also have received from My Father
b. also I will give to him the morning star

###### v29
a. the one being able to hear
b. let him pay attention to what the Spirit says to the churches
