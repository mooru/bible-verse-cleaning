###### v1
a. now having called to Him His twelve disciples
b. He gave to them authority over unclean spirits
c. in order to drive them out
d. and to heal every sickness and every disease

###### v2
a. now the names of the twelve apostles are these
b. first Simon the one called Peter
c. and Andrew his brother
d. and James the son of Zebedee
e. and John his brother

###### v3
a. Philip
b. and Bartholomew
c. Thomas
d. and Matthew the tax collector
e. James the son of Alphaeus
f. and Thaddaeus

###### v4
a. Simon the Zealot
b. and Judas the Iscariot
c. also the one who betrayed Him

###### v5
a. these twelve Jesus sent out
b. having instructed them saying
c. do not go off to (the) road of (the) Gentiles
d. and do not enter into (a) city of Samaritans

###### v6
a. but go rather to the lost sheep of the people of Israel

###### v7
a. and going preach saying that
b. the Kingdom of Heaven has come near

###### v8
a. heal those being sick
b. raise to life (the) dead
c. cleanse lepers
d. drive out demons
e. you received without cost
f. give without cost

###### v9
a. acquire not gold and not silver and not copper money in your belts

###### v10
a. no bag for (the) journey and not two coats and not sandals and not (a) stick
b. because the worker (is) worthy of his food

###### v11
a. and into what ever town or village you enter
b. find out who in it is worthy
c. and there stay until you would depart

###### v12
a. and then (when) entering into the house greet it

###### v13
a. and if then the family  is worthy
b. let your peace come upon it
c. but if it is not worthy
d. let your peace turn back to you

###### v14
a. and who ever does not welcome you
b. and do not listen to your words
c. going away out of that house or town
d. shake off the dust of your feet

###### v15
a. truly I say to you
b. it will be more endurable for (the) people of Sodom and Gomorrah on (the) day of judgment
c. than for that town

###### v16
a. look
b. I send you out like sheep in the midst of wolves
c. be then wise as the snakes
d. and (be) pure like the doves

###### v17
a. now be on your guard because of people
b. because they will hand you over to councils
c. and in their synagogues they will flog you

###### v18
a. even before governors and also kings you will be brought for My sake
b. for testimony to them and to the Gentiles

###### v19
a. further when they hand you over
b. do not worry how or what you should say
c. for it will be given to you in that hour what you should say

###### v20
a. because you are not the ones speaking
b. but the Spirit of your Father (is) the One speaking in you

###### v21
a. in addition brother will hand over brother to death
b. and father (will hand over) son
c. and children will rise up against parents
d. and they will have them killed

###### v22
a. and you will be hated by all on account of My Name
b. but the one having endured to (the) end
c. this one will be saved

###### v23
a. and when they persecute you in this town
b. flee to the other
c. because truly I say to you
d. you will by no means complete the towns of Israel
e. until the Son of Man would come

###### v24
a. (a) pupil is not above the teacher
b. and (a) slave not above his master

###### v25
a. (it is) sufficient for the pupil that
b. he should be like his teacher
c. and the slave like his master
d. if they called the master of the household Beelzebul
e. how much more his family members

###### v26
a. be then not afraid of them
b. because nothing is covered up
c. that will not be revealed
d. and (nothing is) secret
e. that will not be made known

###### v27
a. what I say to you in the dark
b. tell (it) in the light
c. and what you hear in secret
d. announce (it) in public

###### v28
a. and do not be afraid of those killing the body
b. but not being able to kill the soul
c. but fear rather the One being able
d. to destroy both soul and body in hell

###### v29
a. are not indeed two sparrows sold for a penny?
b. yet not one of them will fall on the ground without your Father

###### v30
a. further of you also the hair of your head are all counted

###### v31
a. do not then be afraid
b. you are worth more than many sparrows

###### v32
a. everyone then whoever will confess of Me before the people
b. also I will confess of him before My Father the One in [the] heaven

###### v33
a. but whoever would deny Me before the people
b. also I will deny him before my Father the One in [the] heaven

###### v34
a. do not think that
b. I came to bring about peace on the earth
c. I came not to bring about peace but (a) conflict

###### v35
a. for I came to cause revolt
b. (a) man against his father
c. and (a) daughter against her mother
d. and (a) daughter-in-law against her mother-in-law

###### v36
a. and (the) enemies of (a) man (will be) his relatives

###### v37
a. the one loving father or mother more than Me
b. is not worthy of Me
c. and the one loving son or daughter more than Me
d. is not worthy of Me

###### v38
a. and who does not take his cross
b. and follow after Me
c. is not worthy of Me

###### v39
a. the one finding his life
b. will lose it
c. yet the one having lost his life because of Me
d. he will find it

###### v40
a. the one receiving you
b. receives Me
c. and the one receiving Me
d. receives the One having sent Me

###### v41
a. the one receiving (a) prophet in view that (he) is (a) prophet
b. will receive (the) reward of (a) prophet
c. and the one receiving (a) righteous one in view that (he) is (a) righteous one
d. will receive (the) reward of (a) righteous one

###### v42
a. and who ever gives to drink one of these least ones only (a) cup of cold (water) in view that (he) is (a) disciple
b. truly I say to you
c. by no means he will lose his reward

###### v43
a. and then it happened when Jesus finished instructing His twelve disciples
b. He departed from there to teach and preach in their towns
