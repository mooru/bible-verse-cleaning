###### v1
a. do not judge
b. so that you may not be judged

###### v2
a. for by which judgment you judge
b. you will be judged
c. and by which measure you apportion
d. it will be apportioned to you

###### v3
a. and why (do) you see the speck in the eye of your neighbour
b. but the beam in your eye you are not concerned about?

###### v4
a. or how will you say to your neighbour
b. stop
c. let me take out the speck from your eye
d. and look
e. the beam (is) in your eye?

###### v5
a. hypocrite
b. take out first the beam from your eye
c. and then you will see clearly
d. to take out the speck from the eye of your neighbour

###### v6
a. give not what (is) holy to the dogs
b. and throw not your pearls before the pigs
c. so that they not trample them with their feet
d. and having turned around they rip you apart

###### v7
a. ask
b. and it will be given to you
c. seek
d. and you will find
e. knock
f. and it will be opened to you

###### v8
a. because everyone asking
b. receives
c. and the one seeking
d. finds
e. and to the one knocking
f. it will be opened

###### v9
a. or which person is among you
b. whom his son will ask for food
c. he will surely not give him (a) stone?

###### v10
a. or even he will ask for (a) fish
b. he will surely not give him (a) snake?

###### v11
a. if then you being wicked
b. you know how to give good gifts to your children
c. how much more your Father in heaven
d. will give good things
e. to those asking Him

###### v12
a. everything then what ever you want that
b. people do to you
c. so also you do to them
d. for this is the Law and the Prophets

###### v13
a. enter through the narrow gate
b. because wide (is) the gate
c. and broad the road
d. the one leading to destruction
e. and many are the ones going in through it

###### v14
a. because narrow (is) the gate
b. and difficult the road
c. the one leading to the life
d. and there are few finding it

###### v15
a. be alert because of the false prophets
b. who come to you in the clothing of sheep
c. but within themselves they are vicious wolves

###### v16
a. from their deeds you will recognise them
b. surely people gather not grapes from thorn bushes
c. or figs from thistles?

###### v17
a. thus every good tree produces good fruit
b. but the bad tree produces bad fruit

###### v18
a. (a) good tree can not produce bad fruit
b. and (a) bad tree (can) not produce good fruit

###### v19
a. every tree not producing good fruit
b. is cut down
c. and is thrown into fire

###### v20
a. so indeed from their fruit you will recognise it

###### v21
a. not everyone saying to Me
b. Lord Lord
c. will enter into the Kingdom of Heaven
d. but the one doing the will of My Father in heaven

###### v22
a. many will say to Me on that day
b. Lord Lord
c. did we not prophesy in Your Name
d. and in Your Name we drove out demons
e. and in Your Name we did many mighty deeds?

###### v23
a. and then I will declare to them that
b. I never knew you
c. go away from Me
d. (you) the ones doing lawlessness

###### v24
a. each then whoever obeys these words of Mine
b. and does them
c. will be made similar to (a) wise man
d. who built his house on the rock

###### v25
a. and the rain came down
b. and the rivers came
c. and the winds blew
d. and struck against that house
e. and it fell not
f. because it was founded upon the rock

###### v26
a. indeed each one listening to these words of Mine
b. and not doing them
c. will be made similar to (a) foolish man
d. who built his house on the sand

###### v27
a. and the rain came down
b. and the rivers came
c. and the winds blew
d. and struck against that house
e. and it fell
f. and its destruction was great

###### v28
a. and it happened
b. when Jesus finished these words
c. the crowds were greatly astounded at His teaching

###### v29
a. because He was teaching them as one having authority
b. and not like their Law experts
