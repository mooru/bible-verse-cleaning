###### v1
a. does someone of you dare (a) lawsuit
b. having (it) against the other
c. to be judged by the unbelievers
d. and not indeed by God's people?

###### v2
a. or do you not know that
b. God's people will judge the people of the world?
c. now if the world is judged by you
d. are you unworthy of (the) least important cases?

###### v3
a. do you not know that
b. we will judge angels
c. why not indeed things of daily life?

###### v4
a. indeed therefore whenever you have lawsuits of daily life
b. those having been despised by the church
c. do you appoint these ones?

###### v5
a. I say to you in order to shame
b. does not thus no one exist among you (who is) wise
c. who will be able to judge carefully between his fellow believer?

###### v6
a. but (a) fellow believer stands trial against (a) fellow believer
b. and this before unbelievers?

###### v7
a. already indeed [therefore] it is completely (a) failure for you that
b. you have lawsuits against one another
c. why not indeed are you instead mistreated?
d. why not indeed are you instead defrauded?

###### v8
a. but you act unjustly and you defraud
b. and this to fellow believers

###### v9
a. or do you not know that
b. unjust ones will not receive (the) Kingdom of God?
c. do not be deceived
d. not sexually immoral persons not idolaters not adulterers not passive  

###### v10
a. not thieves not greedy persons
b. not drunkards not slanderers not robbers
c. will receive (the) Kingdom of God

###### v11
a. indeed you were these someones
b. but you were purified
c. but you were made morally pure
d. but you were justified by the Name of the Lord Jesus Christ
e. and by the Spirit of our God

###### v12
a. everything is allowed for me
b. but not everything is advantageous
c. everything is allowed for me
d. but I will not be ruled by anything

###### v13
a. food (is) for the belly and the belly (is) for food
b. but God will put an end to both this one and that one
c. further the body (is) not for sexual immorality but for the Lord
d. indeed the Lord (is) for the body

###### v14
a. now God indeed raised the Lord to life
b. and He will raise us to life by His power

###### v15
a. do you not know that
b. your bodies are members of Christ?
c. having taken then the members of Christ
d. shall I make (them) members of (a) prostitute?
e. may it never happen

###### v16
a. [or] do you not know that
b. the one joining himself to (a) prostitute
c. is one body?
d. because He says they will be
e. the two into one body

###### v17
a. further the one joined to the Lord
b. is one spirit

###### v18
a. avoid sexual immorality
b. every sin what ever (a) person does
c. is outside of the body
d. but the one commiting sexual immorality
e. sins against his own body

###### v19
a. or do you not know that
b. your body is (a) temple of the Holy Spirit in you
c. Whom you have from God
d. and you are not of yourself?

###### v20
a. indeed you were redeemed at (a) price
b. then glorify God in your body
