###### v1
a. I then encourage you fellow believers
b. on account of the mercies of God
c. to cause your physical beings to be (a) living sacrifice
d. dedicated (and) pleasing to God
e. (this is) your true worship

###### v2
a. and do not shape your behaviour to this world
b. but be changed by the renewing of your way of thinking
c. so that you can test what (is) the will of God
d. His good and pleasing and perfect (will)

###### v3
a. for I say (this)
b. by means of the grace given to me
c. to everyone being among you
d. not to think highly of yourselves more than one ought to think
e. but to think so as to be sensible
f. as God gave to everyone (a) measure of faith

###### v4
a. for just as we have many parts in one body
b. but all the parts do not have the same function

###### v5
a. so in Christ we the many are one body
b. and members with regard to one another

###### v6
a. and having different gifts
b. according to the grace given to us
c. if prophecy in accordance with the proportion of faith

###### v7
a. if service in our service
b. if one teaching in his teaching

###### v8
a. if one encouraging in his encouragement
b. one sharing in generosity
c. one being active in helping with eagerness
d. one showing mercy with cheerfulness

###### v9
a. love (must be) without pretence
b. hating the evil
c. clinging to the good

###### v10
a. warmly devoted to one another with love for a fellow believer
b. showing one another eagerness in honour

###### v11
a. not lacking in zeal
b. being very enthusiactic
c. serving the Lord

###### v12
a. rejoicing in hope
b. enduring trouble and suffering
c. devoting yourself to prayer

###### v13
a. contributing to the needs of God's people
b. striving for hospitality to strangers

###### v14
a. bless those persecuting [you]
b. bless and do not curse

###### v15
a. rejoice with those rejoicing
b. weep with those weeping

###### v16
a. having the same attitude to one another
b. not being arrogant
c. but associating with the humble
d. do not be wise in your own opinion

###### v17
a. not returning anyone evil for evil
b. thinking ahead (what is) good in the opinion of all people

###### v18
a. if possible by you living in peace with all people

###### v19
a. not revenging yourselves beloved
b. but allow (the) possibility for punishment
c. because it is written
d. revenge (is) Mine
e. I will repay
f. says (the) Lord

###### v20
a. but if your enemy is hungry
b. give him (something) to eat
c. if he is thirsty
d. give him (something) to drink
e. for doing this you will heap coals of fire on his head

###### v21
a. do not be conquered by evil
b. but conquer evil with good
