###### v1
a. now about which you wrote
b. (it is) good for (a) man not to get married

###### v2
a. but because of sexual immorality
b. let each man have his own wife
c. and let each woman have her own husband

###### v3
a. let the husband do his duty to his wife
b. and likewise also the wife to her husband

###### v4
a. the wife does not rule over her own body
b. but her husband (does)
c. and likewise also the husband does not rule over his own body
d. but his wife (does)

###### v5
a. do not deprive one another
b. unless if at all (it) would (be) by agreement for (a) time
c. so that you may give time to prayer
d. then again you may be at the same
e. so that Satan may not tempt you due to your lack of self-control

###### v6
a. now this I say by concession
b. not by command

###### v7
a. now I desire (that) all people be like myself indeed
b. yet each has his own gift from God
c. the one then like this and the other like that

###### v8
a. now I say to the unmarried and to the widows
b. (it is) good for them if they remain like indeed I

###### v9
a. but if they do not exercise self-control
b. let them be married
c. for it is better to marry than to burn with passion

###### v10
a. further to those having been married I command
b. not I but the Lord
c. (a) wife is not to be divorced from (her) husband

###### v11
a. but further if she has been divorced
b. let she remain unmarried
c. or let she be reconciled to her husband
d. also (a) husband is not to divorce (his) wife

###### v12
a. now to the others say I not the Lord
b. if some fellow believer has (a) non-Christian wife
c. and she agrees to live with him
d. let him not divorce her

###### v13
a. also if any woman has (a) non-Christian husband
b. and he agrees to live with her
c. let her not divorce her husband

###### v14
a. because the non-Christian husband is dedicated to God because of his wife
b. and the non-Christian wife is dedicated to God because of the    
c. because consequently your children are ritually unacceptable
d. but now they are dedicated

###### v15
a. but if the non-Christian divorces
b. let the one divorce
c. the Christian brother or the sister has not been bound by such things
d. and so God has called you (to live) in peace

###### v16
a. because what do you know wife
b. whether you will save your husband?
c. or what do you know husband
d. whether you will save your wife?

###### v17
a. however as the Lord assigned to each
b. each as God has called
c. let the one behave in this way
d. yes thus I command in all the churches

###### v18
a. someone was called having been circumcised
b. let him not conceal circumcision
c. someone was called in uncircumcision
d. let him not be circumcised

###### v19
a. the circumcision is nothing
b. also the uncircumcision is nothing
c. but obedience of (the) commandments of God

###### v20
a. let each remain in the role by which he was called in this

###### v21
a. were you called (as a) slave?
b. let (it) not be of concern to you
c. but indeed if you are able to become (a) free person
d. make use of (it) instead

###### v22
a. because the one having been called in (the) Lord (as a) slave
b. is (a) free person of (the) Lord
c. likewise the free person having been called
d. is (a) slave of Christ

###### v23
a. you were redeemed for (a) price
b. do not become slaves of human beings

###### v24
a. fellow believers each in which he was called
b. let him remain in this before God

###### v25
a. now about the unmarried persons
b. I do not have (an) order of (the) Lord
c. but I give (an) opinion
d. as being shown mercy by (the) Lord to be trustworthy

###### v26
a. I then assume this
b. to be good due to the present trouble that
c. (it is) good for (a) person to be as follows

###### v27
a. have you been bound to (a) wife
b. do not seek divorce
c. have you been released from (a) wife
d. do not seek (a) wife

###### v28
a. but if also you would marry
b. you did not sin
c. also if (a) young woman marries
d. she has not sinned
e. but such ones will have hardship in life
f. and so I spare you

###### v29
a. further I say this fellow believers
b. the time has been coming to an end
c. furthermore that also those having wives
d. let them be as not having

###### v30
a. and those weeping
b. as not weeping
c. and those rejoicing
d. as not rejoicing
e. and those buying
f. as not possessing

###### v31
a. and those dealing with the world
b. as not being fully occupied
c. for the form of this world passes away

###### v32
a. and I want you to be without worry
b. the unmarried is anxious about the things of the Lord
c. how he should please the Lord

###### v33
a. but the one having married is anxious about the things of the world
b. how he may please his wife

###### v34
a. indeed he is divided
b. further the unmarried woman and the young woman is anxious about  
c. so that she may be holy both in body and in way of thinking
d. but the one having married is anxious about the things of the world
e. how she may please her husband

###### v35
a. now this I say for the benefit of you yourselves
b. not so that I impose restrictions on you
c. but for the sake of what (is) esteemed
d. and devotion to the Lord without distraction

###### v36
a. now if someone thinks to behave indecently towards his unmarried woman
b. if she is past marriageable age
c. indeed it must be this way
d. let him do what he wants
e. he does not sin
f. let them marry

###### v37
a. and who is firmly of his opinion
b. not having (a) necessary obligation
c. but he has control of his own desire
d. and he has decided this in his own inner self
e. to keep his own unmarried woman
f. he will do well

###### v38
a. so that also the one marrying his own unmarried woman
b. he does well
c. yet the one not marrying
d. he does better

###### v39
a. (a) wife is bound for as long (a) time as her husband lives
b. but if her husband would have died
c. she is free to be married to whom she wishes
d. only in union with (the) Lord

###### v40
a. but she is more fortunate if she would remain like this
b. according to my opinion
c. and I think also I have (the) Spirit of God
