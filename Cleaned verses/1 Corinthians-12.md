###### v1
a. now concerning the things from the Spirit fellow believers
b. I do not want you to be ignorant

###### v2
a. you know that
b. when you were heathen
c. being led astray to the mute idols as you would be led

###### v3
a. therefore I make known to you that
b. no one speaking from (the) Spirit of God
c. says Jesus (is) cursed
d. and no one can say Jesus (is) Lord
e. except from (the) Holy Spirit

###### v4
a. now there are varieties of gifts
b. but the same Spirit

###### v5
a. also there are varieties of ministries
b. yet the same Lord

###### v6
a. and there are varieties of deeds
b. yet the same God
c. the One bringing about everything in all

###### v7
a. further to each is given the revelation of the Spirit
b. for our advantage

###### v8
a. because to the one indeed is given by the Spirit (a) message of wisdom
b. and to another (a) message of knowledge by the same Spirit

###### v9
a. to another faith by the same Spirit
b. and to another gifts of healing by the one Spirit

###### v10
a. and to another deeds of supernatural power
b. [and] to another (the) ability to prophesy
c. [and] to another (the) ability to distinguish between spirits
d. to another kinds of ecstatic languages
e. and to another interpretation of ecstatic languages

###### v11
a. and all these things the One and the same Spirit brings about
b. distributing to each individually just as He desires

###### v12
a. because just as the body is one
b. yet it has many body parts
c. and all the parts of the body being many
d. is one body
e. so also (is) Christ

###### v13
a. because indeed by one Spirit we all were baptised into one body
b. whether Jews or Greeks whether slaves or free persons
c. yes we all were given to drink one Spirit

###### v14
a. also in fact the body is not one body part but many

###### v15
a. if the foot would say that
b. I am not (the) hand
c. I am not part of the body
d. is it not because of this not part of the body?

###### v16
a. and if the ear should say that
b. I am not (the) eye
c. I am not part of the body
d. is it not because of this not part of the body?

###### v17
a. if the whole body (is an) eye
b. where (is) the ability to hear?
c. if (the) whole (is) hearing
d. where (is) the sense of smell?

###### v18
a. but now God put the body parts
b. each one of them in the body just as He wanted

###### v19
a. now if everything was one body part
b. where (is) the body?

###### v20
a. but now (there are) indeed many body parts
b. but one body

###### v21
a. further the eye can not say to the hand
b. I have no need of you
c. or again the head (can not say) to the feet
d. I have no need of you

###### v22
a. certainly by much more the parts of the body seeming
b. to be weaker ones
c. are indispensable

###### v23
a. and those of the body we suppose to be lacking in honour
b. to those we assign excessive honour
c. and the ugly (parts) of us experience much greater attractiveness

###### v24
a. but the attractive (parts) of us have no need (of this)
b. but God put the body together
c. giving much greater honour to the one being inferior

###### v25
a. so that there would not be discord in the body
b. but the parts would have the same concern for one another

###### v26
a. indeed if one body part suffers
b. all the body parts suffer together
c. if [one] body part is glorified
d. all the body parts rejoice together

###### v27
a. now you are (the) body of Christ and body parts as a part of (it)

###### v28
a. and whom then God appointed in the church (are)
b. first apostles
c. second prophets
d. third teachers
e. then (those doing) mighty deeds
f. then gifts of powers to heal
g. abilities to help
h. abilities to lead
i. kinds of ecstatic languages

###### v29
a. surely not all (are) apostles?
b. surely not all (are) prophets?
c. surely not all (are) teachers?
d. surely not all (are doing) mighty deeds?

###### v30
a. surely not all have gifts of powers to heal?
b. surely not all speak in ecstatic languages?
c. surely not all interpret?

###### v31
a. set your hearts then on the better gifts

###### v32
a. if I speak in the languages of human beings and even of angels
b. yet I have not love
c. I have become (a) brass gong or (a) clanging cymbal

###### v33
a. and if I have (the) ability to prophesy
b. and I would know all the mysteries
c. and all the knowledge
d. and if I have all the faith
e. so as to remove mountains
f. but I do not have love
g. I am nothing

###### v34
a. even if I shall give away all my possessions
b. and if I would give over my body so that I may boast
c. but I do not have love
d. I accomplish nothing
