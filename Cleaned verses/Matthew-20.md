###### v1
a. because the Kingdom of Heaven is like (a) man (being) master of a household
b. who went out just at early morning
c. to hire workers for his vineyard

###### v2
a. and having agreed with the workers for (a) denarius for the day
b. he sent them into his vineyard

###### v3
a. then having gone out about (the) third hour
b. he saw others standing in the marketplace not working

###### v4
a. then he said to them
b. go also you into the vineyard
c. and what ever is proper
d. I will pay you

###### v5
a. and then they went off
b. [and] again having gone out about (the) sixth and ninth hour
c. he did likewise

###### v6
a. and having gone out about the eleventh (hour)
b. he found others standing (there)
c. then he says to them
d. why do you stand here idle the whole day?

###### v7
a. they say to him
b. because no one hired us
c. he says to them
d. go also you into the vineyard

###### v8
a. now having become evening
b. the owner of the vineyard says to his foreman
c. call the workers
d. and pay them their wages
e. beginning from the last until the first

###### v9
a. now those having come about the eleventh hour
b. they received each (a) denarius

###### v10
a. now the first having come
b. they supposed that
c. they will receive more
d. yet they also received themselves each (a) denarius

###### v11
a. now having received (a denarius)
b. they complained against the master of the household

###### v12
a. saying
b. these last ones worked one hour
c. yet you dealt with them equal to us
d. the ones having endured the hardship of the day
e. yes (we endured) the scorching heat

###### v13
a. but he having replied said to one of them
b. friend I do not mistreat you
c. did you not agree with me for (a) denarius?

###### v14
a. take what (is) yours
b. and go
c. I now want to give to this last one as also to you

###### v15
a. [or] is it not allowed for me to do what I want with what (is) mine?
b. or is it (that) you (are) jealous
c. because I am generous?

###### v16
a. likewise the last will be (the) first
b. and the first (will be the) last

###### v17
a. then Jesus going up to Jerusalem
b. took aside the twelve [disciples] privately
c. and on the road He said to them

###### v18
a. look
b. we are going up to Jerusalem
c. indeed the Son of Man will be handed over to the chief priests and  
d. and then they will condemn Him to death

###### v19
a. and they will hand Him over to the Gentiles
b. so as to mock (Him)
c. and to flog (Him)
d. and to crucify (Him)
e. and then on the third day He will be raised to life

###### v20
a. then came to Him the mother of the sons of Zebedee along with her sons
b. bowing down and asking something from Him

###### v21
a. and He said to her
b. what do you want?
c. she says to Him
d. say that these two sons of mine may sit
e. one on Your right side
f. and one on your left side
g. in Your kingdom

###### v22
a. then Jesus having answered said
b. you do not know what you ask
c. can you drink the cup
d. that I am about to drink?
e. they say to Him
f. we can

###### v23
a. He says to them
b. you will indeed drink My cup
c. but to sit on My right side and on (My) left side
d. [this] is not mine to give
e. but for whom it has been prepared by My Father

###### v24
a. now the ten having heard
b. they were indignant with the two brothers

###### v25
a. Jesus then having called them to (Him) said
b. you know that
c. the rulers of the nations reign over them
d. indeed the important persons rule over them

###### v26
a. it shall not be so among you
b. but who ever desires among you to be great
c. he must be your servant

###### v27
a. and who ever would want among you to be (the) first
b. he must be your slave

###### v28
a. just as the Son of Man did not come to be served
b. but to serve
c. and to give His life (as a) ransom on behalf of many

###### v29
a. now as they were going out from Jericho
b. (a) large crowd followed Him

###### v30
a. and look
b. two blind men sitting by the road
c. having heard that
d. Jesus is passing by
e. they shouted saying
f. have mercy on us  [Lord] Son of David

###### v31
a. but the crowd rebuked them
b. so that they should be quiet
c. but the (two) shouted louder saying
d. have mercy on us  Lord Son of David

###### v32
a. then Jesus having stopped
b. called them and said
c. what do you desire I shall do for you?

###### v33
a. they say to Him
b. Lord  that our eyes may be able to see

###### v34
a. now Jesus having felt compassion
b. He touched their eyes
c. and immediately they gained sight
d. and then they followed Him
