###### v1
a. now before the festival of the Passover Jesus knowing that
b. His time came that
c. He should depart from this world to the Father
d. having loved His own in the world
e. He loved them completely

###### v2
a. and now supper taking place
b. the devil having already put into the heart that
c. Judas son of Simon Iscariot should betray Him

###### v3
a. knowing that
b. the Father gave everything to Him into His power
c. also that
d. He went out from God
e. and is on His way to God

###### v4
a. He stands up from the supper
b. and takes off His outer garment
c. and having taken (a) towel He girded Himself

###### v5
a. then He pours water into the basin
b. and He began to wash the feet of the disciples
c. and to dry with the towel by which He was girded

###### v6
a. so then He comes to Simon Peter
b. he says to Him
c. Lord do You wash my feet?

###### v7
a. Jesus answered and said to him
b. what I do
c. you do not understand right now
d. but you will understand after this

###### v8
a. Peter says to Him
b. You shall certainly not wash my feet forever
c. Jesus answered him
d. if I do not wash you
e. you have no part with Me

###### v9
a. Simon Peter says to Him
b. Lord  not my feet only
c. but also my hands and my head

###### v10
a. Jesus says to him
b. the one having been washed has no need
c. except his feet to be washed
d. he is surely completely clean
e. in fact you are clean
f. but indeed not all (of you)

###### v11
a. for He knew the one betraying Him
b. because of this He said that
c. indeed you are not all clean

###### v12
a. now when He washed their feet
b. [then] He put on His outer garment
c. and He reclined again
d. He said to them
e. do you understand what I have done to you?

###### v13
a. you call Me Teacher and Lord
b. and you speak correctly
c. for I am

###### v14
a. if then I the Lord and the Teacher washed your feet
b. also you must wash the feet of one another

###### v15
a. because I gave you (an) example that
b. just as I did to you
c. also you should do

###### v16
a. truly truly I say to you
b. (a) slave is not greater than his master
c. nor (is an) apostle greater than the one having sent him

###### v17
a. if you know these things
b. you are fortunate when you do them

###### v18
a. I do not speak about all of you
b. I know whom I chose
c. but the Scripture must be fulfilled namely
d. the one eating My bread
e. he lifted his heel against Me

###### v19
a. from now on I tell you before it happens
b. so that when it happens you may believe that
c. I am

###### v20
a. truly truly I say to you
b. the one accepting who ever I may send
c. accepts Me
d. and the one accepting Me
e. accepts the One having sent Me

###### v21
a. having said these things
b. Jesus was greatly upset in His inner being
c. and He testified and said
d. truly truly I say to you that
e. one of you will betray Me

###### v22
a. the disciples looked at one another
b. being uncertain about whom He speaks

###### v23
a. one of His disciples was dining in the place of honour next to Jesus
b. the one whom Jesus loved

###### v24
a. Simon Peter then motioned to this one to ask
b. who ever it may be about whom He speaks

###### v25
a. that one then dining thus in the place of honour next to Jesus says to Him
b. Lord who is it?

###### v26
a. Jesus answers
b. it is that one for whom I will dip the piece of bread
c. and then I will give (it) to him
d. having then dipped the piece of bread
e. [He takes (it) and] He gives (it) to Judas son of Simon Iscariot

###### v27
a. and after the piece of bread
b. then Satan entered into that one
c. Jesus now says to him
d. what you are doing
e. do (it) quickly

###### v28
a. [now] as to this no one of those dining knew for what He spoke to him

###### v29
a. because some thought
b. since Judas had the money bag
c. that Jesus says to him
d. buy of what we have need for the festival
e. or that he should give something to the poor

###### v30
a. having taken then the piece of bread
b. he went out quickly
c. and it was night

###### v31
a. then when he went out
b. Jesus says
c. now the Son of Man is glorified
d. and God is glorified in Him

###### v32
a. [if God is glorified in Him]
b. also God will glorify Him in Himself
c. and He will glorify Him right away

###### v33
a. children I am with you still (a) short time
b. you will seek Me
c. yet just as I said to the Jews that
d. where I am going to
e. you can not come
f. also to you I say right now

###### v34
a. (a) new command I give you namely
b. love one another
c. just as I loved you
d. actually also you must love one another

###### v35
a. by this everyone will know that
b. you are My disciples
c. if you have love for one another

###### v36
a. Simon Peter says to Him
b. Lord  where are You going?
c. Jesus answered [him]
d. where I am going
e. you can not follow Me now
f. but you will follow later

###### v37
a. Peter says to Him
b. Lord why can I not follow You right now?
c. I will lay down my life for You

###### v38
a. Jesus answers
b. you will lay down your life for Me?
c. truly truly I say to you
d. (a) rooster will certainly not crow
e. until you will deny Me three times
