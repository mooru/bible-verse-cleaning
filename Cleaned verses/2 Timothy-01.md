###### v1
a. Paul (an) apostle of Christ Jesus
b. by (the) will of God
c. according to (the) promise of life in Christ Jesus

###### v2
a. to Timothy (a) beloved son
b. grace mercy peace from God (our) Father
c. and Christ Jesus our Lord

###### v3
a. I have thanks to God
b. Whom I worship since (my) ancestors with (a) pure conscience
c. because I unceasingly make mention about you
d. in my prayers night and day

###### v4
a. deeply desiring to see you
b. remembering your tears
c. so that I may be filled with joy

###### v5
a. having remembrance of the genuine faith in you
b. that resided first in your grandmother Lois
c. and also (in) your mother Eunice
d. and I am convinced that (it resides) also in you

###### v6
a. for this reason I remind you
b. to reactivate the gracious gift of God
c. that is in you through the laying on of my hands

###### v7
a. for God did not give us (an) attitude of timidity
b. but of power and of love and of sound judgement

###### v8
a. be then not ashamed of the witness for our Lord
b. and not (of) me His prisoner
c. but join (with me) in suffering for the Gospel
d. in accordance with (the) power of God

###### v9
a. the One having saved us
b. and having called (us) with (a) dedicated calling
c. not according to our deeds
d. but according to His own plan and grace
e. the (grace) given to us in union with Christ Jesus since all time

###### v10
a. but now having been revealed
b. by the appearing of our Saviour Christ Jesus
c. indeed having made death ineffective
d. and having revealed life and immortality by the Gospel

###### v11
a. to which I was appointed preacher and apostle and teacher

###### v12
a. for which reason also I suffer these things
b. but I am not ashamed
c. for I know in Whom I have believed
d. and I am certain that
e. He is powerful
f. to guard closely what is entrusted to me for that day

###### v13
a. follow (the) example of (the) sound message
b. that you heard from me
c. with faith and love in union with Christ Jesus

###### v14
a. guard the good what is entrusted
b. by (the) Holy Spirit dwelling in us

###### v15
a. you know this namely
b. all those in Asia turned away from me
c. of whom are Phygelus and Hermogenes

###### v16
a. may the Lord grant mercy to the household of Onesiphorus
b. because he often encouraged me
c. yes he was not ashamed of my imprisonment

###### v17
a. but having come to be in Rome
b. he looked for me eagerly and he found (me)

###### v18
a. may the Lord grant to him
b. to find mercy from (the) Lord on that day
c. indeed as much as he took care of (me) in Ephesus
d. you know well
