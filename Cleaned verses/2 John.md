###### v1
a. the elder
b. to (the) chosen lady and her children
c. whom I really love
d. and not I alone
e. but also all those having knowledge of the truth

###### v2
a. because of the truth remaining in us
b. and will be with us forever

###### v3
a. grace mercy peace will be with us
b. from God (the) Father
c. and from Jesus Christ the Son of the Father
d. in truth and love

###### v4
a. I rejoiced greatly that
b. I have found among your children (those) living in truth
c. because we received command from the Father

###### v5
a. and now I ask you lady
b. not as writing to you (a) new command
c. but one that we had from (the) beginning that
d. we should love one another

###### v6
a. and this is love that
b. we should live according to His commands
c. this is His command
d. as you heard from (the) beginning that
e. we should live in it

###### v7
a. that  many deceivers went out into the world
b. those not confessing Jesus Christ coming in (a) physical body
c. this (one) is the deceiver and also the antichrist

###### v8
a. watch yourselves that
b. you should not loose what we brought about
c. but (that) you should receive (a) full reward

###### v9
a. everyone failing to obey
b. and not remaining in the teaching of Christ
c. does not have God
d. the one remaining in the teaching
e. this one has both the Father and the Son

###### v10
a. if someone comes to you
b. and does not bring this teaching
c. do not receive him into (your) house
d. and do not speak greetings to him

###### v11
a. because the one speaking greetings to him
b. shares in doing his wicked deeds

###### v12
a. having many things to write to you
b. I did not want (to do it) with paper and ink
c. but I hope to come to be with you
d. and to talk face to face
e. so that our joy may be complete

###### v13
a. the children of your chosen sister greet you
