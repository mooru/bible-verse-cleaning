###### v1
a. after this Jesus again revealed Himself to His disciples at the Sea of Tiberias
b. and He appeared as follows

###### v2
a. they were together
b. Simon Peter
c. and Thomas the one called Didymus
d. and Nathanael the one from Cana of Galilee
e. and the sons of Zebedee
f. and two others of His disciples

###### v3
a. Simon Peter says to them
b. I am going to fish
c. they say to him
d. we too are coming with you
e. they went out and got into the boat
f. and during that night they caught nothing

###### v4
a. but early morning already having come
b. Jesus stood on the shore
c. yet the disciples did not know that it is Jesus

###### v5
a. Jesus then says to them
b. children do you not have any fish?
c. they answered Him no

###### v6
a. and then He said to them
b. throw the net on the right side of the boat
c. and then you will find
d. they then threw
e. and no longer were they capable to pull it because of the large number of fish

###### v7
a. then that disciple whom Jesus loved says to Peter
b. it is the Lord
c. Simon Peter then having heard that
d. it is the Lord
e. he girded his outer garment
f. because he was naked
g. and he threw himself into the sea

###### v8
a. but the other disciples came with the boat
b. because they were not far from the land
c. indeed about two hundred cubits away
d. dragging the net (full) of the fish

###### v9
a. then when they disembarked upon the land
b. they see (a) charcoal fire being there
c. and fish lying on (it)
d. and also bread

###### v10
a. Jesus says to them
b. bring some of the fish that you caught just now

###### v11
a. Simon Peter then went aboard
b. and he pulled the net onto the land
c. full of big fish one hundred (and) fifty three
d. yet being so many the net was not torn

###### v12
a. Jesus says to them
b. come here have breakfast
c. but no one of the disciples dared to ask Him
d. who are You?
e. knowing that
f. He is the Lord

###### v13
a. Jesus comes
b. and He takes the bread
c. and He gives to them
d. then the fish likewise

###### v14
a. this (was) already (the) third (time) Jesus appeared to the disciples
b. having been raised from (the) dead

###### v15
a. then when they had breakfast
b. Jesus says to Simon Peter
c. Simon son of John do you love Me more than these ones?
d. he says to Him
e. yes Lord You know that
f. I love You
g. He says to him
h. take care of My lambs

###### v16
a. He says to him again (a) second (time)
b. Simon son of John do you love Me?
c. he says to Him
d. yes Lord You know that
e. I love You
f. He says to him
g. take care of My sheep

###### v17
a. He says to him the third (time)
b. Simon son of John do you love Me?
c. Peter became sad
d. because the third (time) He said to him do you love Me?
e. and he says to Him
f. Lord You know everything
g. You know that
h. I love You
i. [Jesus] says to him
j. take care of My sheep

###### v18
a. truly truly I say to you
b. when you were younger
c. you girded yourself
d. and you walked where you wanted
e. but when you grow old
f. you will stretch out your hand
g. and another will gird you
h. and will bring (you) where you do not want

###### v19
a. and this He said indicating by what kind of death he will glorify God
b. then having said this
c. He says to him
d. follow Me

###### v20
a. Peter having turned around
b. he sees the disciple whom Jesus loved following
c. and who dined at the meal in the place of honour (next to) Him
d. and said
e. Lord who is the one betraying You?

###### v21
a. Peter then having seen him
b. he says to Jesus
c. Lord and what (about) him?

###### v22
a. Jesus says to him
b. if I want him to remain until I come
c. what with you?
d. you follow Me

###### v23
a. this message then went out among the fellow believers that
b. that disciple (will) not die
c. but Jesus did not say to him that
d. he (will) not die
e. but (He said)
f. if I want him to remain until I come
g. [what with you]?

###### v24
a. this is the disciple witnessing about these things
b. and the one having written these things
c. and we know that
d. his witness is true

###### v25
a. now there are also many other things that Jesus did
b. which if written one by one
c. I imagine not even the world itself (can) contain the written books
