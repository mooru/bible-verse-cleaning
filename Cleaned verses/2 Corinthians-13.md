###### v1
a. this third (time) I am coming to you
b. by (the) speaking of two and even three witnesses
c. every statement will be put into force

###### v2
a. I have said already and I say before
b. like being present the second (time)
c. indeed being absent now
d. to those and all the rest having sinned in the past that
e. when I come again
f. I will not spare

###### v3
a. because you seek evidence of Christ speaking through me
b. He who is not weak concerning you
c. but He is able concerning you

###### v4
a. for indeed He was crucified out of weakness
b. but He lives by means of (the) power of God
c. indeed also we are weak in union with Him
d. but we will live with Him
e. by means of (the) power of God on behalf of you

###### v5
a. examine yourselves
b. if you really are in the faith
c. examine yourselves
d. or do you not recognise yourselves that
e. Jesus Christ (is) in you?
f. unless if indeed you are worthless

###### v6
a. further I hope that
b. you know that
c. we are not worthless

###### v7
a. also then we are praying to God
b. that you do not commit evil at all
c. not that we may seem to be regarded as worthy
d. but that you may do the good thing
e. though we may be like bad ones

###### v8
a. for we can not (do) anything against the truth
b. but for the sake of the truth

###### v9
a. in fact we rejoice when we may be weak
b. but you may be powerful
c. this indeed we pray
d. namely your adequacy

###### v10
a. because of this I write these things being absent
b. so that being present
c. I will not behave harshly
d. according to my authority
e. that the Lord gave to me for building up
f. and not for tearing down

###### v11
a. what remains fellow believers be greeted
b. be made adequate be encouraged
c. have the same attitude live in peace
d. surely the God of love and peace will be with you

###### v12
a. greet each other with (a) holy kiss
b. all God's people greet you

###### v13
a. the grace of the Lord Jesus Christ
b. and the love of God
c. and the fellowship of the Holy Spirit
d. (be) with you all
