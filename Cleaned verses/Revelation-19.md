b. of (a) large crowd in heaven saying
c. hallelujah
d. the salvation and the glory and the power (are) of our God

###### v2
a. because His judgments (are) true and just
b. because He judged the great prostitute
c. who perverted the earth by her fornication
d. yes He revenged the blood of His servants by her power

###### v3
a. then (a) second (time) they have said
b. hallelujah
c. surely her smoke is going up forever and ever

###### v4
a. then fell down the twenty four elders and the four living beings
b. and they worshipped God the One sitting upon the throne
c. saying
d. amen hallelujah

###### v5
a. then (a) voice from the throne came out saying
b. praise to our God all His servants
c. [yes] those worshipping Him the small ones and the big ones

###### v6
a. further I heard like (a) voice of (a) large crowd
b. indeed like (the) voice of many waters
c. and like (the) voice of strong thunders
d. saying
e. hallelujah
f. because (the) Lord reigned
g. [our] God the Almighty

###### v7
a. let us rejoice and be extremely joyful
b. yes let us give the glory to Him
c. because the wedding of the Lamb came
d. yes His wife made herself ready

###### v8
a. further it was given to her that
b. she should be clothed in fine linen shining clean
c. because the fine linen is the righteous deeds of God's people

###### v9
a. then he says to me
b. write
c. fortunate (are) those having been invited to the feast of the wedding of the Lamb
d. then he says to me
e. these are the true words of God

###### v10
a. then I fell down before his feet to worship him
b. further he says to me
c. pay attention (do) not
d. I am (a) fellow servant of you
e. as well as of your fellow believers
f. the ones having the testimony of Jesus
g. worship God
h. because the testimony of Jesus is the Spirit of the prophecy

###### v11
a. then I saw heaven having been opened
b. now look
c. (a) white horse
d. and the One sitting upon it
e. [being called]  Faithful and True
f. and in righteousness He judges and makes war

###### v12
a. and His eyes [like] (a) flame of fire
b. and on His head many diadem crowns
c. He having (a) Name having been written
d. that no one knows except Himself

###### v13
a. and having been clothed in (a) robe
b. having been dipped in blood
c. also His Name was called
d. the Word of God

###### v14
a. then the armies [the ones] in heaven
b. followed Him on white horses
c. having been clothed in pure white fine linen

###### v15
a. and out of His mouth (a) sharp sword comes out
b. so that with it He would strike down the nations
c. indeed He himself will rule them with (a) rod made of iron
d. indeed He himself tramples the wine press
e. of the wine of the wrath of the anger of God the Almighty

###### v16
a. also He has on the robe and on the thigh of Him (a) Name written
b. King of kings and Lord of lords

###### v17
a. then I saw one angel standing on the sun
b. and he shouted [with] (a) loud voice
c. saying to all the birds the ones flying in midheaven
d. come here
e. be gathered together for the big banquet of God

###### v18
a. so that you may eat (the) flesh of kings
b. and (the) flesh of generals and (the) flesh of (the) powerful
c. and (the) flesh of horses as well as of those sitting upon them
d. and (the) flesh of all free persons as well as slaves
e. both small ones and big ones

###### v19
a. then I saw the beast and the kings of the earth
b. as well as their armies gathered together
c. to make war against the One sitting upon the horse
d. and against His armies

###### v20
a. then the beast was seized
b. as well as the false prophet with him
c. the one having performed the signs before him
d. by which he deceived those having received the image of the beast
e. as well as those worshipping his image
f. being alive the two were thrown into the lake of fire
g. the one burning with sulphur

###### v21
a. then the rest were killed by the sword
b. of the One sitting upon the horse
c. by the (sword) coming out of His mouth
d. then all the birds were fed by means of their flesh
