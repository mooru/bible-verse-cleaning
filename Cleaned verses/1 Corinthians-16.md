###### v1
a. now about the collection for God's people
b. just as I commanded the churches of Galatia
c. do you also likewise

###### v2
a. on (the) first of (the) week let each of you in the judgment of himself throw in
b. saving up what thing ever you gained in business
c. so that not when I would come
d. then collections would take place

###### v3
a. then when I would arrive
b. who ever you regard as worthwhile
c. I will send these ones with letters
d. to take your gift to Jerusalem

###### v4
a. and if it may be proper that also I go
b. they will travel with me

###### v5
a. further I will come to you
b. when I have gone through Macedonia
c. because I am travelling through Macedonia

###### v6
a. and perhaps I will stay with you
b. or also spend the winter (there)
c. so that you may send me on my way
d. where ever I may travel

###### v7
a. for I do not want to visit you right now in passing by
b. because I hope to stay with you some time
c. if the Lord would allow

###### v8
a. now I will stay in Ephesus until Pentecost

###### v9
a. because for me (a) big and effective door has opened
b. yet many being hostile

###### v10
a. but when Timothy would come
b. watch out that he may be without fear with you
c. because he is doing the work of (the) Lord as also I

###### v11
a. let no one then despise him
b. but help him on his way in peace
c. so that he may come to me
d. because I expect him with the fellow believers

###### v12
a. now with regard to Apollos our fellow believer
b. many times I asked him earnestly
c. that he should come to you with the fellow believers
d. and certainly there was no plan that he would come now
e. but he will come when he has a chance to

###### v13
a. be alert
b. stand firm in the faith
c. be brave become strong

###### v14
a. let all things of you happen in love

###### v15
a. now I appeal to you fellow believers
b. you know the household of Stephanas that
c. it is (the) first of Achaia
d. and they gave themselves for ministry to God's people

###### v16
a. so that also you should submit to such ones
b. and also to everyone working together and labouring

###### v17
a. and I rejoice about the arrival of Stephanas and Fortunatus and Achaicus
b. because these ones have made up for your absence

###### v18
a. because they have refreshed my inner being and also that of you
b. acknowledge then such ones

###### v19
a. the churches of Asia greet you
b. Aquila and Prisca greet you much in (the) Lord
c. with the church at their home

###### v20
a. all the fellow believers greet you
b. greet one another with (a) holy kiss

###### v21
a. the greeting (is) with my own hand Paul

###### v22
a. if anyone does not love the Lord
b. let him be cursed
c. come our Lord

###### v23
a. the grace of the Lord Jesus (is) with you

###### v24
a. my love (is) with you all in union with Christ Jesus
