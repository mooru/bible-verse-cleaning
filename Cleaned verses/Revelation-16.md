###### v1
a. then I heard (a) loud voice from the temple
b. saying to the seven angels
c. go along
d. and pour out the seven bowls of the wrath of God onto the land

###### v2
a. then the first departed
b. and he poured out his bowl onto the earth
c. and (a) harmful and sickly sore came to be upon the people
d. the ones having the mark of the beast
e. and the ones worshipping his image

###### v3
a. then the second poured out his bowl onto the sea
b. and it became blood like of (a) dead one
c. and every living creature died the ones in the sea

###### v4
a. then the third poured out his bowl onto the rivers
b. and the springs of the waters
c. and it became blood

###### v5
a. then I heard the angel of the waters saying
b. righteous are You the One being
c. and the One (who) was
d. the holy One
e. because You judged these things

###### v6
a. because they murdered holy ones and prophets
b. and You have given them blood to drink
c. they are deserving

###### v7
a. then I heard the altar saying
b. yes Lord God the Almighty
c. true and proper (are) Your judgments

###### v8
a. then the fourth poured out his bowl on the sun
b. and it was given to him to harm by heat the people with fire

###### v9
a. yes the people were scorched by (a) great scorching heat
b. yet the Name of God was blasphemed
c. the One having the power over these plagues
d. yet they did not repent to give Him glory

###### v10
a. then the fifth poured out his bowl on the throne of the beast
b. and his kingdom happened to having become dark
c. and they were biting their tongues because of the pain

###### v11
a. yet they blasphemed the God of heaven
b. because of their pains and because of their sores
c. yet they did not repent from their deeds

###### v12
a. then the sixth poured out his bowl
b. on the great river the Euphrates
c. and its water was dried up
d. so that the road of the kings would be made ready
e. of the ones from (the) rising of (the) sun

###### v13
a. and then I saw from the mouth of the dragon
b. as well as from the mouth of the beast
c. and also from the mouth of the false prophet
d. three unclean spirits like frogs

###### v14
a. for they are evil spirits of demons performing signs
b. who are going forth to the kings of the whole world
c. to gather them together for the battle
d. of the great day of God the Almighty

###### v15
a. pay attention
b. I am coming like (a) thief
c. fortunate (is) the one being awake
d. and guarding his clothes
e. so that he may not walk around naked
f. and they may see his shameful state

###### v16
a. then they gathered them together into the place
b. the one called in Hebrew Armageddon

###### v17
a. then the seventh poured out his bowl upon the air
b. and (a) loud voice came out of the temple
c. from the throne saying
d. it has happened

###### v18
a. then occurred lightnings and sounds and thunders
b. also (a) great earthquake happened
c. like did not happen since which (time a) person was on the earth
d. so great (an) earthquake so big

###### v19
a. then the big city became into three parts
b. and the cities of the nations fell
c. yes Babylon the great was remembered in the sight of God
d. to give to her the cup of the wine of the fury of His anger

###### v20
a. then every island disappeared quickly
b. and mountains were not found

###### v21
a. and big hail approximately weighing a talent
b. came down from the sky upon the people
c. then the people blasphemed God
d. because of the plague of the hail
e. because the plague of it is exceedingly great
