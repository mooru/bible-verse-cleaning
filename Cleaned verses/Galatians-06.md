###### v1
a. fellow believers
b. if then (a) person is caught in any transgression
c. you the spiritual people must make such one fully adequate
d. with (a) spirit of gentleness
e. watching out for yourself
f. so that you also may not be tempted

###### v2
a. carry the burdens of one another
b. and in this way you will obey the law of Christ

###### v3
a. for if anyone thinks to be someone important
b. while being nothing
c. he deceives himself

###### v4
a. let everyone then examine his own conduct
b. and then he will only have reason to be proud about himself
c. and not with reference to someone else

###### v5
a. because everyone will carry his own burden

###### v6
a. and let him share
b. the one being taught the message
c. with the one teaching (him) in all good things

###### v7
a. do not be deceived
b. God is not ridiculed
c. because what ever (a) person sows
d. this also he will reap

###### v8
a. because the one sowing by means of his own sinful nature
b. from his sinful nature he will reap destruction
c. but the one sowing by means of the Spirit
d. from the Spirit he will reap eternal life

###### v9
a. so let us not give up doing good
b. because in our own time we will reap
c. not giving up

###### v10
a. so then while we have (the) opportunity
b. let us do good with regard to all people
c. and especially to the family members of the faith

###### v11
a. look with what large letters I wrote to you with my own hand

###### v12
a. as many as desire to make a good showing in (the) flesh
b. these ones compel you to be circumcised
c. only so that they may not be persecuted for the cross of Christ

###### v13
a. because not even those circumcised
b. they themselves keep the Law
c. but they want you to be circumcised
d. so that they may boast about your flesh

###### v14
a. but for me may it not happen to boast
b. except about the cross of our Lord Jesus Christ
c. because of which (the) world is crucified to me
d. and I to (the) world

###### v15
a. because neither circumcision is something important
b. nor uncircumcision
c. but (a) new creation

###### v16
a. and as many as live in accordance with this principle
b. peace to them and mercy
c. indeed to the Israel of God

###### v17
a. finally let no one cause me troubles
b. because I bear the marks of Jesus on my body

###### v18
a. the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ
b. (be) with your inner being fellow believers
c. amen
