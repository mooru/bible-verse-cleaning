###### v1
a. then He having gone out of the temple
b. one of His disciples says to Him
c. Teacher look what kind of stones and what kind of buildings

###### v2
a. then Jesus said to him
b. do you see these large buildings?
c. there will certainly not be left in place here (a) stone upon (a) stone
d. that will certainly not be torn down

###### v3
a. then He having sat down on the Mount of Olives across the temple
b. Peter and James and John and Andrew asked Him privately

###### v4
a. tell us
b. when will this be and what (will be) the sign
c. when all these things will be about to come to an end

###### v5
a. then Jesus began to say to them
b. watch out (that) no one would deceive you

###### v6
a. many will come in My Name
b. saying that
c. I am
d. and they will deceive many

###### v7
a. but when you hear of wars and news of wars
b. do not be startled
c. it must happen
d. but (it is) not yet the end

###### v8
a. for nation will be raised against nation and kingdom against kingdom
b. there will be earthquakes from place to place
c. there will be famines
d. these things (are the) beginning of great sufferings

###### v9
a. but you pay attention to yourselves
b. they will hand you over to councils
c. and they will whip (you) in synagogues
d. and you will stand before governors and kings for the sake of Me
e. as (a) testimony to them

###### v10
a. also the gospel must first be preached to all the nations

###### v11
a. then when they lead you handing (you) over
b. do not worry beforehand what you should say
c. but what ever will be given to you at that time
d. say that
e. for not you are the ones speaking but the Holy Spirit

###### v12
a. then brother will hand over brother to death
b. and (a) father (his) child
c. and children will rebel against parents
d. and they will kill them

###### v13
a. and you will be hated by all because of My Name
b. but the one having endured to (the) end
c. this one will be saved

###### v14
a. but when you see the horrible thing which defiles
b. being where it should not (be)
c. let the one reading consider carefully
d. then let those in Judea flee into the mountains

###### v15
a. [and] let not the one on the housetop go down
b. and let him not go into to take something out of his house

###### v16
a. and the one in the field let him not go back to the things behind
b. to fetch his cloak

###### v17
a. but disaster to those being pregnant
b. and to those nursing a baby in those days

###### v18
a. and pray that it does not happen in winter

###### v19
a. because those days will be suffering
b. such as like that has not happened since (the) beginning of creation
c. which God created up to the present
d. and would never ever happen

###### v20
a. indeed if (the) Lord did not reduce in number the days
b. not any human being would be saved
c. but for the sake of the chosen ones which He chose
d. He reduced in number the days

###### v21
a. then at that time if someone would say to you
b. look here (is) the Messiah
c. look there
d. do not believe

###### v22
a. for false messiahs and false prophets will be raised up
b. and they will produce signs and wonders
c. in order to deceive if possible the chosen ones

###### v23
a. but you beware
b. I have told you everything beforehand

###### v24
a. certainly in those days after that suffering
b. the sun will become dark
c. and the moon will not give its light

###### v25
a. and the stars will be falling out of the sky
b. and the powers those in the heavens will be shaken

###### v26
a. and then they will see the Son of Man coming on clouds
b. with much power and splendour

###### v27
a. and then He will send out the angels
b. and He will gather together [His] the chosen ones from the four  

###### v28
a. now from the fig tree learn the parable
b. when already its branch becomes tender
c. and it sprouts leaves
d. you know that the summer is near

###### v29
a. likewise also you when you see these things happening
b. you know that
c. it is near very soon

###### v30
a. truly I say to you that
b. this generation will not at all pass away
c. until all these things happened

###### v31
a. heaven and earth will pass away
b. but My words will never ever pass away

###### v32
a. and about that day or the hour no one knows
b. not the angels in heaven and not the Son
c. except the Father

###### v33
a. beware be alert
b. for you do not know when it is the time

###### v34
a. like (a) man away on a journey having left his house
b. and having given to his slaves authority
c. (giving) to each his work
d. and he commanded the doorkeeper that he should be alert

###### v35
a. you then be alert
b. for you do not know when the master of the house comes
c. or evening or midnight or before dawn or early morning

###### v36
a. not having come suddenly
b. He may find you sleeping

###### v37
a. but what I say to you I say to all
b. be alert
