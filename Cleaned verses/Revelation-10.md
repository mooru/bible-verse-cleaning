###### v1
a. then I saw another mighty angel
b. coming down out of heaven
c. having been clothed in (a) cloud
d. and (having) the rainbow upon his head
e. and his face (is) like the sun
f. and his feet (are) like pillars of fire

###### v2
a. and holding in his hand
b. (a) little scroll having been opened
c. then he put his right foot on the sea
d. and his left (foot) on the land

###### v3
a. and he shouted with (a) loud voice
b. like (a) lion roaring
c. then when he shouted
d. the seven thunders spoke their voices

###### v4
a. and then when the seven thunders spoke
b. I was about to write
c. and I heard (a) voice from heaven saying
d. seal what the seven thunders spoke
e. and do not write them down

###### v5
a. then the angel whom I saw standing on the sea and on the land
b. lifted up his right hand toward heaven

###### v6
a. and then he made an oath by the One living forever and ever
b. Who created heaven and the things in it
c. and also the earth and the things in it
d. as well as the sea and the things in it
e. because time shall be no longer

###### v7
a. but in the days of the sound of the seventh angel
b. when he was about to blow the trumpet
c. then was fulfilled the deep secret of God
d. when He announced the good news (to) His servants the prophets

###### v8
a. then the voice that I heard from heaven
b. speaking to me again and saying
c. go along take the book
d. the one having been opened by the hand of the angel
e. the one standing on the sea and on the land

###### v9
a. then I went to the angel
b. saying to him to give to me the little scroll
c. and he says to me
d. take and eat it up
e. surely it will make your belly bitter
f. but in your mouth it will be sweet like honey

###### v10
a. then I took the little scroll from the hand of the angel
b. and I ate it up
c. surely in my mouth it was sweet as honey
d. yet when I ate it
e. my belly was made bitter

###### v11
a. then they say to me
b. you must again prophesy to peoples and nations
c. and to languages and to many kings
