###### v1
a. then it happened when Jesus finished these words
b. He departed from Galilee
c. and He went to the regions of Judea accross the Jordan River

###### v2
a. then (a) large crowd followed Him
b. and He healed them there

###### v3
a. now Pharisees approached Him
b. trying to trap Him and saying
c. whether it is allowed for (a) man
d. to divorce his wife for any reason?

###### v4
a. but He having answered said
b. have you not read that
c. the Creator made them from (the) beginning male and female?

###### v5
a. then He said
b. on account of this (a) man shall leave his father and his mother
c. and he will be joined to his wife
d. and the two shall be one person as a result

###### v6
a. so that they are no longer two but one person
b. what then God joined in marriage
c. let no person separate

###### v7
a. they say to Him
b. why then did Moses command to give (a) certificate as notice of divorce
c. and then dismiss [her]?

###### v8
a. He says to them that
b. Moses in line with your stubbornness allowed you to divorce your wives
c. but from (the) beginning it has been not so

###### v9
a. but I say to you that
b. who ever divorces his wife lest because of fornication
c. and marries another
d. he commits adultery

###### v10
a. the disciples [of Him] say to Him
b. if thus is the relation of the man with the woman
c. it is not advantageous to marry

###### v11
a. then He said to them
b. not everyone is able to understand [this] statement
c. but for whom it has been given

###### v12
a. because there are impotent males
b. who from (the) mother's womb was born like this
c. also there are eunuchs
d. who were made eunuchs by the people
e. and then there are celibates
f. who let themselves be celibates for the sake of the Kingdom of Heaven
g. the one able to accept (it) let him accept (it)

###### v13
a. then children were brought to Him
b. so that He may lay His hands on them
c. and pray (for them)
d. but the disciples denounced them

###### v14
a. then Jesus said
b. let the children remain
c. and do not prevent them to come to Me
d. because the Kingdom of Heaven belongs to ones like these

###### v15
a. then having placed His hands on them
b. He departed from there

###### v16
a. now look
b. someone having come to Him said
c. teacher
d. what good thing must I do
e. so that I may have eternal life?

###### v17
a. then He said to him
b. why do you ask Me about what (is) good?
c. One is the good One
d. but if you desire to attain to the life
e. obey the commandments

###### v18
a. he says to Him
b. which ones?
c. then Jesus said
d. the one do not murder do not commit adultery
e. do not steal do not give false witness

###### v19
a. honour your father and your mother
b. and love your fellow being like yourself

###### v20
a. the young man says to Him
b. all these I obeyed
c. what do I lack in addition?

###### v21
a. Jesus said to him
b. if you want to be perfect
c. go away sell your possessions
d. and give to [the] poor
e. and you will have treasure in heaven
f. then come here follow Me

###### v22
a. now the young man having heard the statement
b. he went away being sad
c. for he was one having many possessions

###### v23
a. Jesus then said to His disciples
b. truly I tell you that
c. (a) rich one will enter with difficulty into the Kingdom of Heaven

###### v24
a. and again I say to you
b. it is easier that (a) camel go through (the) eye of (a) needle
c. than that (a) rich man enter into the Kingdom of God

###### v25
a. then the disciples having heard
b. they were very greatly astounded saying
c. who then can be saved?

###### v26
a. then Jesus having looked straight at them said
b. for people this is impossible
c. but for God everything (is) possible

###### v27
a. then Peter having replied said to Him
b. look
c. we have left everything
d. and we followed You
e. what then will it be for us?

###### v28
a. and Jesus said to them
b. truly I say to you that
c. you the ones having followed Me
d. in the new age when the Son of Man sits on (the) throne of His glory
e. also you will sit on twelve thrones
f. judging the twelve tribes of Israel

###### v29
a. and all who left behind  houses or brothers or sisters or father or  
b. for the sake of My Name
c. will receive (a) hundred times as much
d. and also will receive eternal life

###### v30
a. but many first ones will be last
b. and last ones (will be) first
