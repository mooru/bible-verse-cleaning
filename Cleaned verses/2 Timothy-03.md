###### v1
a. I warn (you) before God and Christ Jesus
b. the One being about to judge (the) living and (the) dead
c. both at His appearance
d. and at His Kingdom

###### v2
a. preach the message
b. persist in (it) at a favourable (and) unfavourable time
c. rebuke reprimand appeal
d. with all patience and  teaching

###### v3
a. because (a) time will come
b. when the sound teaching will not be accepted
c. but according to their own evil desires
d. they will greatly increase teachers for themselves
e. being desirous of hearing what they want to hear

###### v4
a. and then they will turn their hearing away from the truth
b. and they will stray after myths

###### v5
a. but you be sober-minded in all things
b. suffer hardship
c. do (the) work of (an) evangelist
d. finish fully your ministry

###### v6
a. indeed due to these things they are
b. those slipping into the homes
c. and getting control over foolish women
d. burdened by the guilt of sins
e. being guided by all sorts of lusts

###### v7
a. always being taught
b. yet never being able to come into knowledge of truth

###### v8
a. in which way also Jannes and Jambres resisted Moses
b. likewise also they resist the truth
c. people being depraved in mind
d. worthless with regard to the faith

###### v9
a. but they will not accomplish up to a great deal
b. for their lack of understanding will be clear to all
c. like also the (lack) of them was

###### v10
a. further you followed my teaching
b. my  behaviour my purpose my faith
c. my patience my love my endurance

###### v11
a. my persecutions my sufferings
b. which happened to me in Antioch in Iconium in Lystra
c. which persecutions I endured
d. yes from them all the Lord rescued me

###### v12
a. indeed all then desiring
b. to live piously in union with Christ Jesus
c. will be persecuted

###### v13
a. but evil people and hypocrites will keep on going to the worse
b. deceiving and being deceived

###### v14
a. but you persist in what you learned
b. and you came to believe
c. knowing from whom you have learned

###### v15
a. and that from childhood you know [the] holy Scriptures
b. the ones able to make you wise for salvation
c. through faith in Christ Jesus

###### v16
a. all Scripture (is) inspired by God
b. and (is) beneficial for teaching
c. for rebuking for correcting faults
d. for instruction in righteousness

###### v17
a. so that the person of God may be qualified
b. equipped for every good work
