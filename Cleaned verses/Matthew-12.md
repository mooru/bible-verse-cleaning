###### v1
a. at that time Jesus travelled on the Sabbath through the grain fields
b. now His disciples were hungry
c. and they began to pick heads of wheat and eat

###### v2
a. now the Pharisees having seen (it) said to Him
b. look
c. Your disciples are doing
d. what is not allowed to do on (the) Sabbath

###### v3
a. but He said to them
b. have you not read what David did
c. when he was hungry and also those with him

###### v4
a. how he entered into the House of God
b. and (how) they ate the consecrated bread
c. that was not allowed for him to eat
d. and not for those with him
e. except for the priests only?

###### v5
a. or have you not read in the Law that
b. on the Sabbaths the priests in the temple ritually defiles the Sabbath
c. yet they are guiltless?

###### v6
a. but I say to you that
b. He greater than the temple is here

###### v7
a. and if you had known what it stands for
b. I want mercy and not sacrifice
c. you would not have condemned the innocent

###### v8
a. the Son of Man is indeed Lord of the Sabbath

###### v9
a. now having gone from there
b. He went into their synagogue

###### v10
a. and look
b. (there was a) man having (a) paralyzed hand
c. and they asked Him saying
d. whether it is allowed to heal on the Sabbath?
e. so that they could accuse Him

###### v11
a. but He said to them
b. what man will be from you
c. who will have one sheep
d. and if it may fall on the Sabbath into (a) pit
e. will he not indeed grab it and restore? (it)

###### v12
a. how much then (a) person differs from (a) sheep
b. so that it is allowed on the Sabbath to do good

###### v13
a. then He says to the man
b. stretch out your hand
c. and then he stretched out
d. and it was restored healthy like the other

###### v14
a. now the Pharisees having left
b. took council against Him
c. how they could destroy Him

###### v15
a. but Jesus having known (it)
b. went away from there
c. then (a) great [crowd] followed Him
d. and He healed them all

###### v16
a. yet He strictly commanded them that
b. they should not make Him known

###### v17
a. so that the saying was fulfilled
b. by Isaiah the prophet saying

###### v18
a. look
b. My Servant Whom I chose
c. My beloved
d. in Whom My inner being delighted
e. I will let My Spirit be upon Him
f. and He will announce justice to the nations

###### v19
a. He will not quarrel neither will He shout
b. and not will anyone in the wide streets hear His voice

###### v20
a. (a) crushed reed He will not break
b. and (a) smoldering wick He will not put out
c. until He would lead out justice to victory

###### v21
a. indeed in His Name nations will hope

###### v22
a. then was brought to Him (a) demon possessed blind and dumb man
b. and He healed him
c. so that the dumb man could speak and see

###### v23
a. and the whole crowd was amazed and said
b. is He not really the Son of David?

###### v24
a. but the Pharisees having heard said
b. He does not drive out the demons
c. except by means of Beelzebul (the) ruler of the demons

###### v25
a. but knowing their thoughts He said to them
b. every kingdom divided against itself will be destroyed
c. and every city or household divided against itself will not remain

###### v26
a. indeed if Satan drives out Satan
b. he was divided against himself
c. how then will his kingdom continue?

###### v27
a. and if I by Beelzebul drive out the demons
b. by whom do your followers drive (them) out?
c. because of this they themselves will be your judges

###### v28
a. but because by (the) Spirit of God I drive out the demons
b. then the Kingdom of God already came upon you

###### v29
a. or how can someone enter into the house of the strong man
b. and plunder his things
c. if not he first ties up the strong man?
d. and then he will properly plunder his house

###### v30
a. the one not being with Me
b. he is against Me
c. and the one not gathering with Me
d. he scatters

###### v31
a. because of this I say to you
b. every sin and blasphemy will be forgiven people
c. but the blasphemy of the Spirit will not be forgiven

###### v32
a. and who ever speaks (a) word against the Son of Man
b. it will be forgiven him
c. but who ever speaks against the Holy Spirit
d. it will not be forgiven him
e. neither in this age nor in the one coming

###### v33
a. either make the tree good
b. and then the fruit of it (is) good
c. or make the tree bad
d. and then the fruit of it (is) bad
e. because the tree is known by the fruit

###### v34
a. brood of vipers
b. how can you say good things (while) being wicked?
c. because from the abundance of the heart the mouth speaks

###### v35
a. the good person causes good things to come from the good storeroom
b. and the bad person causes bad things to come from the bad storeroom

###### v36
a. further I say to you that
b. every careless word that people speak
c. they will give account of it on (the) day of judgment

###### v37
a. by your words indeed you will be acquitted
b. and by your words you will be condemned

###### v38
a. then some of the Law experts and Pharisees spoke to Him saying
b. teacher we want to see (a) sign by you

###### v39
a. but having answered He said to them
b. (a) wicked and unfaithful generation desires (a) sign
c. yet (a) sign will not be given to her
d. except the sign of Jonah the prophet

###### v40
a. because just as Jonah was in the belly of the big fish three days and three nights
b. thus the Son of Man will be in the depths of the earth three days and  

###### v41
a. (the) Ninevite people will rise to life at the judgement along with this generation
b. and they will condemn her
c. for they repented at the preaching of Jonah
d. and look
e. more than Jonah (is) here

###### v42
a. (the) queen of (the) south will be raised up at the judgment along with this generation
b. and she will condemn it
c. because she came from the ends of the earth
d. to hear the wisdom of Solomon
e. and look
f. more than Solomon (is) here

###### v43
a. now when the unclean spirit comes out from the person
b. he goes around through waterless places
c. looking for (a) place to rest
d. yet he finds not

###### v44
a. then he says
b. I will turn back to my home from where I left
c. and having gone he finds (it) empty swept and decorated

###### v45
a. then he goes away
b. and he brings along with himself seven other evil spirits more wicked than himself
c. and then having gone in he resides there
d. and the last of that man becomes worse than the first
e. thus it will be also with this wicked generation

b. look

d. desiring to speak to Him

b. who is My mother
c. and who are My brothers?

###### v49

b. look
c. My mother and My brothers

###### v50
