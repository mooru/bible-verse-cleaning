###### v1
a. understand what kind of love the Father has given to us
b. that we may be called children of God
c. indeed we are
d. because of this the world does not know us
e. because it did not know Him

###### v2
a. beloved
b. now we are children of God
c. and it was not yet shown what we will be
d. we know that when it will be shown
e. we will be like Him
f. because we will see Him as He is

###### v3
a. and everyone who has this hope in Him
b. purifies himself morally
c. just as He is morally pure

###### v4
a. everyone committing sin
b. also commits lawlessness
c. indeed sin is lawlessness

###### v5
a. and you know that He appeared
b. in order to do away with the sins
c. yet in Him there is no sin

###### v6
a. everyone remaining in Him
b. does not sin
c. everyone sinning
d. has no perception of Him
e. and has no knowledge of Him

###### v7
a. children
b. let no one deceive you
c. the one doing what is right
d. is just
e. as He is just

###### v8
a. the one doing sin
b. is from the devil
c. because the devil sins from (the) beginning
d. for this the Son of God appeared
e. so that He may undo the works of the devil

###### v9
a. anyone born from God
b. does not do sin
c. because His nature remains in him
d. and he is not able to sin
e. because he has been born from God

###### v10
a. by this it is evident
b. (who are) the children of God
c. and (who are) the children of the devil
d. everyone not doing what is right
e. is not from God
f. also the one not loving his fellow believer

###### v11
a. because this is the message
b. that you heard from (the) beginning
c. that we should love one another

###### v12
a. not like Cain (who) was from the evil one
b. and he killed his brother
c. and because of what did he kill him?
d. because his deeds were evil
e. but those of his brother (were) righteous

###### v13
a. [and] be not surprised fellow believers
b. if the world hates you

###### v14
a. we know that we have gone from death to life
b. because we love the fellow believers
c. the one not loving
d. remains in death

###### v15
a. anyone hating his fellow believer
b. is (a) murderer
c. and you know that every murderer does not have eternal life   

###### v16
a. by this we know love
b. because He laid down His life for our sake
c. also we must for our fellow believers lay down our lives

###### v17
a. and who ever has the possessions of the world
b. and sees his fellow believer having need
c. and closes his heart from him
d. how does the love of God remain in him?

###### v18
a. children
b. let us not love with appearance neither with talk
c. but with deed and truth

###### v19
a. [and] by this we will know that we are from the truth
b. and before Him we will have confidence regarding our inner self

###### v20
a. because if our inner self condemns (us)
b. God in fact is greater than our inner self
c. and He knows everything

###### v21
a. beloved
b. if [our] inner self does not condemn (us)
c. we have confidence before God

###### v22
a. and what ever we ask we receive from Him
b. because we obey His commands
c. and we do what (is) pleasing in His judgment

###### v23
a. and this is His command namely
b. we must believe in the Name of His Son Jesus Christ
c. and we must love one another
d. as He gave us command

###### v24
a. and the one obeying His commands
b. remains in Him
c. and He in him
d. and by this we know that He remains in us
e. by the Spirit Whom He gave to us
