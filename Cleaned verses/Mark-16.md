###### v1
a. when the Sabbath passed
b. Mary Magdalene and Mary the mother of James and Salmone bought 
c. so that having gone they could anoint Him

###### v2
a. then very early morning on the first (day) of the week they come to  the tomb
b. the sun having risen

###### v3
a. then they said to themselves
b. who will roll away for us the stone from the entrance of the tomb?

###### v4
a. then having looked up they see that
b. the stone has been rolled away
c. it was indeed very large

###### v5
a. then having gone into the tomb
b. they saw (a) young man sitting on the right side
c. he was clothed in (a) long shining robe
d. and they were alarmed

###### v6
a. but he says to them
b. do not be alarmed
c. you are looking for Jesus the Nazarene the One crucified
d. He has been raised to life
e. He is not here
f. see the place where they laid Him

###### v7
a. certainly go tell His disciples and Peter that
b. He goes ahead of you to Galilee
c. there you will see Him
d. just as He told you

###### v8
a. then having gone out
b. they ran away from the tomb
c. for trembling and amazement took possession of them
d. and they said nothing to no one
e. because they were afraid

###### v9
a. then having risen early morning on (the) first (day) of (the) week
b. He appeared first to Mary Magdalene from whom He drove out seven demons

###### v10
a. she having gone
b. told those having been with Him who were mourning and weeping

###### v11
a. now they having heard that
b. He lives and was seen by her
c. they did not believe

###### v12
a. then after these things He appeared in (a) diffent visual form to two of them walking
b. while they were travelling to (the) countryside

###### v13
a. then they having gone away reported to the others
b. but they did not believe them

###### v14
a. [but] later while they were eating a meal
b. He appeared to the eleven
c. and reprimanded their unbelief and stubbornness
d. because they did not believe those having seen Him raised to life

###### v15
a. also He said to them
b. go to the world
c. preach the whole gospel to the entire creation

###### v16
a. the one having believed and having been baptised
b. will be saved
c. but the one having not believed
d. will be condemned

###### v17
a. and these signs will accompany for those having believed
b. in My name they will drive out demons
c. they will speak new languages

###### v18
a. [and with their hands] they will pick up snakes
b. even if they drink something deadly
c. it will not at all harm them
d. on sick people they will lay hands
e. and they will be healthy

###### v19
a. now then the Lord Jesus after speaking to them
b. He was taken up into heaven
c. and then He sat down at the right side of God

###### v20
a. then they having gone out
b. they preached everywhere
c. while the Lord worked together with (them)
d. and caused (people) to believe the message
e. through the accompanying signs
