###### v1
a. on that day Jesus having left the house
b. He sat down by the sea

###### v2
a. and (a) large crowd assembled with Him
b. so that He having embarked into (a) boat sat down
c. and the whole crowd stood on the shore

###### v3
a. then He spoke to them many things in parables saying
b. look
c. (a) sower went out to sow

###### v4
a. now during his sowing
b. some then fell along the road
c. and the birds having come ate it up

###### v5
a. and other fell on the rocky ground
b. where it did not have much soil
c. and it sprouted right away
d. because of not having depth of soil

###### v6
a. now (the) sun having come up it was scorched
b. also because of not having root it withered

###### v7
a. and other fell among the thorn plants
b. and then the thorn plants grew up
c. and they choked them

###### v8
a. but other fell on the good soil
b. and it produced fruit
c. some what (is) one hundred others what (is) sixty others what (is) thirty

###### v9
a. the one able to hear let him listen

###### v10
a. then the disciples having approached said to Him
b. due to what do you speak to them in parables?

###### v11
a. and He having answered said to them that
b. to you it has been given to know the mysteries of the Kingdom of Heaven
c. but to them it has not been given

###### v12
a. for whoever has
b. to him it will be given
c. and he will be provided abundantly
d. but whoever does not have
e. even what he has
f. it will be taken away from him

###### v13
a. because of this I speak to them in parables
b. because seeing they do not perceive
c. and hearing they do not understand
d. neither do they understand

###### v14
a. indeed to them the prophesy of Isaiah is being fulfilled saying
b. by hearing you will hear
c. yet by no means you may understand
d. and seeing you will see
e. yet by no means you may understand

###### v15
a. because the mind of this nation became unable to understand
b. and they were slow to understand
c. and they refused to learn
d. so that they may not see with the eyes
e. and they may hear with the ears
f. and with the mind they may understand
g. and then they may change their ways
h. and I will heal them

###### v16
a. but happy (are) your eyes because they see
b. and your ears because they hear

###### v17
a. because truly I say to you that
b. many prophets and righteous people longed
c. to see what you see
d. yet they did not see
e. and to hear what you hear
f. yet they did not hear

###### v18
a. you then understand the parable of the sower

###### v19
a. anyone hearing the message of the Kingdom
b. and not understanding
c. the evil one comes
d. and snatches what has been sown in his mind
e. this one is the one having been sown along the road

###### v20
a. but the one having been sown on the rocky ground
b. this one is the one hearing the message
c. and then right away with joy receiving it

###### v21
a. but he has no root in himself
b. and he is temporary
c. now having come trouble or persecution because of the message
d. immediately he gives up believing

###### v22
a. further the one having been sown among the thorn plants
b. this one is the one hearing the message
c. yet the concern of the world's standards
d. and the deception of wealth
e. overwhelms the message
f. and it becomes unproductive

###### v23
a. but the one having been sown on the good soil
b. this one is the one listening to the message
c. and understanding (it)
d. who then produces results
e. and produces some what (is) one hundred others what (is) sixty others what (is) thirty

###### v24
a. another parable He told them saying
b. the Kingdom of Heaven is similar to
c. (a) man having sown good seed in his field

###### v25
a. but while the people were asleep
b. his enemy came
c. and he sowed also weeds among the wheat
d. and then he went away

###### v26
a. but when the blades sprouted
b. and produced fruit
c. then appeared also the weeds

###### v27
a. and the slaves of the master of the household having approached said to him
b. sir did you not indeed sow good seed in your field?
c. from where then does it have weeds?

###### v28
a. and then he said to them
b. (a) hostile man did this
c. then the slaves say to him
d. do you then want we having gone pluck them?

###### v29
a. but he says no
b. lest plucking the weeds
c. you might uproot the wheat with them

###### v30
a. let both remain to grow together until the harvest
b. then at (the) time of the reaping I will say to the reapers
c. gather first the weeds
d. and tie them into bundles
e. in order to burn them
f. and then collect together the wheat into my barn

###### v31
a. another parable He told them saying
b. the Kingdom of Heaven is like (a) seed of (the) mustard plant
c. that (a) person having taken sowed in his field

###### v32
a. though it is smallest of all the seeds
b. but when it grew it is larger than the garden plants
c. and it becomes (a) tree
d. so that the wild birds come
e. and they make nests in its branches

###### v33
a. another parable He told them
b. the Kingdom of Heaven is like yeast
c. that (a) woman having taken put into three measures of flour
d. until it was completely leavened

###### v34
a. all these things Jesus spoke in parables to the crowds
b. and without (a) parable He spoke nothing to them

###### v35
a. so that the (word) spoken by the prophet was fulfilled saying
b. I will start my speaking by means of parables
c. I will announce things kept secret since (the) creation [of world]

###### v36
a. then having dismissed the crowds
b. He went into the house
c. then His disciples approached Him saying
d. explain to us the parable of the weeds of the field

###### v37
a. and He having answered said
b. the One sowing the good seed is the Son of Man

###### v38
a. and the field is the world
b. and the good seed these are the people of God
c. and the weeds are the people of the evil one

###### v39
a. and the enemy the one having sown these is the devil
b. and the harvest is (the) end of (the) times
c. and the reapers are (the) angels

###### v40
a. just as then the weeds are gathered
b. and burned down by fire
c. thus it will be at the end of the world system

###### v41
a. the Son of Man will send out His angels
b. and they will gather out of His Kingdom
c. all those causing people to sin
d. also the ones doing lawlessness

###### v42
a. and they will throw them into the furnace of fire
b. there will be weeping and grinding of teeth

###### v43
a. then the righteous will shine as the sun in the Kingdom of their Father
b. the one able to hear let him listen

###### v44
a. the Kingdom of Heaven is similar to (a) treasure
b. hidden in the field
c. that (a) man having found hid
d. and because of his joy he goes away
e. and he sells everything whatever he has
f. and he buys that field

###### v45
a. again the Kingdom of Heaven is similar to (a) person
b. (a) merchant trying to obtain beautiful pearls

###### v46
a. and finding one expensive pearl
b. having gone off he sold all whatever he had
c. and he bought it

###### v47
a. again the Kingdom of Heaven is like (a) dragnet
b. thrown into the sea
c. and having collected some of every kind (of fish)

###### v48
a. when it was filled
b. having drawn (it) up on the shore
c. and having sat down they gathered the good ones into containers
d. but the bad ones they threw away

###### v49
a. so it will be at the end of times
b. the angels will go out
c. and they will separate the wicked from among the righteous

###### v50
a. and they will throw them into the furnace of fire
b. there will be weeping and grinding of teeth

###### v51
a. do you understand all these things?
b. they say to Him  yes

###### v52
a. and then He said to them
b. because of this every teacher having been made a disciple to the Kingdom of Heaven
c. is like (a) person (being a) master of a household
d. who brings forth out of his storeroom new and old things

###### v53
a. then it happened when Jesus finished these parables
b. He departed from there

###### v54
a. and having come into His hometown
b. He taught them in their synagogue
c. so that they are amazed and say
d. from where (is) to this One this wisdom and the abilities?

###### v55
a. is not this One the son of the carpenter?
b. is not His mother called Mary?
c. and His brothers (called) James and Joseph and Simon and Judas?

###### v56
a. and His sisters are they not indeed all with us?
b. from where then all these things to this One?

###### v57
a. and then they took offense at Him
b. but Jesus said to them
c. (a) prophet is not without honour
d. except in his hometown
e. and even in his household

###### v58
a. further He did there not many miracles
b. because of their unbelief
