###### v1
a. in those days again (a) large crowd being there
b. and not having something they could eat
c. having called His disciples to Him He says to them

###### v2
a. I feel compassion for the crowd
b. because already three days they are staying with Me
c. and they do not have something they could eat

###### v3
a. further if I will send them away very hungry to their home
b. they will faint on the journey
c. indeed some of them came from far away

###### v4
a. then His disciples answered Him that
b. how will one be able to feed them bread here in (the) desert?

###### v5
a. then He asked them
b. how many loaves of bread do you have?
c. and they said seven

###### v6
a. then He orders the crowd to sit down to eat on the ground
b. then having taken the seven loaves
c. having given thanks he broke the bread
d. and He gave to His disciples
e. so that they may give food to (them)
f. and they gave the food to the crowd

###### v7
a. also they had (a) few small fish
b. then having asked God to bless them
c. He said also to give these (as) food to (them)

###### v8
a. and they ate
b. and they ate enough
c. and they gathered (an) abundance of pieces seven large baskets

###### v9
a. now they were about four thousand
b. and then He sent them away

###### v10
a. and right then having got into the boat with His disciples
b. He came into the regions of Dalmanutha

###### v11
a. then the Pharisees went out and began to argue with Him
b. seeking from Him (a) sign from heaven
c. trying to trap Him

###### v12
a. and groaning deeply in His spirit He says
b. why does this generation seek (a) sign?
c. truly I tell you that
d. (a) sign will be given to this generation

###### v13
a. then having departed from them
b. again having embarked He went away to the other side

###### v14
a. and they forgot to take bread
b. and they did not have with them in the boat except one loaf

###### v15
a. then He ordered them saying
b. pay attention
c. watch out for the yeast of the Pharisees and the yeast of Herod

###### v16
a. then they reasoned with one another that
b. they do not have bread

###### v17
a. then having known He says to them
b. why do you reason that
c. you do not have bread?
d. do you not yet perceive and do you not understand?
e. are you unwilling to learn in your inner selves?

###### v18
a. having eyes you do not see
b. and having ears you do not hear?
c. and do you not remember

###### v19
a. when I broke the five loaves for the five thousand
b. how many large baskets full of pieces did you pick up?
c. they say to Him twelve

###### v20
a. when (I broke) the seven for the four thousand
b. how many large baskets full of pieces did you pick up?
c. then they say [to Him] seven

###### v21
a. and He said to them
b. do you not yet understand?

###### v22
a. then they come into Bethsaida
b. and they bring to Him (a) blind man
c. and they asked Him earnestly that
d. He should touch him

###### v23
a. then having taken hold of the hand of the blind man
b. He led him outside the village
c. then having spit into his eyes
d. having laid His hands on him
e. He asked him
f. is it that you see anything?

###### v24
a. and having looked up he said
b. I see people that
c. like trees I see (them) walking

###### v25
a. then again He placed His hands on his eyes
b. and then he saw clearly
c. and he was restored
d. yes he looked straight at everything clearly

###### v26
a. then He sent him to his home saying
b. do not even go into the village

###### v27
a. then Jesus and His disciples went out into the villages of Caesarea Philippi
b. and during the journey He asked His disciples saying to them
c. who do the people say I am?

###### v28
a. and they spoke saying to Him [that]
b. John the Baptiser
c. and others Elijah
d. and others (say) that
e. (He is) one of the prophets

###### v29
a. then He Himself asked them
b. and you who do you say I am?
c. Peter having answered says to Him
d. You are the Messiah

###### v30
a. and He commanded them that
b. they should speak to no one about Him

###### v31
a. then He began to teach them that
b. the Son of Man must suffer much
c. and be rejected by the elders and the chief priests and the Law experts
d. and be killed
e. and after three days rise to life

###### v32
a. and with boldness He spoke the message
b. now Peter having taken Him aside
c. he began to rebuke Him

###### v33
a. then He having turned around
b. and having looked at His disciples
c. He rebuked Peter and says
d. go away behind Me Satan
e. because you do not think about the things of God but the things of people

###### v34
a. then having called to Him the crowd together with His disciples
b. He said to them
c. if anyone intends to follow after Me
d. let him disregard himself
e. and let him take up his cross
f. and let him follow Me

###### v35
a. for who ever wants to save his live
b. will lose it
c. but who ever will lose his life for my sake and the gospel
d. will save it

###### v36
a. because what does it help (a) person to gain the whole world
b. and that his life is lost?

###### v37
a. because what would (a) person give in payment for his life?

###### v38
a. for who ever is ashamed of Me and My message in this adulterous and sinful generation
b. also of him the Son of Man will be ashamed
c. when He comes in the glory of His Father with the holy angels

###### v39
a. then He said to them
b. truly I say to you that
c. there are some of those standing here
d. who will surely not experience death
e. until they would see the Kingdom of God having come with power
