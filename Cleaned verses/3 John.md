###### v1
a. the elder
b. to Gaius the beloved
c. whom I really love

###### v2
a. beloved
b. concerning all things I pray that you get along well and be healthy
c. just as your inner self gets along well

###### v3
a. I then was very happy
b. about fellow believers coming and witnessing to your truth
c. how you live in truth

###### v4
a. I have no greater joy than this that
b. I hear my children live in the truth

###### v5
a. beloved
b. you do (the) faithful thing
c. what ever you do for the fellow believers
d. also this (you do for) strangers

###### v6
a. who testified to your love before (the) church
b. to whom you will do well
c. having sent (them) on their way worthy of God

###### v7
a. because for the sake of His Name they went out
b. having taken nothing from the Gentiles

###### v8
a. we then have an obligation to help such people
b. so that we may be fellow workers for the truth

###### v9
a. I wrote something to the church
b. but Diotrephes desiring to order them
c. does not listen to us

###### v10
a. because of this when I come
b. I will remind (him) of his deeds
c. which he does with evil words
d. talking nonsense about us
e. and not being satisfied with this
f. and he himself does not welcome the fellow believers
g. and he prevents those wanting (to do it)
h. and he drives (them) out of the church

###### v11
a. beloved
b. do not imitate the evil
c. but the good
d. the one doing good is from God
e. the one doing evil has not experienced God

###### v12
a. Demetrius has been testified to by all
b. and by the truth itself
c. and also we testify
d. and you know that
e. our testimony is true

###### v13
a. I had much to write to you
b. but I do not want to write to you with ink and pen

###### v14
a. further I hope to visit you right away
b. and then we will speak face to face

###### v15
a. peace to you
b. the friends (here) greet you
c. greet the friends (there) by name
