###### v1
a. further welcome the one being weak in faith
b. not with disputes about doubts

###### v2
a. one then believes to eat anything
b. but the weak eats vegetables

###### v3
a. the one eating should not despise the one not eating
b. and the one not eating should not condemn the one eating
c. because God accepted him

###### v4
a. who are you judging another's servant?
b. to his own master he stands or fails
c. but he will continue
d. because the Lord can uphold him

###### v5
a. the one then [indeed] considers (a) day above (a) day
b. but another considers every day (alike)
c. let each one be completely certain in his own mind

###### v6
a. the one respecting the day respects (it) for (the) Lord
b. and the one eating eats for (the) Lord
c. because he gives thanks to God
d. and the one not eating does not eat for (the) Lord
e. and he gives thanks to God

###### v7
a. for no one of us lives for himself
b. and no one dies for himself

###### v8
a. and for when we live
b. we live for the Lord
c. and when we die
d. we die for the Lord
e. and so then when we live
f. and when we die
g. we are of the Lord

###### v9
a. for this indeed Christ died and became alive again
b. so that He may be Lord of both (the) dead and (the) living

###### v10
a. and you why do you judge your fellow believer?
b. or you also why do you despise your fellow believer?
c. for we all will stand before the judgment seat of God

###### v11
a. for it is written
b. I live says (the) Lord that
c. to Me every knee shall bow
d. and every person shall confess God

###### v12
a. so [then] each of us will give account [to God] about himself

###### v13
a. let us then no longer judge one another
b. but rather consider this
c. not to cause (a) stumbling or trap for the fellow believer

###### v14
a. I know and I am convinced because of (the) Lord Jesus that
b. nothing (is) defiled on account of itself
c. except for the one regarding something to be defiled
d. to that one (it is) defiled

###### v15
a. for if your fellow believer is distressed because of food
b. you no longer live in accordance with love
c. do not ruin by your food that one for whom Christ died

###### v16
a. let not then the good of you be slandered

###### v17
a. because the Kingdom of God is not food and drink
b. but righteousness and peace and joy in (the) Holy Spirit

###### v18
a. the one then serving Christ in this (way)
b. (is) pleasing to God
c. and respected by people

###### v19
a. so then let us pursue the things of peace
b. and the things of edification for one another

###### v20
a. do not destroy because of food the work of God
b. all (food) indeed (is) pure
c. but evil to the person eating because of (causing) offence

###### v21
a. (it is) good not to eat meat
b. and not to drink wine
c. and nothing by which your fellow believer takes offence

###### v22
a. (the) faith [that] you have
b. have (it) by yourself in the opinion of God
c. happy (is) the one not judging himself with regard to what he judges to be good

###### v23
a. but the one doubting is condemned if he eats
b. since (it is) not from faith
c. and all that (is) not from faith is sin
