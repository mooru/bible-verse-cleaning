###### v1
a. at that time Herod the tetrarch heard the news about Jesus

###### v2
a. and he said to his servants
b. this Man is John the Baptiser
c. he himself was raised from the dead
d. and because of this the powers work in Him

###### v3
a. because Herod having arrested John
b. he chained [him]
c. and he put (him) away in prison
d. because of Herodias the wife of Philip his brother

###### v4
a. because John said to him
b. it is not allowed to you to have her

###### v5
a. yet wanting to kill him
b. he feared the common people
c. because they regarded him as (a) prophet

###### v6
a. now (the) birthday celebration of Herod happened
b. the daughter of Herodias danced in the midst
c. and she pleased Herod

###### v7
a. therefore he swore to her by (an) oath
b. to give (her) what ever she might ask

###### v8
a. and then she having been urged by her mother
b. said give me here on (a) plate the head of John the Baptiser

###### v9
a. now the king made sad because of the oath and the people associating
b. he commanded (her request) to be granted

###### v10
a. and having sent
b. he cut John the head off in the prison

###### v11
a. and then his head was brought on (a) plate
b. and it was given to the girl
c. and she brought (it) to her mother

###### v12
a. then his disciples having come there
b. removed the dead body
c. and they buried him
d. then having gone they informed Jesus

###### v13
a. now Jesus having heard (it)
b. went away from there in (a) boat to (a) deserted place alone
c. yet the crowds having heard
d. came after Him on foot from the cities

###### v14
a. then having left (the boat)
b. He saw (a) large crowd
c. and He had compassion for them
d. and He healed their sick

###### v15
a. now evening having come
b. the disciples approached Him saying
c. the place is uninhabited
d. and the hour is already late
e. let the crowd go away
f. so that having gone into the villages
g. they may buy food for themselves

###### v16
a. but [Jesus] said to them
b. they have no need to go away
c. you give them to eat

###### v17
a. but they say to Him
b. we have not (food) here except five loaves and two fish

###### v18
a. and He said
b. bring them here to Me

###### v19
a. and having ordered the crowd to sit down to eat on the grass
b. having taken the five loaves and the two fish
c. having looked up to the sky He said a blessing
d. and having broken (it) He gave the food to the disciples
e. and the disciples (gave) to the crowd

###### v20
a. and they all ate
b. and they were satisfied
c. and they took up the leftover of the pieces
d. twelve large baskets full

###### v21
a. now those eating were about five thousand men
b. apart from women and children

###### v22
a. then right away He compelled the disciples to get into the boat
b. and to go ahead of Him to the other side
c. while He let the crowd go away

###### v23
a. then having dismissed the crowd
b. He went up onto the mountain alone to pray
c. and evening having come He was there alone

###### v24
a. now the boat already was away many stades from the land
b. tormented by the waves
c. because the wind was against (them)

###### v25
a. then in (the) fourth watch of the night He came to them
b. walking on the sea

###### v26
a. now the disciples having seen Him walking on the sea
b. were distressed saying that
c. it is (a) ghost
d. and they shouted because of fear

###### v27
a. but immediately [Jesus] spoke to them saying
b. have courage  I am I
c. do not be afraid

###### v28
a. then Peter having replied said to Him
b. Lord  if You are You
c. command me to come to You on the water

###### v29
a. and He said  come
b. then Peter having got down from the boat
c. walked on the water
d. and he came to Jesus

###### v30
a. but noticing the [strong] wind
b. he was afraid
c. and having begun to sink
d. he shouted saying
e. Lord  save me

###### v31
a. and immediately Jesus having stretched out His hand took hold of him
b. and He says to him
c. you of little faith about what did you doubt?

###### v32
a. now they having got up into the boat
b. the wind ceased

###### v33
a. then those in the boat worshipped Him saying
b. You are truly (the) Son of God

###### v34
a. then having crossed over
b. they came upon the land at Gennesaret

###### v35
a. now the people of that place having recognized Him
b. they sent word to all that surrounding region
c. and they brought to Him all the ones being sick

###### v36
a. and they asked Him earnestly that
b. they may merely touch the fringe of His coat
c. and as many as touched
d. they were made well
