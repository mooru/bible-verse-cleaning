###### v1
a. then Jesus spoke to the crowds and to His disciples saying

###### v2
a. the Law experts and the Pharisees are authorities on the Law of Moses

###### v3
a. everything then what ever they say to you
b. do (it) and obey (it)
c. but act not in accordance with their deeds
d. because they speak
e. yet they do not act (accordingly)

###### v4
a. further they bind on heavy burdens [and hard to bear]
b. and they place (them) on the shoulders of the people
c. but they themselves are not willing to remove them with their finger

###### v5
a. they further do all their deeds
b. in order to be seen by the people
c. because they make their phylacteries broad
d. and they make their tassels large

###### v6
a. they further love the seat of honour at the banquets
b. as well as the seats of honour in the synagogues

###### v7
a. and also the  greetings in the marketplaces
b. and to be called by the people rabbi

###### v8
a. but you should not be called rabbi
b. because One is your Teacher
c. and you all are fellow believers

###### v9
a. further do not call (anyone) of you on the earth father
b. because One is your Father the heavenly One

###### v10
a. and do not be called instructors
b. because One is your Instructor the Christ

###### v11
a. further the greater of you shall be your servant

###### v12
a. but whoever will exalt himself will be humbled
b. and whoever will humble himself will be exalted

###### v13
a. now horror to you Law experts and Pharisees hypocrites
b. because you shut the Kingdom of Heaven in front of the people
c. indeed you do not enter
d. and you do not allow those entering to go in

###### v15
a. horror to you Law experts and Pharisees hypocrites
b. because you travel about the sea and the dry land
c. to make one proselyte
d. and when it happens
e. you make him (a) son of hell twice as much than you

###### v16
a. horror to you blind leaders saying
b. who ever swears by the temple
c. it is nothing
d. but who ever swears by the gold of the temple
e. he is under obligation

###### v17
a. (you) foolish and blind what indeed is more important
b. the gold
c. or the temple consecrating the gold?

###### v18
a. and who ever swears by the altar
b. it is nothing
c. but who ever swears by the gift upon it
d. he is under obligation

###### v19
a. (you) blind
b. what indeed (is) more important
c. the gift
d. or the altar dedicating the gift to God?

###### v20
a. the one then having sworn by the altar
b. he swears by it and by everything upon it

###### v21
a. and the one having sworn by the temple
b. he swears by it and by the One dwelling in it

###### v22
a. and the one having sworn by heaven
b. he swears by the throne of God and by the One sitting upon it

###### v23
a. horror to you Law experts and Pharisees hypocrites
b. because you tithe mint and dill and cumin
c. yet you gave up the more important things of the Law
d. namely justice and mercy and trust
e. these [now] (you) were always compelled to do
f. and those not to stop (doing)

###### v24
a. blind leaders
b. you the ones straining out the gnat
c. but swallowing the camel

###### v25
a. horror to you Law experts and Pharisees hypocrites
b. because you clean the outside of the cup and of the dish
c. but on the inside they are full of violent greed and lack of self-control

###### v26
a. blind Pharisees
b. clean first what is inside the cup
c. so that also its outside may become clean

###### v27
a. horror to you Law experts and Pharisees hypocrites
b. because you are very similar to whitewashed tombs
c. who from outside indeed appear to be beautiful
d. but from inside they are full of dead bones and all sorts of immorality

###### v28
a. thus also you from outside then appear to the people to be righteous
b. but within you are full of hypocrisy and lawlessness

###### v29
a. horror to you Law experts and Pharisees hypocrites
b. because you build the tombs of the prophets
c. and you decorate the monuments of the righteous

###### v30
a. also you say
b. if we were there in the days of our forefathers
c. we would not have been their partners in the killing of the prophets

###### v31
a. so then you witness to yourselves that
b. you are followers of those having murdered the prophets

###### v32
a. and you must fulfill the measure of your forefathers

###### v33
a. snakes brood of vipers
b. how would you escape from the punishment of hell?

###### v34
a. because of this look
b. I send to you prophets and wise men and teachers
c. some among them you will kill and crucify
d. also some among them you will whip in your synagogues
e. and (some) you will persecute from city to city

###### v35
a. so that there may come upon you
b. all righteous blood poured out upon the earth
c. from the death of the righteous Abel
d. up to the death of Zechariah son of Barachiah
e. whom you mudered between the temple and the altar

###### v36
a. truly I say to you
b. all these things will happen in the days of this generation

###### v37
a. Jerusalem Jerusalem
b. the one having killed the prophets
c. and having stoned to death those having been sent to her
d. how often I wanted to gather together your inhabitants
e. in which manner (a) hen gathers together her chicks under her wings
f. yet you wanted not

###### v38
a. look
b. your house is left uninhabited for you

###### v39
a. indeed I say to you
b. you will certainly not see Me after a short time from now
c. until you would say
d. blessed (is) the One coming in (the) Name of (the) Lord
