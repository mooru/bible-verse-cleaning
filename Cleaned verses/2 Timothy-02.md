###### v1
a. you therefore my son
b. become able by grace
c. the (grace) from Christ Jesus

###### v2
a. and what you heard from me by many witnesses
b. entrust that to faithful people
c. who will be able also to teach others

###### v3
a. join in suffering as (a) good soldier of Christ Jesus

###### v4
a. no one being a soldier
b. is involved in the affairs of daily life
c. so that he may please the commander

###### v5
a. further also if someone is an athlete
b. he will not receive the prize
c. unless he competes correctly

###### v6
a. the hard-working farmer
b. must first have a share of the fruit

###### v7
a. consider well what I say
b. indeed the Lord will give you insight in everything

###### v8
a. remember Jesus Christ
b. having been raised from (the) dead
c. from (the) lineage of David
d. according to my gospel

###### v9
a. for which I suffer hardship to the point of chains
b. as (an) evildoer
c. but the message of God is not chained

###### v10
a. due to this I endure all for the sake of the elect
b. so that also they themselves may have salvation
c. the (salvation) by Christ Jesus
d. with everlasting glory

###### v11
a. the statement (is) trustworthy
b. for if we died with (Him)
c. also we will live with (Him)

###### v12
a. if we endure
b. also we will reign with (Him)
c. if we will deny (Him)
d. also He will deny us

###### v13
a. if we are faithless
b. He remains faithful
c. for He can not be untrue to Himself

###### v14
a. remind (people) of these things
b. warning (them) before God
c. not to argue about words
d. (it is) useful for nothing
e. for harm of the hearers

###### v15
a. be eager yourself
b. to cause (you) to be worthy to God
c. (a) worker not feeling shame
d. teaching correctly the message of the truth

###### v16
a. and avoid the worldly foolish talk
b. for they will increase to more ungodliness

###### v17
a. and their message will have (a) spread like cancer
b. of whom is Hymenaeus and Philetus

###### v18
a. who lost their way with regard to the truth
b. saying that [the] resurrection has already happened
c. indeed they upset the faith of some

###### v19
a. however the firm foundation of God remains firmly
b. having this inscription
c. (the) Lord knows those being His
d. and
e. turn away from unrighteousness
f. everyone calling the Name of the Lord

###### v20
a. now in (a) large house there is not only golden and silver vessels
b. but also of wood and clay
c. indeed first some for honour and then others for dishonour

###### v21
a. if then someone cleanses himself from these
b. he will be (a) vessel for honour
c. dedicated
d. useful to the Lord
e. made ready for every good work

###### v22
a. and avoid the youthful lusts
b. and pursue righteousness faith love peace
c. along with those appealing to the Lord
d. with (a) pure inner being

###### v23
a. and avoid foolish and ignorant disputes
b. knowing that they cause fights to happen

###### v24
a. and (a) servant of (the) Lord must not fight
b. but (must) be gentle to all
c. able to teach
d. patient

###### v25
a. with gentleness teaching those showing hostility
b. if perhaps God may give them repentance
c. resulting in (the) knowledge of truth

###### v26
a. and may return to their senses
b. free from the snare of the devil
c. having been controlled by him
d. in order to (do) his will
