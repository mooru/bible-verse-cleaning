###### v1
a. now after (the) Sabbath
b. at the dawning on (the) first (day) of (the) week
c. Mary Magdalene and the other Mary came to observe the tomb

###### v2
a. and look
b. (a) great earthquake occurred
c. for (an) angel of (the) Lord having descended from heaven
d. and having approached rolled away the stone
e. and then sat down upon it

###### v3
a. and his appearance was as lightning
b. and his clothing white as snow

###### v4
a. and because of fear for him those guarding were filled with anxiety
b. and they became like dead ones

###### v5
a. then having spoken the angel said to the women
b. you must not fear
c. because I know that
d. you are seeking Jesus the crucified

###### v6
a. He is not here
b. because He was raised to life as He said
c. come here
d. see the place where He lay

###### v7
a. then go away quickly
b. tell His disciples that
c. He was raised from the dead
d. and look
e. He is going ahead of you to Galilee
f. there you will see Him
g. look
h. I told you

###### v8
a. then having left quickly from the grave with fear and great joy
b. they ran to inform His disciples

###### v9
a. and look
b. Jesus met up with them
c. saying greetings
d. and the women having come to (Him)
e. took hold of His feet
f. and they worshipped Him

###### v10
a. then Jesus says to them
b. do not be afraid
c. go tell My brothers
d. that they should go to Galilee
e. and there they will see Me

###### v11
a. now while they were going
b. look
c. some of the guard having gone into the city
d. told the chief preists all the things having happened

###### v12
a. then having gathered with the elders
b. and having taken council
c. they gave enough money to the soldiers

###### v13
a. saying
b. you must say that
c. His disciples having come during (the) night
d. stole Him
e. while we were asleep

###### v14
a. and if this would be heard by the governor
b. we will convince [him]
c. and we will cause you not to be worried

###### v15
a. then they having received the money
b. they did as they were taught
c. and this statement was spread among (the) Jews up to the present [days]

###### v16
a. now the eleven disciples made their way toward Galilee
b. to the mountain where Jesus commanded them

###### v17
a. then having seen Him they worshipped (Him)
b. yet some doubted

###### v18
a. then having approached Jesus spoke to them saying
b. to Me was given all power in heaven and on [the] earth

###### v19
a. having gone then make all the nations disciples
b. baptising them in the Name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit

###### v20
a. teaching them to obey all (the) things
b. as many as I commanded you
c. and look
d. I am with you all the days up to the end of the age
