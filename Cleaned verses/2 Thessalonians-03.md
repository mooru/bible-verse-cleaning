###### v1
a. finally pray for us fellow believers
b. so that the message of the Lord may spread
c. and be respected
d. just as also with you

###### v2
a. also that we may be rescued
b. from the bad and wicked people
c. because the faith (is) not of all

###### v3
a. however the Lord is faithful
b. Who will strengthen you
c. and will guard (you) from the evil one

###### v4
a. further we trust in (the) Lord about you that
b. what we command (you)
c. [also] you do and will do

###### v5
a. and may the Lord guide your inner selves
b. to the love of God
c. and to the endurance of Christ

###### v6
a. now we command you fellow believers
b. in (the) Name of [our] Lord Jesus Christ
c. you should avoid every fellow believer
d. living lazily
e. and not according to the tradition
f. that they received from us

###### v7
a. because you yourself know
b. how it is necessary to imitate us
c. for we were not lazy among you

###### v8
a. and not without paying we ate bread from anyone
b. but with labour and toil
c. working night and day
d. so as not to be a burden to anyone of you

###### v9
a. not that we have no authority
b. but that we may give ourselves as example to you
c. for you to imitate

###### v10
a. because also when we were with you
b. this we commanded you that
c. if someone does not want to work
d. let him also not eat

###### v11
a. because we hear some living lazily among you
b. not working at all
c. but being busybodies

###### v12
a. we now command such ones
b. and we urge in union with (the) Lord Jesus Christ that
c. working with peaceful living
d. they should eat their own bread

###### v13
a. but you fellow believers do not be discouraged
b. doing good

###### v14
a. but if someone does not obey our message
b. by means of this letter
c. you should take note of this
d. to not associate with him
e. so that he may be ashamed

###### v15
a. yet regard (him) not as (an) enemy
b. but admonish (him) as (a) fellow believer

###### v16
a. now may the Lord of peace Himself give to you
b. the peace always in every way
c. the Lord (be) with you all
