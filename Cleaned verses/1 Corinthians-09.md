###### v1
a. am I not free?
b. am I not (an) apostle?
c. have I not indeed seen Jesus our Lord?
d. are you not my workmanship in (the) Lord?

###### v2
a. if to others I am not (an) apostle
b. certainly indeed I am to you
c. because you are my proof of apostleship in (the) Lord

###### v3
a. my defense to those criticising me is this

###### v4
a. do we not have (the) freedom to eat and to drink?

###### v5
a. do we not have (the) freedom to take along (a) woman (as) Christian sister
b. as also the other apostles and the brothers of the Lord and Cephas?

###### v6
a. or do only I and Barnabas have no freedom not to work?

###### v7
a. who ever is a soldier with own money for support?
b. who plants (a) vineyard
c. and then does not eat its fruit?
d. or who tends (a) flock
e. and do not drink from the milk of the flock?

###### v8
a. do I say these things like (a) human being
b. or does not even the Law say these things?

###### v9
a. because it has been written in the Law of Moses
b. you shall not muzzle (an) ox threshing
c. can there be a concern to God for oxen

###### v10
a. or does He speak at least for our sake?
b. indeed for our sake it was written that
c. the one ploughing must plough in hope
d. and the one threshing in hope of having a share

###### v11
a. if we sowed for you the spiritual things
b. (is it) strange if we reap your material things?

###### v12
a. if others share in your freedom
b. do we not more?
c. but we did not use this freedom
d. certainly we endure all things
e. so that we may cause not any obstacle to the gospel of Christ

###### v13
a. do you not know that
b. those working at the temple
c. eat [the things] from the temple
d. those serving at the altar
e. share at the altar?

###### v14
a. also in this way the Lord commanded
b. for those proclaiming the gospel
c. to live from the gospel

###### v15
a. now I did not make use of any of these things
b. further I did not write these things
c. so that it may happen likewise in the case of me
d. because for me (it is) good rather to die
e. rather (that) no one will cause my boasting to lose power

###### v16
a. indeed if I preach the gospel
b. for me it is not (a) right to boast
c. because it is (an) obligation for me
d. indeed how terrible it is for me
e. if I would not preach the gospel

###### v17
a. for if I do this willingly
b. I have reward
c. but if not willing
d. I have been entrusted with (a) task

###### v18
a. what then is my reward?
b. that preaching the gospel
c. I may explain the gospel free of charge
d. so as not to use my freedom with regard to the gospel

###### v19
a. in fact being free from all
b. I made myself a slave to all
c. so that I may gain more (people)

###### v20
a. indeed for the Jews I became like (a) Jew
b. so that I may gain Jews
c. for those under the Law (I became) like (one) under the Law
d. not being myself under the Law
e. so that I may gain those under the Law

###### v21
a. to those without the Law (I became) like (one) without the Law
b. not being without the Law of God
c. but subject to the law of Christ
d. so that I may gain those without the Law

###### v22
a. to the weak ones I became weak
b. so that I may gain the weak ones
c. to all those I have become everything
d. so that certainly I may save some

###### v23
a. yet everything I do for the sake of the gospel
b. so that I may become its sharer

###### v24
a. do you not know that
b. those running in (the) arena
c. indeed they all run
d. yet one receives the prize?
e. run in such a way
f. so that you may take (the prize)

###### v25
a. further everyone competing
b. excercises self-control in everything
c. those ones indeed then so that they may receive (a) perishable wreath
d. but we (an) imperishable one

###### v26
a. therefore I run in such a way as not aimlessly
b. I box in such a way as not striking air

###### v27
a. but I keep my body under control
b. and make (it) ready for service
c. so that not preaching to others
d. I myself may become worthless
