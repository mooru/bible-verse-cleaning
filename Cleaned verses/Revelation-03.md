###### v1
a. and to the messenger of the church in Sardis write
b. these things says the One holding the seven spirits of God and the seven stars
c. I know your works that
d. you have (the) reputation that you live
e. yet you are dead

###### v2
a. become alive
b. and strengthen the remaining ones
c. who were about to die
d. because I have not discovered your works
e. having been made complete in the opinion of my God

###### v3
a. remember then how you have received and you listened
b. indeed obey
c. and repent
d. but if you would not be watchful
e. I will come like (a) thief
f. and you will not at all know which hour I will come to you

###### v4
a. but you have (a) few persons in Sardis
b. who did not soil their garments
c. indeed they will walk with Me in white (clothes)
d. because they are worthy

###### v5
a. the one conquering will thus be adorned in white clothes
b. and I will surely not wipe out his name from the book of life
c. indeed I will confess his name before My Father
d. and also before His angels

###### v6
a. the one being able to hear
b. let him pay attention to what the Spirit says to the churches

###### v7
a. and to the messenger of the church in Philadelphia write
b. these things says the Holy One the True One
c. the One having the key of David
d. the One opening and no one will shut
e. and shutting and no one opens

###### v8
a. I know your works
b. pay attention
c. I have put in front of you (a) door having been opened
d. that no one is able to shut it
e. because you have little power
f. yet you obeyed My message
g. also you did not deny My Name

###### v9
a. pay attention
b. I will make from the synagogue of Satan
c. those saying that they themselves are Jews
d. yet they are not
e. certainly they lie
f. pay attention
g. I will cause them so that they will come
h. and they will bow down in front of your feet
i. also they will know that
j. I loved you

###### v10
a. because you obeyed My message of endurance
b. indeed I will guard you from the time of testing
c. the one being about to come upon the whole earth
d. to test those living on the earth

###### v11
a. I am coming soon
b. hold on to what you have
c. so that no one may take hold of your crown

###### v12
a. the one conquering
b. I will make him (a) pillar in the temple of My God
c. and out of (it) he will never ever go out
d. in addition also I will write on him the Name of My God
e. also the name of the city of My God (namely) the new Jerusalem
f. the one coming down from the heaven from My God
g. also My new Name

###### v13
a. the one being able to hear let him pay attention to
b. what the Spirit says to the churches

###### v14
a. and to the messenger of the church in Laodicea write
b. these things says the Amen
c. the faithful and true Witness
d. the Origin of the creation of God

###### v15
a. I know your works that
b. you are neither cold nor hot
c. would that you were cold or hot

###### v16
a. thus because you are lukewarm
b. and neither hot nor cold
c. I am about to vomit you out of My mouth

###### v17
a. because you say that
b. I am rich
c. indeed I have become rich
d. and I have need of nothing
e. yet you do not know that
f. you are the wretched one and pitiable
g. and poor and blind and naked

###### v18
a. I advise you to buy from Me
b. gold having been made fiery hot by fire
c. so that you may become rich
d. also white garments
e. so that you may be clothed
f. and the shame of your nakedness may not be revealed
g. also eye salve to rub on your eyes
h. so that you may see

###### v19
a. I as many as ever I love
b. I rebuke and I discipline
c. so then be earnest and repent

###### v20
a. pay attention
b. I have taken stand at the door and I knock
c. if anyone would hear My voice
d. and he would open the door
e. [then] I will come in to him
f. and I will eat a meal with him and he with Me

###### v21
a. the one conquering
b. I shall grant to him to sit with Me on My throne
c. just as also I conquered
d. and I sat down with My Father on His throne

###### v22
a. the one being able to hear let him pay attention to
b. what the Spirit says to the churches
