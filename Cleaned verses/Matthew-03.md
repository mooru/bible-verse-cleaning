###### v1
a. now in those days John the Baptiser appeared
b. preaching in the wilderness of Judea

###### v2
a. [and] saying
b. repent
c. because the Kingdom of Heaven has come near

###### v3
a. for this is what was said by Isaiah the prophet
b. saying
c. (a) voice of one shouting in the wilderness
d. prepare the way of (the) Lord
e. make straight His paths

###### v4
a. now John himself wore
b. his clothing made of (the) hair of (a) camel
c. and (a) leather belt around his waist
d. and his food was locusts and wild honey

###### v5
a. then went out to him
b. Jerusalem and all Judea
c. and all the surrounding region of the Jordan

###### v6
a. and they were baptised in the Jordan River by him
b. confessing their sins

###### v7
a. and having seen many of the Pharisees and Sadducees
b. coming to his baptism
c. he said to them
d. brood of vipers
e. who showed you to flee from the coming punishment?

###### v8
a. produce then results worthy of repentance

###### v9
a. and think not to say to yourselves
b. we have Abraham (as) ancestor
c. for I say to you that
d. God can from these stones raise descendants for Abraham

###### v10
a. but already the axe lies at the root of the trees
b. every tree then not producing good fruit
c. is cut down
d. and is thrown into (the) fire

###### v11
a. I though baptise you in water in accordance with repentance
b. but the One coming after me
c. is greater than I (am)
d. of Whom I am not worthy to carry (His) sandals
e. He will baptise you with (the) Holy Spirit and with fire

###### v12
a. of Whom the winnowing fork (is) in His hand
b. and He will clean out His threshing floor
c. and He will gather His wheat into the storehouse
d. but the chaff He will burn with unquenchable fire

###### v13
a. then Jesus arrived from Galilee
b. at the Jordan where John was
c. to be baptised by him

###### v14
a. but John prevented Him saying
b. I have need to be baptised by You
c. yet You come to me?

###### v15
a. but Jesus having replied said to him
b. leave it right now
c. for thus it is proper for us
d. to fulfill all religious requirements
e. then he allowed Him

###### v16
a. now Jesus having been baptised
b. He went up right away from the water
c. and look
d. the heavens were opened [to Him]
e. and He saw [the] Spirit of God
f. coming down like (a) dove
g. [and] coming onto Him

###### v17
a. and look
b. (a) voice from heaven saying
c. this is My beloved Son
d. in Whom I  took pleasure
