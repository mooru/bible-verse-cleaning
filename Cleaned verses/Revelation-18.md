###### v1
a. after these things I saw another angel
b. coming down out of heaven
c. having great authority
d. indeed the earth was shined upon by his glory

###### v2
a. and he shouted with (a) strong voice saying
b. she fell she fell Babylon the great
c. yes she became (a) dwelling place of demons
d. and (a) haunt of every unclean spirit
e. and (a) haunt of every unclean bird
f. [and (a) haunt of every unclean beast]
g. and of one having been hated

###### v3
a. because from the wine of the intense desire of her fornication
b. all the nations have drunk
c. and the kings of the earth committed fornication with her
d. indeed the merchants of the world became rich from the power of her sensual living

###### v4
a. then I heard another voice from heaven saying
b. go out My people from her
c. so that you would not associate with her sins
d. and so that you may not experience from her plagues

###### v5
a. because her sins increased greatly
b. indeed God remembered her unrighteous acts

###### v6
a. repay her as also she herself repaid
b. indeed double the double according to her deeds
c. in the cup in which she mixed
d. mix for her twice as much

###### v7
a. as much as she glorified herself
b. and she lived sensually
c. give to her so much torment and sorrow
d. because in her inner self she says that
e. I sit (as a) queen
f. indeed (a) widow I am not
g. and sorrow I would never see

###### v8
a. because of this in one day her plagues will come
b. death and sorrow and famine
c. and by fire she will be burned down
d. because God (is a) mighty Lord
e. the One having judged her

###### v9
a. then the kings of the earth will weep and mourn because of her
b. the ones having committed fornication with her
c. and having lived sensually (with her)
d. when they would see the smoke of her burning

###### v10
a. having taken stand from far away
b. because of the fear of her torment
c. saying horror horror the city the great one
d. Babylon the strong city
e. because in one hour came your punishment

###### v11
a. then the merchants of the earth weep
b. and they are sad because of her
c. because no one buys their cargo any longer

###### v12

b. and cloth of fine linen and purple cloth and silk cloth and scarlet
c. and every wood of citron
d. and every object made of ivory
e. and every object from very precious wood
f. and of bronze and of iron and of marble

###### v13
a. and cinnamon and spice
b. and incense and perfume and frankincense
c. and wine and olive oil and fine flour and wheat
d. and cattle and sheep and horses and wagons
e. and slaves and lives of people

###### v14
a. and your ripe fruit of the lust of your inner self
b. has passed away from you
c. and all the splendid things and the glamorous things
d. were lost from you
e. and no longer never ever will they find them

###### v15
a. the merchants of these having become rich because of her
b. they will stand from far away
c. because of the fear of her torment
d. weeping and mourning

###### v16
a. saying horror horror the city the great one
b. the one having been clothed in fine linen and purple and scarlet cloth
c. and having been adorned with [in] gold and precious stone and pearl

###### v17
a. for in one hour so much wealth was destroyed
b. and every captain of a ship and every sea traveller
c. also sailors and as many as are working on the sea
d. they stood from far away

###### v18
a. and they cried out
b. seeing the smoke of her burning
c. saying
d. who (is) similar to the city the great one?

###### v19
a. and they threw dust on their heads
b. and they shouted weeping and wailing saying
c. horror horror the city the great one
d. in which all became rich
e. the ones having the boats on the sea
f. because of her wealth
g. because in one hour it was destroyed

###### v20
a. rejoice over her o heaven
b. also God's people as well as the apostles and the prophets
c. because God judged the condemnation of you by her

###### v21
a. then one strong angel picked up (a) stone like (a) large millstone
b. and threw (it) into the sea saying
c. thus with (a) sudden violence will Babylon the great city be thrown down
d. and not ever would it be found anymore

###### v22
a. then (the) sound of harpists and of musicians
b. as well as of flutists and of trumpeters
c. would not at all be heard in you anymore
d. and any craftsman of any craft
e. would not at all be found in you anymore
f. also (the) sound of (a) millstone would not ever be heard in you anymore

###### v23
a. and (the) light of (a) lamp would not at all shine in you anymore
b. and (the) voice of (a) bridegroom and bride would not ever be heard in you anymore
c. for your merchants were the very important persons of the land
d. for by your sorcery all the nations were deceived

###### v24
a. yes in her (the) blood of prophets and of devout ones was found
