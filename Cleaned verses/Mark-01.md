###### v1
a. (the) beginning of the good news of Jesus Christ [Son of God]

###### v2
a. just as it has been written in Isaiah the prophet
b. pay attention
c. I send My angel ahead of You
d. who will prepare Your way

###### v3
a. (a) voice crying out in the desert
b. prepare the way of (the) Lord
c. make straight His paths

###### v4
a. it happened (that) John was baptising in the desert
b. and preaching (a) baptism of repentance for forgiveness of sins

###### v5
a. and the whole Judean region went out to him
b. and all the inhabitants of Jerusalem
c. and they were baptised by him in the Jordan river
d. confessing their sins

###### v6
a. now John was dressed in camel hair
b. and (a) leather belt around his waist
c. and eating locusts and wild honey

###### v7
a. and he preached saying
b. the One (who) comes after me (is) stronger than I
c. of Whom I am not worthy
d. having bent to untie the straps of His sandals

###### v8
a. I baptised you with water
b. but He will baptise you with (the) Holy Spirit

###### v9
a. then it happened in those days
b. Jesus came from Nazareth of Galilee
c. and He was baptised in the Jordan river by John

###### v10
a. and immediately coming up out of the water
b. He saw the sky being split open
c. and the Spirit like (a) dove coming down upon Him

###### v11
a. and (a) voice came out of the sky
b. You are My beloved Son
c. with You I am pleased

###### v12
a. and then the Spirit leads Him out into the desert

###### v13
a. and He was in the desert for forty days
b. being tempted by Satan
c. and He was among the animals
d. and the angels served Him

###### v14
a. now after John was handed over
b. Jesus went to Galilee
c. preaching the good news of God

###### v15
a. and saying that
b. the time has been fulfilled
c. and the Kingdom of God has come near
d. repent
e. and believe in the good news

###### v16
a. then going along the sea of Galilee
b. He saw Simon and Andrew the brother of Simon
c. casting a fishnet into the sea
d. for they were fishermen

###### v17
a. then Jesus said to them
b. come after Me
c. and I will make you to be fishers of people

###### v18
a. and immediately having left the nets
b. they followed Him

###### v19
a. now having moved on a little
b. He saw James the son of Zebedee and John his brother
c. and they were in the boat making the nets ready

###### v20
a. and then He called them
b. and having left their father Zebedee in the boat with the hired workers
c. they went away after Him

###### v21
a. and they entered into Capernaum
b. and then on the Sabbath having gone into the synagogue
c. He taught

###### v22
a. and they were greatly impressed with His teaching
b. for He was teaching them as one having authority
c. and not like the experts in the Law

###### v23
a. and then there was in their synagogue (a) man with (an) evil spirit
b. and he shouted saying

###### v24
a. what for us and for you Jesus (the) Nazarene?
b. did You come to destroy us?
c. I know You who You are
d. the Holy One of God

###### v25
a. then Jesus reprimanded him saying
b. be silent
c. and go out of him

###### v26
a. then the evil spirit having thrown him into a fit
b. and having shouted with (a) loud cry
c. he went out of him

###### v27
a. and all were amazed
b. so that they spoke to one another saying
c. what is this?
d. (a) new teaching with power
e. also He commands the evil spirits
f. and they obey Him

###### v28
a. then the news about Him went out immediately everywhere
b. into the whole surrounding region of Galilee

###### v29
a. now then having gone out of the synagogue
b. He came to the house of Simon and Andrew
c. along with James and John

###### v30
a. now the mother-in-law of Simon was lying having a fever
b. and then they tell Him about her

###### v31
a. and having come there He healed her
b. having taken her hand
c. and the fever left her
d. and then she served them

###### v32
a. now evening having come
b. when the sun set
c. they brought to Him all
d. those being ill
e. and those being demon possessed

###### v33
a. then the whole town was gathered at the door

###### v34
a. and He healed many being ill with various diseases
b. and He cast out many demons
c. and He did not allow the demons to speak
d. because they knew Him

###### v35
a. then early morning still at night having got up He went out
b. and He went away to (an) uninhabited place
c. and there He prayed

###### v36
a. then Simon as well as those with him searched for Him

###### v37
a. and they found Him
b. and they say to Him that
c. all look for You

###### v38
a. and He says to them
b. let us go elsewhere to the neighbouring towns
c. so that I will preach also there
d. because for this I came

###### v39
a. then He went preaching in their synagogues in the whole Galilee
b. and casting out demons

###### v40
a. now (a) leper comes to Him
b. asking Him [and kneeling down]
c. and saying to Him that
d. if You want
e. You can heal me

###### v41
a. then He having felt compassion
b. stretching out His hand He touched (him)
c. and He says to him
d. I want to
e. be healed

###### v42
a. and immediately the leprosy went away from him
b. and he was healed

###### v43
a. then having spoken sternly to him
b. He immediately sent him away

###### v44
a. and He says to him
b. pay attention
c. you should say nothing to no one
d. but leave
e. show yourself to the priest
f. and present (the offerings) for your purification
g. what Moses commanded
h. for (a) witness to them

###### v45
a. then he having departed
b. he began to tell many things
c. and to spread the news
d. so that He was no longer able
e. to openly enter into (the) town
f. but He was outside in lonely places
g. and they came to Him from everywhere
