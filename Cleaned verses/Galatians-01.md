###### v1
a. Paul (an) apostle
b. not from people
c. and not by people
d. but by Jesus Christ
e. and God (the) Father
f. the One having raised Him from (the) dead

###### v2
a. and all the fellow believers with me
b. to the churches of Galatia

###### v3
a. grace to you and peace
b. from God our Father
c. and (the) Lord Jesus Christ

###### v4
a. the One having given Himself for our sins
b. so that He might take us out from the present evil world
c. according to the will of God (who is) also our Father

###### v5
a. to Whom (be) the glory forever and ever amen

###### v6
a. I am amazed because you so quickly turn away from
b. the One having called you by (the) grace [of Christ]
c. to (a) different gospel

###### v7
a. that is not another one
b. except that there are some upsetting you
c. indeed wanting to change the gospel of Christ

###### v8
a. but even if we
b. or (an) angel from heaven
c. should bring (a) gospel [to you]
d. contrary to what we preached to you
e. let him be cursed

###### v9
a. as I have said before
b. also now I say again
c. if someone brings you (a) gospel
d. contrary to what you received
e. let him be cursed

###### v10
a. and do I now persuade people or God?
b. or do I seek to please people?
c. if nevertheless I pleased people
d. I would not have been (a) servant of Christ

###### v11
a. indeed I make known to you fellow believers
b. the gospel preached by me
c. that it is not according to (a) human being

###### v12
a. because I received it also not from (a) human being
b. and I was not taught (it)
c. but (it is) by revelation of Jesus Christ

###### v13
a. now you heard about my conduct once in Judaism that
b. I persecuted the church of God to (the) extreme
c. indeed I attacked to destroy it

###### v14
a. and I advanced in Judaism
b. beyond many contemporaries among my nation
c. being (an) extreme enthusiast of my ancestral traditions

###### v15
a. but when [God] preferred better
b. the One having set me apart from my mother's womb
c. and having called (me) by His grace

###### v16
a. to reveal His Son to me
b. so that I should preach Him among the Gentiles
c. right away I did not ask advice of (any) person

###### v17
a. and I did not go up to Jerusalem to those (who were) before me  
b. but I went away to Arabia
c. and then again I returned to Damascus

###### v18
a. later after three years I went up to Jerusalem
b. to visit Cephas and get information
c. and I stayed with him for fifteen days

###### v19
a. and I did not see another one of the apostles
b. except James the brother of the Lord

###### v20
a. and what I write to you
b. look (it is) before God that
c. I do not lie

###### v21
a. afterwards I went to the regions of Syria and of Cilicia

###### v22
a. and I was not known in person
b. by the churches of Judea that (are) in Christ

###### v23
a. but they were only receiving news that
b. the one persecuting us formerly
c. now preaches the faith
d. that he formerly attacked to destroy

###### v24
a. and they glorified God because of me
