###### v1
a. if I speak in the languages of human beings and even of angels
b. yet I have not love
c. I have become (a) brass gong or (a) clanging cymbal

###### v2
a. and if I have (the) ability to prophesy
b. and I would know all the mysteries
c. and all the knowledge
d. and if I have all the faith
e. so as to remove mountains
f. but I do not have love
g. I am nothing

###### v3
a. even if I shall give away all my possessions
b. and if I would give over my body so that I may boast
c. but I do not have love
d. I accomplish nothing

###### v4
a. love is patient
b. love acts kindly
c. it is not jealous
d. [love] does not boast
e. it is not proud

###### v5
a. it does not behave indecently
b. it does not seek the things of itself
c. it is not upset
d. it does not keep record of the evil

###### v6
a. it does not rejoice in the unjust deed
b. but it rejoices with the truth

###### v7
a. it endures in all (circumstances)
b. it believes in all (circumstances)
c. it hopes in all (circumstances)
d. it bears up in all (circumstances)

###### v8
a. love never fails
b. now if (it is) prophecies
c. they will cease
d. if ecstatic languages
e. they will stop
f. if knowledge
g. it will be put an end to

###### v9
a. because we know partially
b. and we prophesy partially

###### v10
a. but when the perfect comes
b. the partial will be put an end to

###### v11
a. when I was (a) small child
b. I spoke like (a) small child
c. I thought like (a) small child
d. I reasoned like (a) small child
e. when I have become (a) man
f. I have put an end to the things of the small child

###### v12
a. in fact we look presently through (a) mirror into (a) riddle
b. but then face to face
c. right now I know in part
d. but then I shall understand just as also I was understood

###### v13
a. yet now these three remains faith hope love
b. now (the) greater of these (is) love
