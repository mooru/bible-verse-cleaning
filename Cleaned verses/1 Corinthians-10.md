###### v1
a. now fellow believers I do not want you to be ignorant that
b. our ancestors were all under the cloud
c. and they all crossed over through the sea

###### v2
a. and all were baptised in Moses by means of the cloud and by means of the sea

###### v3
a. and they all ate the same supernatural food

###### v4
a. and they all drank the same supernatural drink
b. because they drank from (the) supernatural rock following (them)
c. now the rock was Christ

###### v5
a. but God was not pleased with the majority of them
b. because they were killed in the desert

###### v6
a. now these ones became examples for us
b. so that we (should) not be greatly desiring bad things
c. just as also they desired greatly

###### v7
a. and do not become idolaters just as some of them
b. just as it has been written
c. the people sat down to eat and to drink
d. and they stood up to play

###### v8
a. and do not commit sexual immorality
b. just as some of them committed sexual immorality
c. and on one day twenty three thousand died

###### v9
a. and let us not put Christ to the test
b. just as some of them attempted
c. and they were destroyed by snakes

###### v10
a. and do not complain
b. just as some of them complained
c. and they were destroyed by the destroyer

###### v11
a. now these things happened to them as examples
b. and it was written as (a) warning for us
c. to whom the end of the ages is about to come

###### v12
a. so that the one supposing to remain firmly
b. let him beware (that) he does not fail

###### v13
a. temptation has not taken hold of you except (a) human (one)
b. but God (is) faithful
c. Who will not allow you to be tempted
d. beyond what you are able
e. but He will with the temptation also bring about the means of escape
f. so as to be able to have endured

###### v14
a. therefore my beloved ones
b. flee from idolatry

###### v15
a. I speak as to wise ones
b. you judge what I say

###### v16
a. the cup of blessing that we bless
b. is it not indeed fellowship with the blood of Christ?
c. the bread that we break
d. is it not indeed fellowship with the body (of) Christ?

###### v17
a. in fact (there is) one loaf of bread
b. we the many are one body
c. because we all share from the one loaf of bread

###### v18
a. consider Israel according to (their) physical state
b. are those eating the sacrifices not partners of the altar?

###### v19
a. what then do I say?
b. that sacrificial meat is something important
c. or that (an) idol is something important?

###### v20
a. on the contrary that which they sacrifice (is) for demons
b. indeed not for God [they sacrifice]
c. further I do not want you to be partners of the demons

###### v21
a. you can not drink (the) cup of (the) Lord and (the) cup of demons
b. you can not share in the table of (the) Lord and (the) table of demons

###### v22
a. or do we make the Lord jealous?
b. we are surely not stronger than He?

###### v23
a. everything is allowed
b. but not all things are advantageous
c. everything is allowed
d. but not all things build up

###### v24
a. let no one seek his own but that of the other

###### v25
a. eat everything being sold in (the) meat market
b. carefully judging nothing for the sake of the conscience

###### v26
a. because the earth (is) of the Lord as well as its contents

###### v27
a. if one of the non-Christians invites you
b. and you wish to go
c. eat everything being given to you
d. evaluating nothing carefully for the sake of the conscience

###### v28
a. but if someone would say to you
b. this is sacrificed to a deity
c. do not eat for the sake of that one having informed (you) as well as the conscience

###### v29
a. now I mean conscience indeed not that of yourself but the one of the other (person)
b. why then is my freedom judged by (the) conscience of another?

###### v30
a. if I eat with thanks
b. why am I defamed because of what I give thanks for?

###### v31
a. then whether I eat or I drink or I do something
b. I do all for (the) glory of God

###### v32
a. behave without causing offence to indeed Jews and Greeks and the church of God

###### v33
a. just as also I please all in everything
b. not seeking the benefit of myself but that of the majority
c. so that they may be saved

###### v34
a. be my imitators just as also I of Christ
