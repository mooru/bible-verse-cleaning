###### v1
a. now Jesus having been born in Bethlehem of Judea
b. in (the) days of Herod the king
c. look
d. magi from (the) east appeared in Jerusalem

###### v2
a. saying
b. where is the one born King of the Jews?
c. because we saw His star in the east
d. and we came to worship Him

###### v3
a. now having heard (this) king Herod was distressed
b. and all Jerusalem with him

###### v4
a. and having gathered all the chief priests and Law experts of the nation
b. he inquired from them
c. where the Messiah is to be born

###### v5
a. and they said to him
b. in Bethlehem of Judea
c. because thus it is written by the prophet

###### v6
a. indeed you Bethlehem land of Judah
b. by no means are you (the) least of the rulers of Judah
c. because from you will go out (a) Leader
d. Who will guide and help My people Israel

###### v7
a. then Herod having secretly called the magi
b. he exactly found out from them the time of the appearing of (the) star

###### v8
a. and having sent them to Bethlehem he said
b. having left try to find out accurately about the Child
c. and when you have found (Him)
d. inform me
e. so that also I having gone may worship Him

###### v9
a. and they having heard the king they departed
b. and look
c. the star that they saw in the east
d. went ahead of them
e. until having come it stopped above where the Child was

###### v10
a. and having seen the star
b. they rejoiced (with) very much joy

###### v11
a. and having come into the house
b. they saw the Child with Mary His mother
c. then falling down they worshipped Him
d. and having opened their treasure boxes
e. they presented gifts to Him
f. gold and frankincense and myrrh

###### v12
a. and having been revealed in (a) dream
b. not to return to Herod
c. they went away by another way to their country

###### v13
a. now they having gone away
b. look
c. (an) angel of (the) Lord appears to Joseph in (a) dream
d. saying
e. having got up
f. take along the Child and His mother
g. and flee to Egypt
h. and be there until I would tell you
i. because Herod is about to try to find the Child
j. to destroy Him

###### v14
a. then he having got up
b. he took along the Child and His mother by night
c. and he went away to Egypt

###### v15
a. and he was there until the death of Herod
b. so that what was said by (the) Lord would come true
c. speaking through the prophet
d. out of Egypt I called My Son

###### v16
a. then Herod having realised that
b. he was tricked by the magi
c. he was really extremely angry
d. and having sent (men)
e. he killed all the boys
f. those in Bethlehem
g. and (those) in all its regions
h. from two years and less
i. according to the time that he learned exactly from the magi

###### v17
a. then was fulfilled what was spoken
b. by Jeremiah the prophet saying

###### v18
a. (a) cry was heard in Ramah
b. weeping and loud lamenting
c. Rachel weeping (for) her children
d. and she wanted not to be consoled
e. because they are not

###### v19
a. now Herod having died
b. look
c. (an) angel of (the) Lord appears in (a) dream to Joseph in Egypt

###### v20
a. saying
b. having got up
c. take along the Child and His mother
d. and go to (the) land (of) Israel
e. because those seeking the life of the Child have died

###### v21
a. and he having got up
b. took along the Child and His mother
c. and entered into (the) land (of) Israel

###### v22
a. and having heard that
b. Archelaus is king of Judea
c. instead of his father Herod
d. he was afraid to go back there
e. but divinely directed in (a) dream
f. he went away to the regions of Galilee

###### v23
a. and having arrived he resided in (a) town called Nazareth
b. so that what was said by the prophets came true that
c. He will be called (a) Nazarene
