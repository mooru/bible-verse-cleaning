###### v1
a. now having seen the crowds
b. He went up on the mountain
c. and He having sat down
d. His disciples came to Him

###### v2
a. and He having begun to speak
b. He taught them
c. saying

###### v3
a. happy (are) the humble
b. for to them belongs the Kingdom of Heaven

###### v4
a. happy (are) those mourning
b. for they will be encouraged

###### v5
a. happy (are) the gentle
b. for they will receive the earth

###### v6
a. happy (are) those longing and yearning to do what God requires
b. for they will be satisfied

###### v7
a. happy (are) the merciful
b. for they will receive mercy

###### v8
a. happy (are) those pure in mind
b. for they will see God

###### v9
a. happy (are) the peacemakers
b. for they will be called children of God

###### v10
a. happy (are) those persecuted for doing what God requires
b. for to them belongs the Kingdom of Heaven

###### v11
a. happy are you when they insult you
b. and persecute (you)
c. and say any wicked thing against you [lying] on account of Me

###### v12
a. rejoice and be very glad
b. for your reward (is) great in heaven
c. for likewise they persecuted the prophets before you

###### v13
a. you are the salt for all mankind
b. but if the salt becomes tasteless
c. with what will it be salted?
d. for nothing it is capable anymore
e. except for being thrown away
f. to be trampled on by the people

###### v14
a. you are the light of the people of the world
b. (a) city can not be hidden
c. being on (a) mountain

###### v15
a. and they light not (a) lamp
b. and then they place it under (a) box
c. but on the lampstand
d. and it shines for all those in the house

###### v16
a. likewise let your light shine before the people
b. so that they may see your good deeds
c. and then they may glorify your Father in heaven

###### v17
a. think not that
b. I came to do away with the Law or the Prophets
c. I came not to do away with
d. but to give true meaning to (them)

###### v18
a. because truly I say to you
b. until the heaven and the earth would pass away
c. not one letter or one mark will ever pass away from the Law
d. until all would happen

###### v19
a. who ever then do away with one of the least of these commandments
b. and should thus teach the people
c. will be called (the) least in the Kingdom of Heaven
d. but who ever carries (them) out
e. and should teach (them)
f. this one will be called great in the Kingdom of Heaven

###### v20
a. because I say to you that
b. if not your righteousness abounds more than (that) of the Law experts and Pharisees
c. you will never enter into the Kingdom of Heaven

###### v21
a. you heard that
b. it was said to the people of old
c. you shall not murder
d. and who ever murders
e. will be liable to condemnation

###### v22
a. but I say to you that
b. everyone being angry with his fellow being
c. will be liable to condemnation
d. and who ever may say to his fellow being numskull
e. will be liable to the Sanhedrin
f. and who ever may say fool
g. will be liable to the hell of fire

###### v23
a. when then you bring your gift to the altar
b. and there you remember that
c. your fellow being has something against you

###### v24
a. leave your gift there in front of the altar
b. and go off first make peace with your fellow being
c. and then having come
d. present your gift

###### v25
a. be settling with your accuser quickly
b. while you are with him on the road
c. so that the accuser may not hand you over to the judge
d. and the judge to the servant
e. and you be thrown into jail

###### v26
a. truly I say to you
b. you will never come out from there
c. until you would have paid the last penny

###### v27
a. you heard that
b. it was said
c. you shall not commit adultery

###### v28
a. but I say to you that
b. everyone looking at (a) woman in order to lust for her
c. already commited adultery with her in his mind

###### v29
a. and if your right eye causes you to sin
b. take it out
c. and throw (it) away from you
d. for it is advantageous for you that
e. one of your body parts be destroyed
f. and not your whole body be thrown into hell

###### v30
a. and if your right hand causes you to sin
b. cut it off
c. and throw (it) away from you
d. for it is advantageous to you that
e. one of your body parts be destroyed
f. and not your whole body go away into hell

###### v31
a. further it was said
b. who ever divorces his wife
c. let him give to her (a) notice of divorce

###### v32
a. but I say to you that
b. everyone divorcing his wife
c. except for (the) reason of sexual immorality
d. causes her to commit adultery
e. and who ever marries (the) divorced woman
f. commits adultery

###### v33
a. also you heard that
b. it was said to the people of old
c. you shall not break an oath
d. but you shall pay back your oaths to the Lord

###### v34
a. but I tell you not to swear at all
b. and (swear) not by the heaven
c. because it is (the) throne of God

###### v35
a. and (swear) not by the earth
b. because it is (the) footstool of His feet
c. and (swear) not by Jerusalem
d. because it is (the) city of the great King

###### v36
a. and swear not by your head
b. because you are not able to make one hair white or black

###### v37
a. but let your word be yes yes no no
b. and what (is) beyond this is from the evil one

###### v38
a. you heard that
b. it was said
c. eye for (an) eye and tooth for (a) tooth

###### v39
a. but I tell you not to resist the evil person
b. and whoever slaps you on the right cheek [of you]
c. turn to him also the other

###### v40
a. and to the one wanting for you to stand trial
b. and to take your shirt
c. allow him also (to take) your coat

###### v41
a. and whoever forces you to carry a load one mile
b. go along with him two (miles)

###### v42
a. to the one asking you
b. give
c. and the one wanting to borrow money from you
d. refuse not to help

###### v43
a. you heard that
b. it was said
c. you should love your neighbour
d. and you should hate your enemy

###### v44
a. but I say to you
b. love your enemies
c. and pray for those persecuting you

###### v45
a. so that you may be children of your Father in heaven
b. because His sun comes up upon bad and good people
c. and He sends rain upon righteous and unrighteous people

###### v46
a. for if you love those loving you
b. what reward have you?
c. do not even the tax collectors do the same?

###### v47
a. and if you greet only your fellow countrymen
b. what exceptional are you doing?
c. do not even the heathen do the same?

###### v48
a. you then should be perfect
b. as your heavenly Father is perfect
