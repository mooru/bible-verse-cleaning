###### v1
a. now on the first (day) of the week Mary Magdalene comes
b. early morning while still being dark to the tomb
c. and she sees the stone removed from the tomb

###### v2
a. then she runs
b. and she comes to Simon Peter
c. and to the other disciple whom Jesus loved
d. and she says to them
e. they took the Lord out of the tomb
f. and we do not know where they put Him

###### v3
a. then Peter and the other disciple went out
b. and they went to the tomb

###### v4
a. and the two ran together
b. then the other disciple ran ahead faster than Peter
c. and he came first to the tomb

###### v5
a. and having bent over he sees the linnen cloths lying
b. nevertheless he did not enter

###### v6
a. then also comes Simon Peter following him
b. and he went in into the tomb
c. and he sees the linnen cloths lying

###### v7
a. and also the face cloth
b. that was upon His head
c. not lying with the linnen cloths
d. but separately having been rolled up at one place

###### v8
a. then indeed also the other disciple went in
b. the one having arrived first at the tomb
c. and he saw and believed

###### v9
a. for not yet they understood the Scripture that
b. He must come back  to life from (the) dead

###### v10
a. then again the disciples went back home

###### v11
a. but Mary had taken stand outside at the tomb weeping
b. but while she was weeping
c. she bent over looking into the tomb

###### v12
a. and she sees two angels in radiant (clothes) sitting
b. one at the head and one at the feet
c. where the body of Jesus lay

###### v13
a. and they say to her
b. madam why do you weep?
c. she says to them
d. because they took away my Lord
e. and I do not know where they laid Him

###### v14
a. having said this she turned to the behind
b. and she observes Jesus standing
c. yet she had known not that
d. it is Jesus

###### v15
a. Jesus says to her
b. madam why do you weep?
c. who are you looking for?
d. she thinking that
e. it is the gardener
f. she says to Him
g. sir if you carried Him away
h. tell me where you laid Him
i. and I will take Him

###### v16
a. Jesus says to her
b. Mary
c. having turned she says to Him in the Hebrew language
d. Rabbouni  [which means Teacher]

###### v17
a. Jesus says to her
b. do not hold on to Me
c. for not yet have I ascended to the Father
d. but go to My brothers
e. and say to them
f. I am ascending to My Father
g. and your Father
h. and My God
i. and your God

###### v18
a. Mary Magdalene goes telling the disciples that
b. I have seen the Lord
c. and He said these things to her

###### v19
a. being now evening on that first day of (the) week
b. and the doors having been closed where the disciples were
c. because of fear of the Jews
d. Jesus came and stood in their midst
e. and He says to them
f. peace to you

###### v20
a. then having said this
b. He showed them His hands and His side
c. the disciples then rejoiced having seen the Lord

###### v21
a. then He [Jesus] said to them again
b. peace to you
c. just as the Father has sent Me
d. also I send you

###### v22
a. then having said this
b. He breathed on (them)
c. and He says to them
d. receive (the) Holy Spirit

###### v23
a. whenever you would forgive someone's sins
b. they are forgiven to them
c. whenever you keep someone's
d. they have been kept

###### v24
a. now Thomas one of the twelve
b. the one called Didymus
c. was not with them
d. when Jesus came

###### v25
a. so the other disciples said to him
b. we have seen the Lord
c. but he said to them
d. unless I would see in His hands the scar of the nails
e. and I would put my finger into the scar of the nails
f. and I would put my hand into His side
g. I will by no means believe

###### v26
a. and then eight days later His disciples again were inside
b. and also Thomas with them
c. Jesus comes
d. the doors having been locked
e. and He stood in their midst
f. and He said
g. peace to you

###### v27
a. then He says to Thomas
b. put your finger here
c. and look at My hands
d. and bring your hand
e. and put (it) into My side
f. and do not be unbelieving
g. but believing

###### v28
a. Thomas replied and said to Him
b. my Lord and my God

###### v29
a. Jesus says to him
b. have you believed because you have seen Me?
c. blessed (are) those not having seen yet having believed

###### v30
a. many other signs indeed then also Jesus did before the disciples [of His]
b. that is not been written in this book

###### v31
a. but these have been written
b. so that you may believe that
c. Jesus is the Messiah
d. the Son of God
e. and so that believing
f. you may have life in His Name
