###### v1
a. Simeon Peter (a) slave and apostle of Jesus Christ
b. to those having received (a) faith equal to ours
c. by means of (the) justification of our God and Saviour Jesus Christ

###### v2
a. may grace to you and peace be increased greatly
b. through knowledge of God and of Jesus our Lord

###### v3
a. how great (is) everything (given) to us from His divine power
b. the things having been given for life and piety
c. through the knowledge of the One having called us
d. by His own amazing might and powerful deed

###### v4
a. by which the precious and very important promises were given to us
b. so that by these things you may become partners of divine nature
c. having escaped the corruption in the world due to evil desires

###### v5
a. and for this very (reason) now having done your best
b. add virtue to your faith
c. and to virtue (add) knowledge

###### v6
a. and to knowledge (add) self-control
b. and to self-control (add) endurance
c. and to endurance (add) godliness

###### v7
a. and to godliness (add) love for fellow believers
b. and to love for fellow believers (add) loving concern

###### v8
a. indeed these things being with you
b. and having in abundance
c. it causes (you) not to be useless and not unproductive
d. with respect to the knowledge of our Lord Jesus Christ

###### v9
a. because to whom these things are not present
b. he is blind
c. being short-sighted
d. having experienced forgetting of the cleansing of his long ago sins

###### v10
a. therefore more fellow believers do your best
b. to make your calling and choosing certain
c. because doing this
d. you will by no means ever sin

###### v11
a. because in this way there will be richly provided for you the arrival
b. into the eternal kingdom of our Lord and Saviour Jesus   

###### v12
a. therefore I must always remind you about these things
b. although (I remind the) ones knowing
c. indeed those established in the present truth

###### v13
a. further I regard (it) proper
b. for as long as I am in this body
c. to stir you up by reminding (you)

###### v14
a. knowing that the time of my death is very soon
b. just as also our Lord Jesus Christ made (it) known to me

###### v15
a. and also I will always do my best
b. that you be able after my death
c. to do remembrance of these things

###### v16
a. because not having imitated cleverly contrived myths
b. we made known to you the power and presence of our Lord Jesus  
c. certainly having become eyewitnessess of His great power

###### v17
a. yes having received from God (the) Father honour and glory
b. (a) special voice having been experienced by Him
c. from the Majestic Glory
d. this One is My Son My Beloved
e. in Whom I took delight

###### v18
a. indeed we heard this voice
b. brought from heaven
c. being with Him on the holy mountain

###### v19
a. further we have (as) more trustworthy the prophetic message
b. to which you do well
c. paying attention like to (a) lamp shining in (a) gloomy place
d. until (the) day dawns
e. and (the) morning star rises in your inner selves

###### v20
a. knowing this first that
b. every prophecy of Scripture is not of own explanation

###### v21
a. for no prophecy was ever brought about by (the) will of (a) person
b. but being guided by (the) Holy Spirit
c. people spoke from God
