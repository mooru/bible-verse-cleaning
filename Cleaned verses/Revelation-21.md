###### v1
a. then I saw (a) new heaven and (a) new earth
b. because the first heaven and the first earth passed away
c. also the sea is not anymore

###### v2
a. then I saw the holy city (the) new Jerusalem
b. coming down out of heaven from God
c. having been made ready like (a) bride beautified for her husband

###### v3
a. also I heard (a) loud voice from the throne saying
b. look
c. the tabernacle tent of God (is) with the people
d. and He will take up residence with them
e. and they themselves will be His people
f. indeed God Himself will be with them [as their God]

###### v4
a. and He will wipe away every tear from their eyes
b. and death will not be anymore
c. neither mourning nor weeping nor pain will not be anymore
d. [because] the first things passed away

###### v5
a. then the One sitting upon the throne said
b. pay attention
c. I make all things new
d. also he says
e. write
f. because these words are trustworthy and true

###### v6
a. then He said to me
b. (the things) have happened
c. I [am] the alpha and the omega
d. the first and the last
e. I will give to the one thirsting
f. from the spring of the water of life without cost

###### v7
a. the one conquering will inherit these things
b. surely I will be God to him
c. yes he himself will be (a) son to Me

###### v8
a. but to the cowards and unbelievers and abominables and murderers
b. and sexually immoral persons and sorcerers
c. and worshippers of idols and all the liars
d. their part (will be) in the lake the one burning with fire and sulphur
e. that is the second death

###### v9
a. then came one of the seven angels
b. the ones having the seven bowls
c. the ones being full of the seven last plagues
d. and he spoke with me saying
e. come here
f. I will show to you the bride the wife of the Lamb

###### v10
a. then he took me away in spirit onto (a) large and high mountain
b. and he showed me the city the holy Jerusalem
c. coming down out of heaven from God

###### v11
a. having the glory of God
b. the radiance of it like (a) very precious stone
c. just like (a) jasper stone shining like crystal

###### v12
a. having (a) wall big and high
b. having twelve gates
c. and at the gates twelve angels
d. further names having been written on
e. that are [the names] of the twelve tribes of the people of Israel

###### v13
a. from (the) east (there were) three gates
b. and from (the) north three gates
c. and from (the) south three gates
d. and from (the) west three gates

###### v14
a. and the wall of the city having twelve foundations
b. and upon them twelve names
c. of the twelve apostles of the Lamb

###### v15
a. then the one speaking with me
b. had as measure (a) golden measuring rod
c. so that he could measure the city
d. as well as the gates of it and the wall of it

###### v16
a. further the city is set square
b. indeed its length as long as [also] its breadth
c. then he measured the city with the measuring rod
d. to the extent of twelve thousand stade
e. the length and the breadth and the height of it is the same

###### v17
a. then he measured its wall one hundred forty four cubits
b. (the) measure of (a) man that is of (an) angel

###### v18
a. and the foundation of the wall of it (was) jasper
b. yet the city (was) pure gold
c. just like pure crystal

###### v19
a. the foundations of the wall of the city
b. were adorned with every kind of precious stone
c. the foundation stone (was)
d. the first jasper
e. the second sapphire
f. the third agate
g. the fourth emerald

###### v20
a. the fifth sardonyx
b. the sixth carnelian
c. the seventh chrysolite
d. the eighth beryl
e. the ninth topaz
f. the tenth chrysoprase
g. the eleventh hyacinth
h. the twelfth amethyst

###### v21
a. further the twelve gates (were) twelve pearls
b. each one each of the gates
c. was made of one pearl
d. and the wide street of the city (was) pure gold
e. transparent like glass

###### v22
a. yet (a) temple I did not see in it
b. because the Lord God the Almighty is its temple and also the Lamb

###### v23
a. further the city has no need of the sun
b. also not of the moon
c. that they would shine on it
d. because the glory of God shone upon it
e. and its lamp (is) the Lamb

###### v24
a. indeed the nations will live by the light of it
b. further the kings of the earth bring their glory into her

###### v25
a. and her gates would not at all be closed during the day
b. because night will not be there

###### v26
a. further they will bring the glory and the honour of the nations into her

###### v27
a. and never ever would come into her any defiled thing
b. yes [the one] doing what is detestable and (a) lie
c. except the ones having been written in the book of life of the Lamb
