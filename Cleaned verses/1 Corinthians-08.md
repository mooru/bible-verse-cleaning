###### v1
a. now about the sacrificial meat
b. we know that
c. we all have knowledge
d. knowledge makes proud
e. but love builds up

###### v2
a. if someone thinks to have known something
b. he understood not yet how he should know

###### v3
a. but if someone loves God
b. this one has been known by Him

###### v4
a. about the meat in fact the sacrificial meat
b. we know that
c. (there is) no idol in (the) world
d. and that
e. no one (is) God except One

###### v5
a. for also if indeed what is called gods exist
b. whether in heaven or on earth
c. just as there are many gods and many lords

###### v6
a. but to us (there is) one God
b. the Father from Whom all things and we for the purpose of Him
c. and one Lord Jesus Christ
d. through Whom all things and we through Him

###### v7
a. but not in all (is) the knowledge
b. but until now some by the custom of the idol
c. eat (it) as sacrificial meat
d. and their conscience being weak is defiled

###### v8
a. but food will not show us to be true to God
b. neither if we would not eat do we fail to attain
c. nor will we have a greater benefit if we would eat

###### v9
a. but beware so that not this freedom of yours
b. may become (a) stumbling to the morally weak

###### v10
a. for if someone would see you the one having knowledge
b. reclining to eat in (a) temple of an idol
c. will he not his conscience being weak
d. be strengthened in order to eat the sacrificial meat?

###### v11
a. indeed the one being weak is destroyed by your knowledge
b. your fellow believer for the sake of whom Christ died

###### v12
a. and in this way sinning against your fellow believers
b. and so harming their conscience being weak
c. you sin against Christ

###### v13
a. therefore if food causes my fellow believer to sin
b. I would by no means forever eat meat
c. so that I will not cause my fellow believer to sin
