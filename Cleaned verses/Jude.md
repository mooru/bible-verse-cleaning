###### v1
a. Jude
b. (a) slave of Jesus Christ
c. and brother of James
d. to those loved by God (the) Father
e. and to those kept by Jesus Christ
f. to (those) called

###### v2
a. may mercy to you and peace and love be increased

###### v3
a. beloved
b. doing all eagerness to write to you
c. because of our mutual salvation
d. I had (an) obligation to write to you
e. urging (you) to struggle
f. for the faith once for all entrusted to God's people

###### v4
a. because some people sneaked in
b. those written about long ago concerning this condemnation
c. ungodly people
d. turning the kindness of our God into extreme immorality
e. and refusing to obey our only Master and Lord Jesus Christ

###### v5
a. I further want to remind you
b. though you know (it) all that
c. the Lord once having delivered His people from Egypt
d. afterwards destroyed those not having believed

###### v6
a. and angels not having retained their own sphere of rule
b. but deserted their own dwelling place
c. He has kept for judgment of (the) great day with everlasting chains  

###### v7
a. just like Sodom and Gomorrah and the towns around them
b. in the same way as those engaged in sexual immorality
c. indeed having homosexual intercourse
d. they are clearly (an) example of suffering punishment of eternal fire

###### v8
a. in the same way then also these people
b. dreaming they indeed defile (their) bodies
c. and they regard as invalid supernatural authority
d. and slander glorious beings

###### v9
a. now when Michael the archangel was debating with the devil
b. he argued about the body of Moses
c. he did not dare to bring (a) judgment of slander
d. but he said may (the) Lord rebuke you

###### v10
a. but these people whatever then they do not understand
b. they slander
c. and whatever they understand naturally like senseless animals
d. by these things they are destroyed

###### v11
a. horror to them because they have behaved the way of Cain
b. and they devoted themselves to the deception for reward of Balaam
c. and by rebellion like Korah they perished

###### v12
a. these people are hidden dangers in your fellowship meals
b. feasting together without reverence for God
c. being their own rule
d. waterless clouds driven along by winds
e. trees in late autumn without fruit completely dead uprooted

###### v13
a. stormy waves of (the) sea causing their shameful deeds to foam up
b. wandering stars for whom the darkness of hell has been kept forever

###### v14
a. and also against them prophesied Enoch (the) seventh after Adam  
b. look (the) Lord came with His ten thousands (of) holy beings to bring     

###### v15
a. and to rebuke all people for all their godless deeds that they did     
b. and for all the harsh things that they spoke against Him (as) ungodly   

###### v16
a. these people are grumblers
b. fault finders
c. living according to their own lusts
d. and their mouth speaks boastful things
e. flattering for gain

###### v17
a. but you beloved
b. remember the words spoken long ago
c. by the apostles of our Lord Jesus Christ

###### v18
a. that they said to you
b. that at (the) end of time there will be mockers
c. living according to the own lusts of the ungodly

###### v19
a. they are those causing divisions
b. wordly people not having (the) Spirit

###### v20
a. but you beloved
b. building yourselves up in your most holy faith
c. praying in (the) Holy Spirit

###### v21
a. keep yourselves in (the) love of God
b. waiting for the mercy of our Lord Jesus Christ
c. leading to eternal life

###### v22
a. and indeed show mercy to those who doubt

###### v23
a. and save others snatching (them) from fire
b. and show others mercy with fear
c. hating even their clothing stained by their sinful nature

###### v24
a. now to Him being able to keep you free from sinning
b. and to let (you) stand before His glory without blame in great joy

###### v25
a. to (the) only God our Saviour because of Jesus Christ our Lord
b. glory prominence power and authority
c. before all time
d. and now
e. and forever
f. amen
