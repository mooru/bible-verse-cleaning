###### v1
a. now I fellow believers was not able to speak to you
b. like to spiritual ones
c. but like to worldy ones
d. like to small children with regard to Christ

###### v2
a. I gave you milk to drink not food
b. because you were not yet able
c. but not even still now you are able

###### v3
a. because you are still worldly
b. because since (there is) among you jealousy and strife
c. are you not indeed worldly
d. and do you (not indeed) behave just like people?

###### v4
a. because when someone would say
b. I indeed belong to Paul
c. and another I (belong to) Apollos
d. are you not human beings?

###### v5
a. what then is Apollos?
b. and what is Paul?
c. servants through whom you believed
d. and to each as the Lord  appointed

###### v6
a. I planted
b. Apollos irrigated
c. but God caused to increase

###### v7
a. so that neither the one planting is someone important
b. nor the one irrigating
c. but God (is) the One causing increase

###### v8
a. now the one planting and the one irrigating are one
b. yet  each will receive his own reward
c. according to his own labour

###### v9
a. because we are fellow workers of God
b. (the) field of God (you are)
c. (the) building of God you are

###### v10
a. according to the grace of God given to me
b. as (a) skillful master builder I laid (a) foundation
c. and another builds upon (it)
d. but let each beware how he builds upon (it)

###### v11
a. because no one can lay (a) different foundation
b. instead of the existing one
c. which is Jesus Christ

###### v12
a. now if someone builds upon the foundation
b. gold silver precious stones wood grass straw

###### v13
a. the work of each will become evident
b. because the day will make (it) known
c. because it will be revealed by fire
d. and the work of each what sort it is
e. the fire [self] will test

###### v14
a. if the work of someone remains
b. that he has built upon (the foundation)
c. he will receive reward

###### v15
a. if the work of someone will be burned down
b. he will suffer loss
c. but he himself will be saved
d. yet thus as through fire

###### v16
a. do you not understand that
b. you are (a) temple of God
c. and the Spirit of God lives in you?

###### v17
a. if someone destroys the temple of God
b. God will destroy him
c. because the temple of God is holy
d. which is you

###### v18
a. let no one deceive himself
b. if someone thinks to be wise among you by this world's standards
c. let him become foolish
d. so that he may become wise

###### v19
a. because the wisdom of this world is foolishness before God
b. because it has been written
c. the One trapping the wise people in their treachery

###### v20
a. and again
b. (the) Lord knows the reasonings of the wise people that
c. they are futile

###### v21
a. so that no one should boast among people
b. because all things are yours

###### v22
a. whether Paul or Apollos or Cephas
b. whether world or life or death
c. whether present or about to be
d. all things (are) yours

###### v23
a. but you (are) of Christ and Christ (is) of God
