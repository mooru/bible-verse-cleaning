###### v1
a. then I saw (a) beast coming up out of the sea
b. having ten horns and seven heads
c. and on his horns ten diadem crowns
d. and on his heads names of blasphemy

###### v2
a. and the beast that I saw
b. was like (a) leopard
c. and his feet like (those) of (a) bear
d. and his mouth like (the) mouth of (a) lion
e. and to it the dragon gave its power
f. also its throne and great power

###### v3
a. and one of his heads (was) like one slaughtered to death
b. then the wound of his death was healed
c. and all the earth was amazed after the beast

###### v4
a. indeed they worshipped the dragon
b. because it gave its power to the beast
c. then they worshipped the beast saying
d. who (is) like the beast
e. and who is able to wage war against him?

###### v5
a. then (a) mouth was given to him
b. speaking surprising things and blasphemies
c. also it was given to him
d. to execute authority for forty [and] two months

###### v6
a. then he opened his mouth for blasphemies against God
b. to blaspheme His Name and His tabernacle tent
c. those coming to dwell in heaven

###### v7
a. also it was given to him to make war against God's people
b. indeed to conquer them
c. yes authority was given to him
d. over every tribe and nation and language and people

###### v8
a. and they will worship him
b. all those living upon the earth
c. of whom the name of that one has not been written
d. in the book of life of the Lamb the One having been slaughtered
e. since (the) creation of (the) world

###### v9
a. if someone is able to hear
b. let him pay attention

###### v10
a. if someone (is) for the purpose of captivity
b. into captivity he goes
c. if someone is to be killed by (a) sword
d. he is to be killed by (a) sword
e. in this case is the endurance and the faith of God's people

###### v11
a. then I saw another beast coming up out of the earth
b. and it had two horns like (a) lamb
c. yet he was speaking like (a) dragon

###### v12
a. now by the authority of the first beast
b. he performs all in front of him
c. and he brings upon the earth
d. and those living in it
e. so that they will worship the first beast
f. of whom was healed the wound of his death

###### v13
a. then he performs great signs
b. in fact he even would make fire
c. to come down from the sky to the earth in front of the people

###### v14
a. indeed he deceives those living upon the earth
b. by means of the signs that were given to him
c. to do in front of the beast
d. telling those living upon the earth
e. to make (an) image for the beast
f. who has the wound of the sword
g. yet he lived

###### v15
a. further it was given to him
b. to give  breath to the image of the beast
c. so that also the image of the beast may speak
d. and may bring about [that]
e. all who if they do not worship the image of the beast
f. would be killed

###### v16
a. then he causes all
b. the unimportant ones and the important ones
c. also the rich ones and the poor ones
d. as well as the free persons and the slaves
e. that they would give to them (a) mark
f. on their right hand
g. or on their forehead

###### v17
a. surely so that not anyone may be able to buy or to sell
b. except the one having the mark
c. (namely) the name of the beast or the number of his name

###### v18
a. in this case wisdom exists
b. the one having (a) mind
c. let him figure out the number of the beast
d. for it is (the) number of (a) person
e. indeed his number (is) six hundred sixty six
