###### v1
a. then (a) measuring rod like (a) stick was given to me saying
b. get up and measure the temple of God
c. also the altar
d. as well as those worshipping in it

###### v2
a. yet the courtyard the one outside the temple let (it) be outside
b. indeed do not measure it
c. because it was given to the Gentiles
d. yes they will trample the holy city for forty months [and] two

###### v3
a. then I will give to My two witnesses
b. and they will prophesy for (a) thousand two hundred (and) sixty days
c. having been clothed in sackcloth

###### v4
a. these are the two olive trees and the two lampstands
b. the ones standing before the Lord of the earth

###### v5
a. and if someone wants to harm them
b. fire goes out from their mouth
c. and utterly destroys their enemies
d. indeed if someone wants to harm them
e. likewise he must self be killed

###### v6
a. these ones have the power to close the sky
b. so that rain would not rain for the days of their prophecy
c. and they have power over the waters
d. to turn them into blood
e. and to strike the earth a blow with every plague
f. whenever they desire

###### v7
a. then when they have finished their testimony
b. the beast ascending from the abyss
c. will make war against them
d. and he will conquer them
e. and he will kill them

###### v8
a. and the dead body of them (will lie) on the wide street of the great city
b. which is called figuratively Sodom and Egypt
c. where also their Lord was crucified

###### v9
a. and from the nations and tribes and languages and peoples they look at
b. the dead body of them for three and (a) half days
c. and they do not allow their dead bodies to be placed in (a) tomb

###### v10
a. and those living on the earth
b. rejoice over them
c. and they are made glad
d. and they will send gifts to one another
e. because these two prophets tormented
f. those living on the earth

###### v11
a. then after three days and (a) half
b. (a) breath of life from God went into them
c. and they stood on their feet
d. then (a) great fear fell upon those seeing them

###### v12
a. and they heard (a) loud voice from heaven
b. saying to them
c. come up here
d. and they went up into heaven in (a) cloud
e. indeed their enemies observed them

###### v13
a. now at that hour (a) great earthquake occurred
b. then (a) tenth of the city was destroyed
c. and they were killed by the earthquake
d. seven thousand persons of (the) people
e. and the rest became terrified
f. and then they gave honour to the God of heaven

###### v14
a. the second horror departed
b. pay attention
c. the third horror comes soon

###### v15
a. then the seventh angel blew the trumpet
b. and there were loud voices in heaven saying
c. the kingdom of the world became
d. (the kingdom) of our Lord
e. and (the kingdom) of His Christ
f. and He shall reign forever and ever

###### v16
a. then the twenty four elders [the ones] in front of God
b. sitting upon their thrones
c. fell down upon their faces
d. and they worshipped God

###### v17
a. saying
b. we thank you Lord God the Almighty
c. the One being and the One was
d. because You have received your great power
e. and You reigned

###### v18
a. now the nations became very angry
b. yet Your wrath came
c. and the time of the dead to be judged
d. and to be given the reward to Your servants
e. to the prophets and to the devout
f. indeed to those respecting Your Name
g. the small ones and the big ones
h. and to destroy the ones destroying the earth

###### v19
a. then was opened the temple of God the One in heaven
b. and the Box of His covenant was seen in His temple
c. then lightnings and voices and thunders occurred
d. also (an) earthquake and  terrible hail
