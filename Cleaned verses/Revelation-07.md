###### v1
a. after this I saw four angels
b. having taken stand at the four corners of the earth
c. holding on to the four winds of the earth
d. so that (the) wind would not blow upon the earth
e. neither on the sea nor against any tree

###### v2
a. then I saw another angel
b. coming up from (the) rising of (the) sun
c. holding (the) seal of (the) living God
d. and he shouted with (a) loud voice to the four angels
e. to whom was given to them to harm the land and the sea

###### v3
a. saying
b. do not harm the land nor the sea nor the trees
c. until we have sealed the servants of our God on their foreheads

###### v4
a. then I heard the number of those having been sealed
b. one hundred forty four thousand
c. having been sealed from every tribe of the nation of Israel

###### v5
a. from (the) tribe of Judah twelve thousand having been sealed
b. from (the) tribe of Reuben twelve thousand
c. from (the) tribe of Gad twelve thousand

###### v6
a. from (the) tribe of Asher twelve thousand
b. from (the) tribe of Naphtali twelve thousand
c. from (the) tribe of Manasseh twelve thousand

###### v7
a. from (the) tribe of Simeon twelve thousand
b. from (the) tribe of Levi twelve thousand
c. from (the) tribe of Issachar twelve thousand

###### v8
a. from (the) tribe of Zebulun twelve thousand
b. from (the) tribe of Joseph twelve thousand
c. from (the) tribe of Benjamin twelve thousand having been sealed

###### v9
a. after this I looked
b. now pay attention
c. (a) large crowd that no one was able to count it
d. from every nation and tribe and people and language
e. standing before the throne and before the Lamb
f. having been clothed in white long robes
g. and (having) palm branches in their hands

###### v10
a. and they called out in (a) loud voice saying
b. salvation (is) by our God the One sitting upon the throne
c. and also by the Lamb

###### v11
a. further all the angels took their stand around the throne
b. and (around) the elders and the four living beings
c. then they fell down before the throne on their faces
d. and they worshipped God

###### v12
a. saying amen
b. the praise and the glory and the wisdom and the thanksgiving
c. and the honour and the power and the might (belongs) to our God
d. forever and ever amen

###### v13
a. then one of the elders declared saying to me
b. these ones having been clothed in white long robes
c. who are they and from where did they come?

###### v14
a. and I said to him
b. my master you know
c. then he said to me
d. these are the ones coming out of the great hardship
e. indeed they washed their long robes
f. indeed they made them white by the blood of the Lamb

###### v15
a. because of this they are before the throne of God
b. and they worship Him day and night in His temple
c. and the One sitting upon the throne
d. will come to dwell with them

###### v16
a. they shall not hunger anymore and not thirst anymore
b. neither not shall the sun cause them to suffer nor any scorching heat

###### v17
a. for the Lamb in the middle of the throne will guide and help them
b. and He will lead them to springs of living waters
c. and God will wipe away every tear from their eyes
