###### v1
a. men brothers and fathers
b. listen to my defence to you now

###### v2
a. so having heard that
b. he was addressing them in the Hebrew language
c. they kept on being more silent
d. and he said

###### v3
a. I am (a) Jewish man
b. having been born in Tarsus of Cilicia
c. and being educated in this city under the direction of Gamaliel
d. taught according to (the) strictness of the Law of our ancestors
e. being (a) zealot for God
f. just as you all are today

###### v4
a. I who persecuted to death this Way
b. binding and handing over to prison men as well as women

###### v5
a. as also the high priest testifies for me
b. as well as the whole Sanhedrin
c. from whom having also received letters to the fellow Jews in  
d. I was on my way
e. bringing also those being there bounded to Jerusalem
f. so that they may be punished

###### v6
a. then it happened to me travelling
b. and approaching Damascus
c. about noon suddenly from the sky (a) bright light shone brightly  

###### v7
a. and I fell to the ground
b. and I heard (a) voice saying to me
c. Saul Saul why do you persecute Me?

###### v8
a. and I answered
b. who are you Lord?
c. and He said to me
d. I am Jesus the Nazarene
e. Whom you persecute

###### v9
a. now those being with me indeed saw the light
b. but the voice they did not hear
c. of the One speaking to me

###### v10
a. and I said
b. what shall I do Lord?
c. and the Lord said to me
d. having stood up go to Damascus
e. and there it will be told to you about all things
f. of which it has been assigned for you to do

###### v11
a. and because I was not able to see due to the brightness of that light
b. being led by hand by those being with me
c. I went into Damascus

###### v12
a. and a certain Ananias (a) devout man concerning the Law
b. being well spoken of by all the Jews living there

###### v13
a. having come to me and having stood nearby
b. he said to me
c. Saul fellow believer see again
d. and I at the same hour was able to look at him

###### v14
a. and he said
b. the God of our ancestors chose you in advance
c. to know His will
d. and to see the righteous One
e. and to hear (a) voice from His mouth

###### v15
a. that you will be (a) witness for Him to all people
b. of what you have seen
c. and you heard

###### v16
a. and now why do you wait?
b. having stood up be baptised
c. and purify your sins
d. having called upon His Name

###### v17
a. then it happened to me having returned to Jerusalem
b. indeed while I was praying in the temple
c. I came into (an) ecstatic vision

###### v18
a. and saw Him saying to me
b. make haste and go quickly out of Jerusalem
c. because they will not accept your testimony about Me

###### v19
a. so I said
b. Lord they themselves know that
c. I was imprisoning
d. and beating throughout the synagogues
e. those believing in You

###### v20
a. and when the blood of Stephen Your witness was being poured out
b. also I myself was standing by
c. and was approving
d. and was keeping watch over the garments of those killing him

###### v21
a. and He said to me
b. go
c. because I will send you far away to (the) Gentiles

###### v22
a. now they were listening to him until this statement
b. then they were raising their voice saying
c. take such one away from the earth
d. because it is not right that he lives

###### v23
a. and while they were shouting
b. and while waving their garments
c. and while throwing dust into the air

###### v24
a. the commanding officer ordered
b. that he be taken into the soldiers' quarters
c. having said that he be interrogated by flogging
d. so that he could find out
e. for what reason they were thus shouting against him

###### v25
a. and when they stretched him out with the thongs
b. Paul said to the centurion standing (there)
c. is it indeed allowed for you to flog a Roman man without trial?

###### v26
a. and then the centurion having heard (this)
b. having gone to the commanding officer
c. he reported saying
d. what are you about to do?
e. because this man is a Roman

###### v27
a. then the commanding officer having come to (him)
b. he said to him
c. tell me
d. are you a Roman?
e. and he said yes

###### v28
a. then the commanding officer answered
b. I acquired this citizenship for (a) large price
c. but Paul said
d. now I was indeed born (a citizen)

###### v29
a. then immediately they withdrew from him
b. those being about to interrogate him
c. also the commanding officer was now afraid
d. having realised that he is a Roman
e. and that he had chained him

###### v30
a. then the next day wanting to know the truth
b. namely why he was accused by the Jews
c. he untied him
d. and he ordered
e. that the chief priests and the whole Sanhedrin gather together
f. then having brought Paul down
g. he set (him) among them
